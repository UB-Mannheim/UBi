

# Research Data Management Seminars (https://www.bib.uni-mannheim.de/lehren-und-forschen/forschungsdatenzentrum/research-data-management-seminars/)



## The Research Data Management Seminars provide you with useful information, tools, and tips for handling your research data.

Target group are (junior) researchers or advanced master students at the University of Mannheim. The topics range from data management planning over data collection and replication to data archiving and publication.
