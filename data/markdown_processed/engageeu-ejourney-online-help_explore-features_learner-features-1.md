# Learner Features: Application Guide

A Learner is defined as a student or participant who engages in the educational activities and mobility opportunities offered by ENGAGE.EU. This guide provides comprehensive support for using the platform as a Learner, focusing specifically on the application process.

## 📝 Application Prerequisites

Before starting your application, ensure you have the following personal details and documents ready:

**Personal Details:**

- Birthdate
- Gender
- Degree Title (e.g., Business Administration)

**Required Documents:**

- Transcript of Records
- Motivation Statement (must be in English)

______________________________________________________________________

## 🚀 Step-by-Step Application Process

Follow these steps to successfully submit and manage your application:

### 1. Registration and Access

- **Step 1:** Register or Log in to the platform.
- **Step 2:** Access the Application Form by clicking the designated "Apply here" button.

### 2. Completing the Form

The application form requires both automatically filled and manually completed information:

**Automatically Filled Fields:**

- First Name
- Surname
- Email Address
- Student ID

**Manually Completed Fields:**

- Select the desired course from the available options.
- Choose your gender.
- Enter your birthdate.
- Specify your degree title.
- **Upload Documents:**
  - Transcript of Records
  - Motivation Letter (PDF and video formats are accepted)

**Optional Fields:**

- You may optionally subscribe to the ENGAGE.EU newsletter.

### 3. Submission and Confirmation

- **Step 3:** Navigate to the upper right corner of the form and click the **“Apply”** button to submit.
- **Confirmation:** A confirmation message will appear on the screen, and you will also receive a confirmation email verifying receipt of your application.

### 4. Managing Your Application Status

#### 🔄 Cancelling or Withdrawing

- **Cancellation:** If you wish to cancel, you can do so under 'My Applications' by clicking the action button.
- **Withdrawal:** Once the application window closes, the action button changes from 'cancel' to 'withdraw'. **Withdrawing an application is final.** Your home university will be informed, and you will not be able to re-apply.

#### 🔔 Decision Handling

- Once the receiving Higher Education Institution (HEI) reviews your application, you will be notified of their decision.
- **If you receive an offer:** You must either **Confirm** your admission to accept the offer or **Reject** it if you do not wish to proceed.
- *Action:* Ensure you respond within the indicated deadline.

### 💡 Troubleshooting and Status Check

**Troubleshooting:**

- If a red box appears around any field, it indicates that the field is either empty or contains an invalid entry. Please complete the field or select a valid option from the drop-down menu.

**Checking Status:**

1. From the main menu on the left, press **“Learning Activity”**.
1. Select **“My extracurricular Applications”**.
1. A list of all your applications and their current status will be displayed on the right.
