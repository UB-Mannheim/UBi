# GESS Student Representatives

GESS student representatives are elected by their respective Center's doctoral students to represent their interests at the Graduate School. This mechanism ensures that student interests, concerns, and needs are considered at every level of decision-making. By receiving feedback, student representatives can drive positive change within the GESS and ensure that student needs are met.

### Responsibilities and Tasks

The responsibilities of the GESS student representatives include:

- **Representation:** Advocating for and communicating student interests to the GESS board and the administration.
- **Feedback Collection:** Proactively meeting and voicing feedback from students.
- **Participation:** Attending meetings with the GESS board and administration to provide feedback on the student experience.
- **Information Dissemination:** Informing the student body about relevant discussions and decisions made at meetings of the student representatives or the GESS board.
