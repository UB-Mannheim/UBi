# SMiP Workshop Program: Specialized Meetings

This document outlines past and upcoming specialized workshops focusing on advanced topics in cognitive modeling, including memory, longitudinal data analysis, and diffusion models.

## 🧠 Memory and Metamemory

- **Date & Time:** 11 to 13 September 2025
- **Location:** Hotel Krone in Hirschberg
- **Organizers:** Arndt Bröder & Beatrice Kuhlmann
- **External Guests:** Monika Undorf (TU Darmstadt), Marie Luisa Schaper (HHU Düsseldorf), and David Shanks (University College London)
- **Contributions:** Details will be announced soon (via email).
- **Learn More:** [Memory and Metamemory](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/program/special-events/small-group-meetings/)

## 📊 Intensive Longitudinal Data

- **Date & Time:** 15 March 2024 (10:00 a.m. – 05:00 p.m.)
- **Location:** University of Mannheim
- **Organizer:** Sabine Sonnentag
- **Description:** This workshop aims to bring together SMiP researchers who work with ESM (Experience Sampling Method) or diary data. The discussion covers pressing problems, potential solutions, and is not limited to data-analysis issues, including questions regarding study planning.
- **Learn More:** [Intensive Longitudinal Data](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/program/special-events/small-group-meetings/)
