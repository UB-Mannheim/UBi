# Decarbonization Seminar

The **Decarbonization Seminar** is a platform that brings together speakers and participants from academia and industry. The seminar discusses the latest advances and challenges associated with the transition towards a decarbonized economy.

**Topics covered include:**

- The economics and management of corporate emissions.
- Decarbonization pathways.
- Incentives for climate action.

## 🗓️ Seminar Logistics and Participation

The seminar sessions are typically held as a hybrid event on **Monday afternoons** from **05:15 to 06:30 PM (CET or CEST)**.

- **To Join In Person:** Please attend the room indicated on the respective speaker page.
- **To Join Virtually:** Advance registration is required using the link provided with each [talk announcement](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/mises/newsletter-decarbonization-seminar/).
- **Stay Informed:** Sign up for the seminar’s mailing list via [this form](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/newsletter-decarbonization-seminar/) to receive updates on upcoming presentations.
