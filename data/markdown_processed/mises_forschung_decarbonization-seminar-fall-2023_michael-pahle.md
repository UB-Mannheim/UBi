## Michael Pahle: Expert Talk on Decarbonization and Climate Policy

**Event Details**

- **Speaker:** Michael Pahle
- **Affiliation:** Potsdam Institute for Climate Impact Research (PIK)
- **Event:** Symposium on „Carbon Emission Pledges and Corporate Decarbonization Initiatives”
- **Date:** September 27, 2023
- **Location:** Frankfurt
- **Admission:** Invitation only (due to limited seating capacity).

### Speaker Profile and Expertise

Michael Pahle is the head of the working group “Climate and Energy Policy” at the Potsdam Institute for Climate Impact Research (PIK). He holds a PhD in economics from TU Berlin.

**Research Focus:**
His research centers on:

- Emission trading systems.
- Public support for carbon pricing.
- Regulatory frameworks necessary to achieve net-zero emissions.

**Key Projects and Advisory Roles:**

- **Ariadne Project:** He serves as the Principle Investigator for “European Climate and Energy Policy” within the BMBF [Ariadne Project](https://www.kopernikus-projekte.de/en/projects/ariadne), which is Germany’s largest social science Energiewende research project.
- **Advisory Capacity:** Michael advises the German government and parliament, the EU Commission, and the EU Parliament on the development, reform, and extension of emission trading mechanisms.
- **Collaboration:** He collaborates with leading EU think tanks, including the European University Institute (Florence) and Bruegel (Brussels).
- **Global Network:** He is a member of the [Global Climate Policy Partnership](https://www.rff.org/gcpp/), a US-based global network dedicated to helping major economies and businesses achieve ambitious climate goals.

**Media Coverage:**
Michael’s work and views on climate policy have been featured in major national and international media outlets, including *FAZ*, *Spiegel*, *Süddeutsche*, *Zeit*, *Handelsblatt*, *El País*, *Politiken*, *Euractiv*, *Financial Times*, *Bloomberg*, *New York Times*, and *The Wall Street Journal*.
