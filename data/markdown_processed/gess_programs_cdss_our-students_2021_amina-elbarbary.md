# Amina Elbarbary

Amina Elbarbary is a PhD candidate in Political Science at the CDSS. She holds both a Bachelor’s and a Master’s degree in Political Science from the University of Mannheim.

## Academic and Professional Background

- **Early Experience:** During her Bachelor’s studies, she gained experience interning at a think tank and research institute focused on conflict resolution, peacebuilding, and peacekeeping operations in Egypt.
- **Work Experience:** She has worked as a working student at SAP SE and served as a research assistant at the Chair of Political Science, Political Economy.
- **Master Thesis Focus:** Her Master Thesis concentrated on analyzing the effect of blasphemy laws on self-censorship regarding politically sensitive topics in both democratic and non-democratic contexts.

## Research Interests

Amina is particularly interested in the intersection of:

- Game theory
- Formal modeling
- How public policies influence individual behaviors and preferences.
