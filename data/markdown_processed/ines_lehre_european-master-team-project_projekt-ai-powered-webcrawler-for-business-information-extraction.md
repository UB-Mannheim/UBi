# AI-powered Webcrawler for Business Information Extraction

The project addresses the growing need for detailed corporate data, driven by regulations such as the German Supply Chain Due Diligence Act. This law mandates that companies must gain detailed information about all suppliers to assess their risk of non-compliance.

### 🌐 Context and Problem Statement

The required information for due diligence is extensive and includes:

- Locations and sublocations
- Owners and responsible key personnel
- Industry of operation
- Revenue and number of employees
- Availability of certifications (e.g., ISO certificates)
- Products, services, and customers

Crucially, this vital data is often available in an **unstructured format** across various company websites, necessitating advanced processing techniques to extract and structure it.

### 🎯 Project Objective

The goal is to design, implement, and deploy an AI-powered webcrawler. This system must efficiently navigate business websites in a hierarchical manner, identify relevant data points, and utilize Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to extract crucial, structured information regarding a company's operations and organizational structure. The final output must be accurate, comprehensive, and ready for analysis.

### 🛠️ Key Deliverables

The project scope includes the development and implementation of four core components:

1. **AI-powered Webcrawler Prototype:**
   - Develop a functional webcrawler capable of scanning diverse websites.
   - Utilize NLP technologies to extract specific data points (e.g., industry, ISO certificates, top management, sublocations, products, services, customers, and subfirms).
1. **Data Processing and Visualization Pipeline:**
   - Implement a robust pipeline to clean, organize, and structure the raw extracted data.
   - Present the processed data in a user-friendly format suitable for subsequent analysis and visualization.
1. **NLP Processing of Crawled Data:**
   - Apply advanced NLP techniques to process the raw crawled data.
   - Structure the data into a pre-processed format optimized for subsequent machine learning steps.
1. **Neural Network-based Categorization:**
   - Employ neural networks to execute sophisticated categorization tasks.
   - Enhance the accuracy and efficiency of information classification within the extracted dataset.

### 🧠 Required Skills and Expertise

Successful execution of this project requires a diverse and interdisciplinary skill set:

- **Programming Proficiency:** Expertise in languages such as Python or JavaScript for web development, data processing, and scripting.
- **Data Science & AI:**
  - Strong understanding of NLP tools and their processing capabilities.
  - Base knowledge of Neural Networks and their practical application in information classification.
- **Web Technologies:**
  - Knowledge of web crawling techniques and data scraping methodologies.
  - Ability to handle various web formats (e.g., HTML, XML).
- **Data Management:**
  - Familiarity with databases and data management principles for storing and retrieving extracted information.
- **Soft Skills:**
  - Effective teamwork, project timeline management, and collaborative problem-solving abilities.
