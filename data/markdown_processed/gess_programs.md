# Doctoral Programs at the University of Mannheim

The Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS) offers 11 internationally competitive and research-oriented doctoral programs across business, economics, and social sciences.

These full-time programs typically last between three to five years and are designed to prepare students for academic careers at leading international universities and research institutions. The program fosters a vibrant and international research community, currently comprising over 230 students from more than 40 countries.

## Doctoral Centers and Programs

The PhD programs are administered through specialized centers in collaboration with the relevant faculties:

### Center for Doctoral Studies in Business (CDSB)

- **Program:** PhD in Business.
- **Administration:** The Business School of the University of Mannheim.
- **Accreditation:** The Business School is triple accredited by AACSB, EFDM (EQUIS), and AMBA.
- **Link:** [Business School](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/)

### Center for Doctoral Studies in Economics (CDSE)

- **Program:** PhD in Economics.
- **Administration:** The Department of Economics of the University of Mannheim.
- **Link:** [Department of Economics](https://www.vwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/)

### Center for Doctoral Studies in Social and Behavioral Sciences (CDSS)

- **Program:** PhD programs in Political Science, Psychology, and Sociology.
- **Administration:** In close collaboration with the School of Social Sciences at the University of Mannheim.
- **Link:** [School of Social Sciences](https://www.sowi.uni-mannheim.de/en/)
