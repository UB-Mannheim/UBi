# Collaborative Research Center for Political Reforms

The 21st century presents enormous challenges to modern societies, including climate change, aging populations, and economic globalization. These issues necessitate sophisticated political reforms, which, if mishandled, can seriously disrupt the economic, political, and social order.

While reform-making is often delayed, compromised, or aborted due to lack of consensus, there is also growing skepticism at the individual level regarding biased information, the fair distribution of reform costs, and the effectiveness of political promises.

## Research Focus and Mission

Our Collaborative Research Center (SFB) aims to provide new scientific insights into the success and failure of political reforms. Our mission is to establish a multidisciplinary international framework of researchers who pursue a rigorous methodological approach, analyzing:

- The various aspects of reform-making.
- The competing interests involved.
- The political contexts and processes.

We draw together junior and senior faculty from across the University of Mannheim in economics, political science, and sociology to deepen the understanding of political reforms.
