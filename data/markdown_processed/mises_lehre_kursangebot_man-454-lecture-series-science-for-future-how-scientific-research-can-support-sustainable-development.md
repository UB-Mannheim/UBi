# MAN 454: Lecture Series “Science for Future – How Scientific Research can Support Sustainable Development”

This interdisciplinary lecture series explores the potential of scientific research to contribute to sustainable development, framed by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

## Course Overview

- **Course Code:** MAN 454
- **Format:** The series comprises 6 sessions, totaling 12 talks (two talks are presented on one day).
- **Structure per Talk:** Each talk includes approximately 60 minutes of presentation followed by a 30-minute discussion.
- **Target Audience:** Open to students from all Bachelor programs.

## Content and Scope

The lecture series brings together researchers from various disciplines at the University of Mannheim to present cutting-edge research and foundational knowledge on sustainability-related topics.

**Participating Disciplines Include (but are not limited to):**

- Economics
- Psychology
- History
- Political Science
- Philosophy
- Information Systems
- Business Management
