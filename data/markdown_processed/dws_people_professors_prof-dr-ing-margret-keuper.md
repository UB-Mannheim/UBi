## Research Profile

I joined the Data and Web Science Group in October 2023 as a [Professor for Machine Learning](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/research/focus-groups/computer-vision-machine-learning-prof-dr-ing-margret-keuper/). I am also affiliated with the [Max-Planck-Institute for Informatics](https://www.mpi-inf.mpg.de/de/departments/computer-vision-and-machine-learning/people/margret-keuper) in Saarbrücken (since November 2021). I have been a member of ELLIS since 2022 and a fellow since 2024.

**Research Interests:**
My research focuses on Computer Vision and Machine Learning, with specific interests in:

- Robustness and Reliability of Deep Learning Models
- Neural Architecture Search (NAS)
- Grouping Problems (e.g., Image and Motion Segmentation, Multiple Object Tracking)
- Efficient Solvers for Large Grouping Problems
- Motion Estimation
- Image Generation and Deep Fake Detection

**Academic History:**

- **PhD:** Completed at the [University of Freiburg](http://www.uni-freiburg.de/) with [Thomas Brox](https://lmb.informatik.uni-freiburg.de/index.php).
- **Previous Positions:** Professor for Visual Computing at the University of Siegen (2021–2023) and Juniorprofessor for Image Processing in Mannheim (2017–2021).
