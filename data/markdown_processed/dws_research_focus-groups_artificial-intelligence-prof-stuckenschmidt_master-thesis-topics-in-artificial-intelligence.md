# Master Thesis Topics in Artificial Intelligence

The Chair for Artificial Intelligence offers various research areas for Master's theses. Below are the current focus areas, example topics, and corresponding contacts.

## ⚙️ Process Mining

**Contact:** Keyvan.Amiri@uni-mannheim.de

**Example Topics:**

- Building a Foundational Model for Predictive Process Mining
- Zero-Shot Prediction in Process Mining

## 🚗 Traffic Prediction and Simulation

**Contact:** Julia.Gastinger@uni-mannheim.de

**Example Topics:**

- Extending the Mannheim City Traffic Simulation with an Emission Model
- Simulating Micro-Mobility in Mannheim City Center
- Data-driven Scheduling of a Heterogeneous Public Transport Fleet.

## 🧠 AI in the Social Sciences

**Contact:** Heiner.Stuckenschmidt@uni-mannheim.de

**Example Topics:**

- Simulating the impact of different content moderation policies on the willingness to participate in online discourse
- Automatic detection of Social Situations through mobile data collection

## 🎓 AI in Higher Education

**Contact:** Lea.Cohausz@uni-mannheim.de

**Example Topics:**

- Concept Creation from Text and Interaction Data
- Adaptive Questionnaire Design / Interactive Recommendations
- Analyzing Bias and Accuracy in Educational Data Mining
- Small Data Settings for Predicting Students’ Answers
