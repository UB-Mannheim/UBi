# Nico Kasparetz

**Academic Details**

- **Year:** 2022
- **Center:** Economics
- **Supervisor:** [Ulrich Wagner](https://www.vwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/wagner/)

## Academic and Professional Background

Nico Kasparetz was born in 1997 in Hamburg, Germany.

**Bachelor Studies (University of Hamburg)**
He completed his bachelor's degree in Economics in his hometown. During this time, he gained practical experience working as a Teaching Assistant (TA) and student tutor for undergraduate macroeconomics, as well as serving as a student assistant at the Chair of Microeconometrics.

**Master Studies (University of Mannheim)**
Nico relocated to Mannheim for his master's program. During his master's studies, he significantly expanded his practical research experience:

- **Internship:** He worked as a full-time research intern at the Deutsche Bundesbank (German Central Bank).
- **Teaching:** He gained teaching experience as a TA for Econometrics.
- **International Experience:** He spent one semester as an exchange student at the University of Sydney in Australia.

## Research Interests

Nico's primary research interest lies in **Environmental Economics**. His interests also extend to related fields, including:

- Development Economics
- Public Economics

For his master’s thesis, he analyzed the impact of the EU Emissions Trading System on the own electricity generation of manufacturing firms.
