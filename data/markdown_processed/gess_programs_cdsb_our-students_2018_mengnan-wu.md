# Mengnan Wu, M.Sc.

Mengnan Wu is a PhD student affiliated with the University of Mannheim.

## Academic Profile and Experience

- **Education:** Mengnan was born in 1993 in Beijing, China. She majored in Finance and obtained her Bachelor's Degree in Economics from the University of International Business and Economics in 2016. She subsequently joined the Finance track of the Mannheim Master in Business Research, where she received core research training provided by GESS.
- **Research Experience:** Complementary to her studies, she gained research experience as a research assistant at the Finance Laboratory of CITIC Trust (Beijing, China) and as a student research assistant at the Department of Economics of Innovation and Industrial Dynamics in the Center for European Economic Research (Mannheim, Germany).
- **Current Role:** After graduating with an M.Sc. in 2018, she joined the Chair of Finance at the University of Mannheim as an academic staff member.

## Research Interests

Mengnan's primary research interests include:

- Capital market
- Corporate finance
- Empirical asset pricing
