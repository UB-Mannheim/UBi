# ACC 452: Rapid Decarbonization: Challenges and Opportunities for Business

This course provides an in-depth overview of the challenges and opportunities that companies face during the transition toward a decarbonized energy economy.

## 🎓 Course Overview

**Target Audience:** Bachelor students in Business Administration, Economics, and related disciplines.

**Focus:** The course introduces the microeconomic theory of corporate decarbonization efforts while reviewing recent practical developments in the field.

## 📚 Key Topics Covered

Participants will gain knowledge in several critical areas of the energy transition:

- Carbon Emissions and Carbon Regulations
- Corporate Decarbonization Initiatives
- Competitive Analysis of Emerging Climate Technologies
- Bringing Low-carbon Products and Production Processes to Market
- Climate-tech Entrepreneurship and Project Finance

## 🎯 Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of the course, participants will be able to:

- Identify the main challenges and opportunities companies encounter on their path toward a net-zero carbon footprint.
- Understand both the theoretical economic concepts central to this transition and recent practical tools.
- Analyze complex case studies related to climate change and business strategy.
