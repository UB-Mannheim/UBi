## Research Focus

My research primarily focuses on formal and philosophical logic and analytic metaphysics. I also maintain research interests in philosophy of language, epistemology, and philosophy of science. Furthermore, I study the history of analytic philosophy, with a special emphasis on Frege, Wittgenstein, and the Vienna Circle. Within my current research projects, I concentrate on Wittgenstein's psychological expressivism.
