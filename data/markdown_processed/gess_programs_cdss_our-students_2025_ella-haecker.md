# Ella Häcker

Ella Häcker is a doctoral candidate in Sociology at the CDSS.

## Academic Background and Experience

Ella Häcker's academic journey includes:

- **Bachelor's Degree:** Sociology at the University of Mannheim. During her undergraduate studies, she gained experience as a research assistant at the chair of Social Data Science and Methodology, led by Prof. Dr. Florian Keusch.
- **Master's Degree:** Sociology with a focus on Social Psychology and Quantitative Methods from Goethe University in Frankfurt.
- **Graduate Research:** She served as a research assistant at both the University of Mannheim and the Wissenschaftszentrum Berlin (WZB).

## Research Focus

Ella's research interests are highly interdisciplinary, focusing on the intersection of social science, data analysis, and political behavior.

**Master's Thesis:**
Her master's thesis examined the influence of individual social class and economic context effects on the individual voting intention for radical right-wing populist parties.

**Key Areas of Interest:**

- Quantitative methods
- Text analysis using machine learning techniques
- Political extremism and radicalization
- Social inequality
