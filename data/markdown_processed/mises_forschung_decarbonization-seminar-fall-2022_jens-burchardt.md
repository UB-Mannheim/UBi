# Dekarbonisierung Seminar: Navigating the Road to Net Zero

**Speaker:** [Jens Burchardt](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar-fall-2022/jens-burchardt/)
**Organization:** Boston Consulting Group (BCG)
**Date:** October 10, 2022
**Time:** 05:15 – 06:30 PM (CEST)
**Format:** Virtual Only

## Seminar Abstract

The seminar addresses the critical challenge of decarbonization in Germany. The core questions explored include:

- How can Germany implement historic measures to almost halve emissions by 2030, given the current reliance on ramping up coal and LNG consumption to compensate for acute gas shortages?
- What steps are necessary to re-establish supply security while simultaneously achieving climate targets?
- How can companies successfully navigate the transformation ahead and create value from the decarbonization process?

## Speaker Profile: Jens Burchardt

Jens Burchardt is BCG’s global expert on climate change. He brings approximately 12 years of international project experience focusing on:

- Climate impact and the energy transition.
- Low-emission technologies (including renewables, hydrogen, power-to-x, and CCUS).
- He serves as an Advisor to the World Economic Forum (WEF), COP26, and several German government commissions on climate.
- He is a TED Speaker and led BCG’s landmark emission reduction studies, “Klimapfade für Deutschland” (“Climate Paths for Germany”), in collaboration with the German industry.
