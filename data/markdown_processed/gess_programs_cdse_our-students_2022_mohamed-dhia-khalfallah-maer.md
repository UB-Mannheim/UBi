# Mohamed Dhia Khalfallah

## Professional Profile

Mohamed Dhia Khalfallah is an economist who joined the CDSE via the Master of Economic Research (MAER) track. He completed his undergraduate studies at the Vienna University of Business and Economics, specializing in economics and finance.

His research interests are broad, covering theoretical macroeconomics (especially heterogeneity and long-term growth mechanisms), international economics, public economics, and development economics. During his studies, he participated in various policy research projects in Tunisia, focusing primarily on the impact of market regulation on productivity and long-term growth. His Bachelor's degree focused on the cultural explanation of cross-country saving rate heterogeneity.

**Interests:**

- **Academic:** Theoretical Macroeconomics, International Economics, Public Economics, Development Economics.
- **Personal:** Basketball, football, and movies.
