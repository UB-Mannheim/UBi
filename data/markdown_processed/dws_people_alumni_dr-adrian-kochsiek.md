## Research Focus

Adrian Kochsiek is a Ph.D. candidate at the [Chair of Data Analytics](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/research/focus-groups/data-analytics-prof-gemulla/). This chair is part of the [Data and Web Science Group](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/home/) at the [University of Mannheim](http://www.uni-mannheim.de/1/english/). He is supervised by [Prof. Dr. Rainer Gemulla](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/professors/prof-dr-rainer-gemulla/).
