# Artificial Intelligence & Human Organizations

Researching the dynamic between algorithmic organizations, AI, and the human element is essential in the 21st century. Modern technologies offer immense potential to enhance decision-making, efficiency, and collaboration, enabling the creation of ethical, inclusive, and forward-thinking organizations that drive positive societal change and improve human well-being.

### Core Research Topics

Our research focuses on several critical areas:

- AI and algorithms in modern organizations.
- Human and artificial decision-making, ethics, and inclusion.
- AI and organizing for human well-being.
- Organization theory and AI.

### The Potential and the Peril of AI

AI holds significant promise for advancing humanity's well-being, offering solutions for:

- Addressing climate change.
- Improving medical diagnostics and treatments.
- Enhancing education.
- Improving workplaces.

However, if not applied carefully, AI carries risks, including:

- Reinforcing existing biases and stereotypes.
- Eroding trust.
- Disrupting careers.
- Exacerbating inequality.

### A Human-Centered Approach

To fully realize AI's potential, the implementation process must be **human-centered, sensitive, and reflective**. Our research actively contributes to shaping a future where AI benefits everyone, supporting a thriving and equitable society, and guiding organizations toward a well-considered vision of a desirable future.
