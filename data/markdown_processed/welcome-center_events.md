# Events and Workshops for International Researchers

The Welcome Center hosts various events designed to help international researchers and students integrate into life and academia in Mannheim.

### Upcoming Events

- **Working Across Cultures: Intercultural Workshop**
  Join this valuable workshop on April 24th to gain insights for a successful research experience in Germany.
- **Guided City Tour**
  Explore Mannheim with a unique guided tour that brings history, innovation, and culture to life.
- **Summer BBQ**
  Save the Date! Join us for the Summer BBQ for International Researchers.
- **Discover German Beer Culture – Brewery Tour**
  Learn about Germany’s famous beer! Join a behind-the-scenes brewery tour to explore brewing traditions, local flavors, and the cultural role of beer.
- **Winter Celebration**
  Looking for community and warmth? Join us for our Winter Celebration.
