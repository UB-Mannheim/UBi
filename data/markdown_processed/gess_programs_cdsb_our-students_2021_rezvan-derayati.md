## Rezvan Derayati

**Position:** Doctoral Student
**Center:** Business
**University:** University of Mannheim

### 🎓 Academic Background & Experience

Rezvan Derayati is a doctoral student who joined the accounting Track of the Center of Doctoral Studies in Business in September 2021.

- **Education:**
  - **B.Sc. (Industrial Engineering):** Polytechnic of Tehran, Iran.
  - **M.Sc. (Economics):** University of Pisa, Italy.
- **Internships & Achievements:**
  - During her graduate studies, she completed an internship at the Italian National Institute of Statistics (ISTAT) in Rome.
  - She won the Special Scholarship for the best international student of the Joint Master Program of Economics (University of Pisa and Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, 2019–2020).
  - She subsequently completed an internship at the Institute of Energy Economics (EWI) at the University of Cologne.

### 💡 Research Interests

Her primary areas of research focus include:

- Sustainability Development
- Energy and Environmental Economics
- Innovation and Growth
