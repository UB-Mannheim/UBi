# LWDA 2018 Conference Overview

The LWDA 2018 conference, which stands for „Lernen, Wissen, Daten, Analysen“ (Learning, Knowledge, Data, Analytics), was held at the University of Mannheim from August 22–24, 2018.

The event was hosted by the [Data and Web Science group](http://dws.informatik.uni-mannheim.de/en/home/) of the [University of Mannheim](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/) and covered recent research across several advanced technological areas.

## Scope and Focus Areas

LWDA is a major academic gathering that brings together various special interest groups of the [Gesellschaft für Informatik](https://gi.de/) (German Computer Science Society). The conference scope includes:

- Knowledge discovery
- Machine learning & data mining
- Knowledge management
- Database management & information systems
- Information retrieval

## Program Structure

The conference program was comprehensive, comprising joint research sessions, keynotes, and specialized workshops organized by each special interest group.

### Workshops (2018)

Five specific workshops were announced for the 2018 event:

- **Information Retrieval:** [Workshop on „Information Retrieval“ (FGIR 2018)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/lwda-2018/calls/fgir-2018/)
- **Knowledge Management:** [Workshop on „Knowledge Management“ (FGWM 2018)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/lwda-2018/calls/fgwm-2018/)
- **Data Mining & Machine Learning:** [Workshop on „Knowledge Discovery, Data Mining, and Machine Learning“ (KDML 2018)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/lwda-2018/calls/kdml-2018/)
- **Business Intelligence:** [Workshop on „Business Intelligence“ (WSBI 2018)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/lwda-2018/calls/wsbi-2018/)
- **Large-Scale Data Management:** [Workshop on „Large-Scale Data Management and Processing – Applications in Research and Industry“ (FGDB 2018)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/lwda-2018/calls/fgdb-2018/)
