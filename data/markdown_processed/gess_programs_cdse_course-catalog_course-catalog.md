# CDSE Course Catalog and Academic Procedures

## Course Registration

The course catalog can be found at [CDSE Course Catalog](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdse/course-catalog/course-catalog/).

**Registration Deadlines (Portal²):**

- **Spring Semesters:** January 31
- **Fall Semesters:** August 31

**Important Registration Notes:**

- For CDSB or CDSS courses, registration must also be completed through Portal².
- If you wish to take a course not listed in the GESS course catalogue, please contact your Center Manager.
- **Doctoral Students:** Doctoral students from [ENGAGE.EU partner universities](https://www.engageuniversity.eu/partners/) are eligible to attend selected courses at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences.
  - To apply, please contact gess.registration@uni-mannheim.de. You must indicate the desired courses and provide a confirmation of enrolment or other documentation of admission/participation in the doctoral program at your home institution upon request.

## Course Evaluation

The program constantly strives for improvement, and all students are asked to evaluate every course attended. These evaluations provide invaluable, anonymous feedback.

- **Process:** Evaluations are anonymous.
- **Access:** Username and password will be sent to students via email after the courses are finished.
- **Data Protection:** Information regarding data handling can be found [according to Article 13 GDPR](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/data-protection-declaration/).
