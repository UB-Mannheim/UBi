## Research Areas

Prof. Rouder's research is divided into two major lines of study:

**1. Substantive Research (Cognitive Processes):**
This area focuses on how people process and combine new perceptual information with pre-existing knowledge to make quick, effortless decisions about the world. Key areas include:

- Memory
- Attention
- Perception
- Categorization
- Letter recognition

**2. Methodological Research (Statistical Modeling):**
This involves developing nonlinear hierarchical models. The goal is to improve estimation and inference, ultimately leading to better theory development across various domains.
