# 4th Annual CCVision Network Meeting (June 23, 2026)

The **Climate Change Vision (CCVision) Network** is a global community of researchers dedicated to investigating the visual dimension of climate change.

**Organized by:**

- [Katharina Prasse](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/researchers/phd-students/katharina-prasse/)
- [Anastasia Kanat](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/administration/#c98828)
- [Prof. Dr.-Ing. Margret Keuper](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/professors/prof-dr-ing-margret-keuper/)

### About the CCVision Network

Our members bring expertise from a diverse range of fields, including:

- Computer Science
- Computational Social Science
- Geography
- Communications

The network represents all career stages, from PhD candidates to senior professors. We host annual meetings designed to bridge the gap between disciplines by sharing insights beyond individual method toolboxes, fostering essential dialogue between **qualitative and quantitative researchers**.

**Join Us!**
We are an open and growing community. Anyone interested in the intersection of climate change and visual culture is warmly invited to join our network.
