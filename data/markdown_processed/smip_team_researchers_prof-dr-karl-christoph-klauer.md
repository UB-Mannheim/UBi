## 📝 Mögliche Themen für Promotion (Ph.D.)

Ph.D.-Studenten sind eingeladen, eigene Themen zu entwickeln. Hier sind einige mögliche Themenbereiche:

- **Multinomial processing-tree models of implicit measures of social cognition with response-latency modeling** (Betreuer: Klauer, Erdfelder)
- **Investigating the role of sufficiency and necessity in moral judgment** (Betreuer: Klauer, Hütter)
- **Integration of response times in threshold models of recognition memory** (Betreuer: Erdfelder, Klauer)
- **Conditions for controllable and uncontrollable learning in evaluative conditioning** (Betreuer: Hütter, Klauer)
- **A life-span perspective on controllable and uncontrollable learning in evaluative conditioning** (Betreuer: Hütter, Klauer, Kuhlmann)
- **Analysis of interpersonal, item-specific, and dynamic predictors of subliminal priming effects** (Betreuer: Kiesel, Klauer)
- **Extending diffusion model analyses to more complex decision making** (Betreuer: Voss, Klauer)
- **Separation of storage and retrieval in episodic memory** (Betreuer: Bröder, Erdfelder, Klauer)
- **Item versus source forgetting rates** (Betreuer: Kuhlmann, Bröder, Klauer, Voss)
