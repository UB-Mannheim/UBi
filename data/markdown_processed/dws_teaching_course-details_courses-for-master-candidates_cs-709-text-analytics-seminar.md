# Text Analytics Seminar (CS 709)

This seminar provides an in-depth exploration of text analytics, designed for both Master and Bachelor students.

## 🎯 Learning Goals

Participants are expected to meet the following requirements throughout the seminar:

- Read, understand, and explore scientific literature related to a chosen topic.
- Analyze how the chosen topic is reflected by media and society.
- Discuss and debate the topic with fellow participants.
- Summarize the topic in a concise report.
- Deliver a presentation focusing on two scientific publications (25 minutes presentation + 20 minutes Q&A/discussion).

## 🎓 Prerequisites

**For Master Students:**

- Successful completion of IE 661 “Text Analytics” OR IE 663 “Web Search and Information Retrieval”.

**For Bachelor Students:**

- Successful completion of “Einführung in Data Science”.

**General Requirement:**

- Fundamental notions of linear algebra and probability theory.
