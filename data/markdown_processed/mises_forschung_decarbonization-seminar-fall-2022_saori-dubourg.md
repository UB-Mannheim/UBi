## Speaker Biography

Saori Dubourg is a member of the Executive Board of BASF SE since May 2017.

### Professional Roles and Expertise

- **Current Leadership:** Since March 2022, she leads the global businesses Monomers, Performance Materials, Petrochemicals, and Intermediates. She is also responsible for the Europe region, the startup trinamiX, and the BASF Corporate Sustainability Board.
- **Previous Responsibilities:** Until February 2022, she was responsible for the global businesses in the Agricultural Solutions, Nutrition & Health, and Care Chemicals divisions.
- **Career History:** She has been with BASF since 1996, holding various functions across the USA, Japan, Singapore, and Hong Kong. Her experience includes strategy and operational leadership in areas such as dispersions, monomers, superabsorbents, nutrition, and pharmaceuticals.
- **Leadership Positions:** She previously led BASF's senior executive human resources and served as president for all country operations and functional areas in Asia Pacific. From 2013 to May 2017, she was the president of the Nutrition & Health division.
- **Strategic Involvement:** In 2018, she played a key role in the acquisition of businesses and assets from Bayer, strengthening BASF's activities in crop protection, biotechnology, and digital farming. In 2020, she oversaw the divestment of the construction chemicals division to the private equity firm Lone Star.

### Advisory and Sustainability Contributions

- **EU Policy:** As a member of the EU Commission's Industry Policy Group, she contributed to the vision paper on Europe 2030, which outlines the future of European industry (published June 2019).
- **Value Creation:** With the theme 'Value to Society', she has focused on holistic and future-oriented corporate steering and accounting for the corporate group. She chairs the steering committee of the Value-Balancing Alliance (founded June 2019), which aims to standardize the measurement of a company's value contribution across environment, society, and economy.
- **German Advisory:** Since January 2020, Saori Dubourg has been a member of the German Council for Sustainable Development, advising the German government on sustainability strategy implementation.
