## 🔬 Research Focus

Adrian Rebmann is a researcher specializing in advanced data analysis techniques, particularly within the field of Process Analytics.

**Key Research Interests:**

- Process Mining
- Preprocessing of Event Data
- Event Abstraction
- Semantic Event Parsing
