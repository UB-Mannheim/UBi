## Speaker Biography

**Atasu Atalay** is a Professor of Technology and Operations Management at INSEAD. He also holds the Bianca and James Pitt Chair in Environmental Sustainability and serves as the Academic Director of the INSEAD Sustainable Business Initiative.

His research focuses on socially and environmentally responsible operations management. Topics of interest include:

- The circular economy
- Extended producer responsibility
- Environmental regulation

His work has been published in prestigious journals, including *Management Science*, *Manufacturing and Service Operations Management*, *Production and Operations Management*, *Journal of Industrial Ecology*, *Harvard Business Review*, and *California Management Review*.

**Awards and Service:**
Atalay has received several accolades, including the Wickham Skinner Best Paper Award (winner 2007, runner up 2014), the Wickham Skinner Early Career Research Award (2012), and the Paul Kleindorfer Award in Sustainability (2013). He currently serves as Editor for the POM Sustainable Operations Department and as associate editor for MSOM.
