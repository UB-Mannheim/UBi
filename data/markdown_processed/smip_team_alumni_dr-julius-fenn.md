## Professional Overview

Dr. Fenn is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Freiburg. He is actively involved in research concerning the ethical implications and public acceptance of emerging technologies. He is open to consultation and collaboration regarding his research interests.

## Research Focus

**Core Area:** Assessing the acceptance and potential ethical issues associated with emerging technologies.
**Scope:** The research focuses on technologies that are currently in the research and development stage and have not yet been integrated into established normative frameworks for decision-making.
**Key Research Topic:** Advancement of Cognitive-Affective Maps – A Novel Tool for Uncovering Ethical Issues in Climate Engineering Technologies.
