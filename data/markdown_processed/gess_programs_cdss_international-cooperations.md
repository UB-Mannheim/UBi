# Doctoral Studies Abroad

CDSS students frequently take the opportunity to conduct part of their doctoral studies internationally.

**Note on Cooperation:**
The CDSS does not offer an organized exchange program with one specific institution. Instead, doctoral students, in collaboration with their supervisors, arrange visits and study periods at internationally recognized universities.

**Previously Visited Universities:**
CDSS students have recently completed visits at the following institutions:

- University College London
- University of Oxford
- Johns-Hopkins-University
- University of Montreal
- University of Barcelona
- Berkeley University of California, Simons Institute
- Oxford Brookes University
- Rice University
- University of Pavia
