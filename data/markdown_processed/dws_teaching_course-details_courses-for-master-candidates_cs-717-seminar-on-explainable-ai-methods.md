# Seminar CS 717: Understanding Explainable AI Methods (HWS 2026)

This seminar provides an in-depth exploration of Explainable AI (XAI) methods. The focus will be placed on Concept Bottleneck Models (CBMs), covering their architecture and practical applications. Students will learn how CBMs enhance interpretability by establishing a link between high-level human concepts and machine learning predictions.

## Seminar Goals and Learning Outcomes

Participants in this seminar will be expected to:

- Read, understand, and explore current scientific literature in the field.
- Summarize a current research topic in a concise report (10 single-column pages plus references).
- Deliver two presentations on the chosen topic: a 3-minute flash presentation and a 15-minute detailed presentation.
- Moderate a scientific discussion regarding a fellow student's topic.
- Review a draft report submitted by a peer.

## Organization Details

- **Instructor:** Prof. Dr.-Ing. Margret Keuper
- **Prerequisites:** A solid background in Machine Learning is required.
- **Capacity:** The maximum number of participants is limited to 6 Master's students.
