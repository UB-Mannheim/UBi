# Larissa Ginzinger

Larissa Katharina Ginzinger is a PhD candidate in the Center for Doctoral Studies in Business at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences.

### Academic and Professional Background

- **Education:** She holds a Bachelor’s degree in Economics and Business Administration and a Master’s degree in Economics and Finance from the Eberhard Karls University Tübingen.
- **International Experience:** During her studies, she completed a semester abroad at the Université Catholique de Louvain, Belgium.
- **Professional Experience:** Her practical experience includes roles such as a company valuation intern at Daimler AG and a research associate at the strategy research department at the Landesbank Baden-Württemberg (LBBW).
- **Research Interests:** Her primary areas of research focus on:
  - Sustainable Finance
  - Corporate Governance
  - Financial Economics
  - Political Economy
