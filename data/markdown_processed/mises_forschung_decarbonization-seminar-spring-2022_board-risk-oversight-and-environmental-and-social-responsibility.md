## Seminar Abstract: Risk Oversight and E&S Performance

Risk oversight has become a critical responsibility for corporate boards. Simultaneously, companies are increasingly recognizing the risks that social and environmental issues pose to investors and stakeholders.

In this study, the research examines the relationship between board risk oversight and a firm's environmental and social (E&S) ratings.

**Key Findings:**
Using proprietary data on the board risk oversight practices of firms from 29 countries, the study demonstrates that:

1. Firms with more robust risk oversight tend to have higher E&S ratings.
1. These firms are more likely to integrate E&S issues into their core strategies.
1. They are also more likely to incorporate E&S issues into executive compensation contracts, ultimately leading to better overall E&S outcomes.
