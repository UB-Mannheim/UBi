# Cooperation Partners

Since the start of the Research Training Group SMiP in October 2017, we have successfully cooperated with several institutions. Key partners include the [Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/) in Mannheim, particularly its [Center for Doctoral Studies in Social and Behavioral Sciences (CDSS)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/).

In 2018, we initiated our first international cooperation with the [Interuniversity Graduate School of Psychometrics and Sociometrics (IOPS)](https://www.iops.nl/). In 2020, we planned a cooperation with the Research Training Group Statistical Modeling of Complex Systems and Processes, though this collaboration had to be postponed due to the pandemic.

## Cooperation with GESS

The GESS facilitates cooperation by opening courses for SMiP members to participate in, and conversely, SMiP provides opportunities for CDSS students.

[Learn more about GESS cooperation](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/cooperations/gess/)

## Cooperation with IOPS

IOPS facilitates cooperation by opening courses for SMiP members to participate in, and conversely, SMiP provides opportunities for IOPS members.

[Learn more about IOPS cooperation](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/cooperations/iops/)
