# SyKo²W² (Synthesis of Completion and Correction for Knowledge Graphs on the Web)

The project SyKo²W² focuses on improving large Web Knowledge Graphs, such as [DBpedia](http://dbpedia.org/), [YAGO](http://www.mpi-inf.mpg.de/yago/), or [Wikidata](https://www.wikidata.org/).

## Key Idea

While methods have been proposed for both completing missing links and identifying incorrect links in knowledge graphs, most existing approaches focus on only one of these problems. The core objective of SyKo²W² is to develop methods that successfully combine both completion and correction within knowledge graphs.

## Expected Outcomes

The overall aim is to develop integrated methods for completion and correction in Web Knowledge Graphs. Intermediate milestones include:

- **Benchmark Dataset:** Collection of a systematic benchmark dataset.
- **Quantitative Comparison:** A quantitative comparison of existing approaches based on the collected dataset.
- **Pipelining Approaches:** Exploration of serial combinations of completion and correction approaches, both with and without a feedback loop.
- **Outlier Detection:** Development and application of model-based outlier detection methods, where the model predicts missing knowledge and the outlier detection finds wrong links.
- **Error Identification:** Development and application of prediction methods that identify errors as a side effect of the prediction process.

## Funding

The project is funded by the state of Baden-Württemberg under the funding scheme for assistant professors (Juniorprofessoren-Programm), with a total funding amount of 150k €.
