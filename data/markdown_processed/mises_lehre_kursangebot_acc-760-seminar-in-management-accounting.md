# ACC 760 Seminar in Management Accounting

This seminar provides an in-depth look at topics within management accounting, covering both theoretical concepts and practical applications.

### Course Objectives

Participants will develop essential managerial skills by addressing fundamental tools in management accounting. Key learning outcomes include:

- Gaining familiarity with management accounting concepts.
- Developing managerial skills crucial for the field.
- Learning to structure a seminar paper and improving academic writing proficiency.

### Course Structure and Details

- **Format:** The course primarily consists of independent group work and scheduled meetings with the course supervisor.
- **Credits:** 6 ECTS
- **Lecturers:** Gunther Glenk, Stefan Reichelstein, Nikolas Wölfing
