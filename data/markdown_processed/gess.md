# Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS)

The Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS) at the [University of Mannheim](http://www.uni-mannheim.de/1/english/) provides comprehensive graduate training. The focus is on empirical and quantitative methods, and their application across business, economics, and the social sciences.

## Doctoral Programs and Centers

GESS administers doctoral training through three specialized centers, each collaborating with a core department of the University of Mannheim:

### 💼 Center for Doctoral Studies in Business (CDSB)

- Administers the doctoral program at the [Business School](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/).
- The Business School is triple accredited by AACSB, EFDM (EQUIS), and AMBA.

### 📈 Center for Doctoral Studies in Economics (CDSE)

- Administers the doctoral program at the [Department of Economics](https://www.vwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/).

### 🧑‍🔬 Center for Doctoral Studies in Social and Behavioral Sciences (CDSS)

- Administers doctoral programs in Political Science, Psychology, Social Data Science, and Sociology.
- This center works in close collaboration with the [School of Social Sciences](https://www.sowi.uni-mannheim.de/en/).

### Academic Rankings

GESS programs provide access to world-class teaching and research, reflected in major rankings:

- **QS University Rankings 2025:** #1 in Germany in Accounting & Finance, #2 in Business & Management, and #3 in Economics & Econometrics (ranking by subject).
- **Shanghai Ranking 2025:** #2 in Germany in Political Science and #3 in Business Administration, Finance, and Management (ranking by subject).
- **Times Higher Education Ranking 2025:** #3 in Germany in Business & Economics and Social Sciences (ranking by subject).
