### Seminar Abstract

The talk will focus on Chevron's emissions reduction journey, demonstrating how a major company can address emissions in a cost-effective manner. Mr. Salas will provide a practical application on the optimization of MACCs using portfolio theory and discuss the critical need for standardized carbon accounting to unlock and accelerate emissions reduction by providing decision-useful information.

### Speaker Biography

Everett Salas is a Principal Strategy Advisor at Chevron New Energies, where he leads Decision and Economic Analysis.

With over a decade of experience, his expertise spans guiding infrastructure investments and developing decarbonization strategies. His deep knowledge includes financial modeling (NPV, ROI, IRR, payback, OpEx/CapEx forecasting) and probabilistic analysis. His professional focus areas are capital allocation, infrastructure finance, and portfolio optimization. He is recognized for his ability to translate complex data into clear decision insights, developing decision-support tools, and aligning diverse stakeholders to deliver actionable energy and climate solutions.
