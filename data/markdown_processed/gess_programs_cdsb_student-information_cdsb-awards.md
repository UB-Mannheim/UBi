# CDSB Doctoral Student Awards

The Center for Doctoral Studies in Business (CDSB) annually recognizes the achievements of its doctoral students through the CDSB Doctoral Student Awards. These awards highlight outstanding contributions across various academic areas, including research excellence, academic performance, publications, and teaching engagement.

## Award Categories

### Best Paper Award

This award honors an outstanding paper that has been accepted or has received an invitation for resubmission in a peer-reviewed journal.

### Award for Interdisciplinarity

This award recognizes outstanding performances and innovative projects undertaken by doctoral students in business that successfully foster interdisciplinarity.

### Teaching Award

The Teaching Award acknowledges the substantial contributions of CDSB doctoral students to the teaching and learning environment at the Business School.
