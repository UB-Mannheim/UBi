# Decarbonizing Transport: The Contribution of Biofuels

## Seminar Overview

This seminar provided an industry perspective on the role of biofuels in the decarbonization of the transport sector.

- **Topic:** Decarbonizing Transport – The Contribution of Biofuels. An industry perspective from CropEnergies.
- **Speaker:** Stephan Meeder
- **Affiliation:** CropEnergies AG (CEO)
- **Date:** September 13, 2021
- **Format:** Live virtual event

## Speaker Profile: Stephan Meeder

Stephan Meeder is the CEO of CropEnergies AG and is also the vice chairman of the European ethanol association ePURE.

**Professional Experience:**

- **Education:** Graduated with a degree in Business Administration from the University of Mannheim and the Ecole Supérieure des Sciences Economiques et Commerciales, Cergy-Pontoise, France. Earned his doctorate from the University of Mannheim (Faculty of Logistics) in 1999.
- **Early Career:** Worked for ABB Structured Finance GmbH (1996–2000). Worked for KPMG in the Corporate Finance Department (2000–2001). Worked at Robert Bosch GmbH in Corporate Finance and M&A (2001–2006).
- **Leadership Roles:**
  - Joined the Corporate Finance department of Südzucker AG (November 2006).
  - Appointed Chief Financial Officer of Saint Louis Sucre SA (May 2007).
  - Appointed to the executive board of CropEnergies AG as Chief Financial Officer (April 2015).
  - CEO of CropEnergies AG (Since July 2020).
