# Peiyuan Zhao

Peiyuan Zhao is a Research Assistant who joined the chair of Prof. Simons as a PhD student in 2024.

### Academic Background

- **Bachelor's Degree:** Business Administration (University of Mannheim).
- **Certificate:** Mannheim Data Analytics Certificate (Mannheim Business School).
- **Experience:** During his Master's studies at the University of Mannheim, he completed internships at PwC and Commerz Real AG.

### Research Interests

His research focuses on the intersection of corporate behavior and financial markets, specifically:

- Voluntary and Mandatory Disclosure
- Disclosure and Finance Market
- Corporate Disclosure Behavior
