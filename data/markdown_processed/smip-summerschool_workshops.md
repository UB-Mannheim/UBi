# Parallel Workshops of the Summer School

The Summer School offers specialized, hands-on workshops designed to provide deep dives into advanced statistical and computational methods relevant to psychology. Participants attend one workshop throughout the program.

### General Workshop Information

- **Format:** Each workshop provides an introduction to the dedicated method, practical applications, hands-on exercises, and an in-depth overview of advanced topics.
- **Participation:** Workshops are limited to a maximum of 15–20 participants.
- **Selection:** The specific workshop topic is selected at the time of registration.
- **Prerequisite:** Basic familiarity with the R programming language is required for all workshops.
