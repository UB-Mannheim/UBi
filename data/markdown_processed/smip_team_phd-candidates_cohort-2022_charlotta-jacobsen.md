# Charlotta Jacobsen

Charlotta Jacobsen is a PhD candidate whose research focuses on the stability and change of narcissism in relation to daily and life events.

## 🧑‍🔬 Kontaktdaten und Betreuung

- **Adresse:** RPTU Kaiserslautern-Landau, Fortstr. 7, Building H – Room 114, 76829 Landau
- **E-Mail:** [charlotta.jacobsen@rptu.de](mailto:charlotta.jacobsen@rptu.de)
- **Primary Advisor:** [Prof. Dr. Eunike Wetzel](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/researchers/prof-dr-eunike-wetzel/) (University of Kaiserslautern-Landau)
- **Additional Supervisors:** Benjamin Hilbig, Sabine Sonnentag
- **Dissertation Proposal:** Short-term Stability and Change in Narcissism in Relation to Daily and Life Events
