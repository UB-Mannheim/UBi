### Research Achievements and Conferences

**Best Paper Award at EURAM 2025**
At the EURAM 2025 in Florence, members of the research group received the Best Paper Award of the EURAM Special Interest Group Family Business.

- **Award Recipients:** Elisa Conz, Franciele Beck, Daniel Magalhães Mucci, and Tatiane Meurer.
- **Paper Title:** “Meaning and Practice of Tradition within Family Businesses: A Prenomenographic Enquiry.”
- **Study Overview:** The study used a qualitative approach (phenomenography) to investigate how tradition is understood and practiced in family businesses. It identified four distinct understandings of tradition: as a company feature, as a family feature, as a brand feature, and as a relational feature.

**23rd Interdisciplinary European Conference on Entrepreneurship Research (IECER) – Iceland**
The research group was actively involved in organizing the 23rd IECER, which took place from September 12–15, 2025, in Iceland. The conference saw over 150 submissions.

- **Organizing Members:** Julian Schenkenhofer, Dennis Steininger, Lina Uebbing, Iuliia Shkrabaliuk, Melania Riefolo, Teresa Fritsch, Giuliana Manganaro, Sahaya Kulandaisamy, Natalie Welch, and Jan-Philipp Ahrens.
- **Conference Tracks:** The call for papers covered diverse topics, including:
  - Entrepreneurs, Entrepreneurial Families, and Family Firms
  - Entrepreneurship and Resilience
  - Dreams in Entrepreneurship: Artificial Intelligence, Crowdfunding, and Future Forms of Business
  - Entrepreneurship in Crisis
  - Family Business
  - Female Entrepreneurship
  - International, Intercultural, and Migrant Entrepreneurship
  - Luxury, Fashion, Sports, Sustainability, Brands, & Brand Personality
  - Leadership, Strategy, Governance, Organization, & Innovation
