# Dr. Felix Bräuer

Dr. Felix Bräuer is affiliated with the University of Mannheim, specializing in Theoretical Philosophy and the Philosophy of Language.

## 🔬 Research Focus

My research centers on **epistemology**, with a particular focus on **social epistemology**, the **philosophy of language**, and the nature and function of **social norms**.

As part of ongoing research, I investigate the relationship between expressivism and Evans' transparency-thesis, particularly concerning problems of self-knowledge.

## 📚 Publications

- **2022:** "The transparency of expressivism," with Wolfgang Freitag. *Synthese* 200, 131. [doi.org/10.1007/s11229-022-03634-w](https://doi.org/10.1007/s11229-022-03634-w).
- **2021:** *Die Worte der Anderen. Eine Untersuchung zur epistemischen Abhängigkeit.* Paderborn, Brill mentis.
- **2021 (Online First):** "Assertion: The Constitutive Rule Account and the Engagement Condition Objection." *Erkenntnis*.
- **2018:** "Looking beyond reductionism and anti-reductionism." *Episteme*, 1–19.
- **2017:** "Testimonien, epistemische Gemeinschaften und praktische Risiken." *Deutsche Zeitschrift für Philosophie* 65(5), 851–871.
