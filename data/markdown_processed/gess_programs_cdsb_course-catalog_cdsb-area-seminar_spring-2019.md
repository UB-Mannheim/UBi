### Seminar Disciplines Covered

The seminar series covered research across several key business areas:

- **Accounting & Taxation:** Focused on topics like executive compensation, mandatory bail-in consequences, and external reporting innovation.
- **Finance:** Explored financial innovation, consumer risk, and institutional investor views on climate risk.
- **Management:** Addressed organizational behavior, cross-cultural effects of lateness, and corporate digital nudging.
- **Information Systems & Operations Management:** Covered topics related to crowdfunding decision-making and the spread of IT professional turnover.
- **Marketing:** Included sessions on market-specific topics (e.g., Tilburg School of Economics and Management).
