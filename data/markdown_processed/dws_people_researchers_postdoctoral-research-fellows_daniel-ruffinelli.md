## Research Profile

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow specializing in Natural Language Processing (NLP). My current research focuses on using interpretability methods to study how natural languages are represented internally by Large Language Models (LLMs).

I am also actively involved in:

- Advising PhD students on their research projects.
- Teaching advanced courses in LLMs and Information Retrieval.

My academic background includes a PhD focused on learning representations of knowledge graphs, supervised by [Prof. Dr. Rainer Gemulla](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/professors/prof-dr-rainer-gemulla/).

**Core Research Interests:**

- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Information Retrieval (IR)
- Representation Learning
- Machine Learning
