# Decarbonization Seminar Spring 2025

The **Decarbonization Seminar** in Spring 2025 brought together speakers and participants from academia and industry to discuss the latest advances and challenges associated with the transition towards a decarbonized energy economy.

### Organization and Sponsorship

The seminar was jointly sponsored by:

- **Mannheim Institute for Sustainable Energy Studies (MISES)**
- **Center for European Economic Research (ZEW)**

The organizing team included:

- [Gunther Glenk](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/area-accounting-taxation/team/gunther-glenk/) (MISES)
- [Marion Ott](https://www.zew.de/team/mot) (ZEW)
- [Nikolas Wölfing](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/area-accounting-taxation/team/dr-nikolas-woelfing/) (MISES)

*Note: The current version of the seminar can be found [here](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar-fall-2025/).*
