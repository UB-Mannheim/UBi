## Research Focus and Interests

Dr. Schumacher's research centers on the intersection of cognitive science and decision-making.

**Key Research Areas:**

- Decision-making processes
- Dynamics in cognition
- Cognitive process models (e.g., Evidence accumulation models, Reinforcement learning models)
- Bayesian statistics
- Simulation-based inference
