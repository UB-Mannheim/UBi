## SMiP IOPS Conference 2024 Overview

The SMiP IOPS Conference took place on **July 20th and 21st, 2024**, in Mannheim. The event welcomed members of SMiP and IOPS, featuring a comprehensive program that included talks, poster presentations, and the presentation of annual awards.

### Keynote Talk

**Speaker:** Prof. Dr. Daniel W. Heck (University of Marburg)
**Topic:** Cognitive and psychometric modeling of truth judgments

Prof. Heck presented on the formal statistical modeling of truth judgments, addressing the "repetition-based truth effect." He highlighted the benefits of using precise mathematical equations over simulation-based models for testing theories, particularly regarding the moderating role of statement plausibility. The talk emphasized the importance of model identifiability, auxiliary assumptions, and validation studies, illustrating how cultural-consensus models can provide a psychometric solution for estimating both the truth status of statements and participant expertise, especially in settings like fact-checking where the actual truth status may be unknown.
