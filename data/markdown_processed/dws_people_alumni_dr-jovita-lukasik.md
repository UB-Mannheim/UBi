# Jovita Lukasik

## 🧑‍🔬 Research Overview

Jovita Lukasik is a Ph.D. candidate specializing in Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Her research is conducted within the Data and Web Science Group at the University of Mannheim and the Computer Vision and Machine Learning group at the Max-Planck-Institute for Informatics.

**Supervision:** Prof. Dr.-Ing. Margret Keuper

**Research Interests:**

- Computer Vision
- Machine Learning
- Neural Architecture Search (NAS)
- AutoML

**Professional Activities:**

- Co-organizing a series of virtual seminars on AutoML.
- Was part of the organization team for the second NAS workshop at ICLR 2021.
