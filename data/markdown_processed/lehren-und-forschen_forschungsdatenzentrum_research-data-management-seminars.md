# Research Data Management Seminars

The **Research Data Management Seminars** at the University of Mannheim offer valuable insights, tools, and strategies for effectively managing research data. 

## Target Audience

These seminars are designed for (junior) researchers and advanced master's students. 

## Seminar Topics

The topics covered include:

- Data management planning
- Data collection and replication
- Data archiving and publication

These sessions aim to equip participants with the necessary skills to handle their research data efficiently. For more information, visit the [Research Data Management Seminars page](https://www.bib.uni-mannheim.de/lehren-und-forschen/forschungsdatenzentrum/research-data-management-seminars/).
