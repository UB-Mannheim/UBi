# Research Report: Valuation of Used Capacity Assets

This document provides access to a technical report detailing the fair market value of used capacity assets, specifically focusing on the forecasting and repurposing of electric vehicle (EV) batteries.

## 📄 Document Details

**Title:** Fair Market Value of Used Capacity Assets: Forecasts for Repurposed Electric Vehicle Batteries
**Format:** PDF Report
**Source:** University of Mannheim

**[Download Report (PDF)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/media/Einrichtungen/mises/Dokumente/Fair_Market_Value_of_Used_Capacity_Assets-2.pdf)**

## 💡 Key Focus Areas

The report addresses critical aspects of the circular economy within the automotive sector, including:

- **Second Life Applications:** Analyzing the potential for repurposing used EV batteries beyond their initial vehicle use.
- **Market Valuation:** Providing forecasts and methodologies for determining the fair market value of these used capacity assets.
- **Sustainability:** Contributing research to sustainable energy storage solutions and resource management.
