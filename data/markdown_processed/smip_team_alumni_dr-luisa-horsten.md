## 🧠 Research Interests

Dr. Horsten's research focuses on the intersection of personality, decision-making, and environmental behavior. Key areas of interest include:

- **Pro-environmental Behavior:** Examining the decision conflict between cooperation and pro-environmental actions.
- **Personality Variables:** Investigating personality traits related to prosocial and pro-environmental behavior.
- **Personality Structure:** Exploring models of personality structure, including the "Dark factor of personality."

*Dr. Horsten is open for consultation and/or collaboration.*
