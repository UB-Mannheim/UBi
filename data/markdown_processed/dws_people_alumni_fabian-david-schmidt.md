## Academic Profile

Fabian David Schmidt is a Ph.D. candidate within the [Chair of Text Analytics for Interdisciplinary Research](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/research/focus-groups/natural-language-processing-and-information-retrieval-prof-ponzetto/). He is supervised by [Prof. Dr. Goran Glavaš](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/professors/prof-dr-goran-glavas/) and is affiliated with the [Data and Web Science Group](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/home/) at the [University of Mannheim](http://www.uni-mannheim.de/1/english/).

## Research Focus

**Research Interests:**

- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Information Retrieval
- Text Analytics

**Projects:**

- **EU In Action:** Applying state-of-the-art natural language processing methods to political text. This project aims to determine the drivers and explain the mechanics of legislative changes at the European level by policy area, conducted in cooperation with the University of Leiden and the University of Strathclyde.

## Publications
