# IE 560 Foundations of Artificial Intelligence – Reasoning and Decision Making

This course provides a foundational introduction to the principles and methods underlying artificial intelligence, with a specific focus on reasoning and decision making.

## Course Topics Covered

The curriculum covers several core areas of AI:

- Logic-based knowledge representation and reasoning
- Probabilistic graphical models for reasoning under uncertainty
- Decision theory and rational decision making
- Markov Decision Processes (MDPs)
- Reinforcement Learning (RL)
