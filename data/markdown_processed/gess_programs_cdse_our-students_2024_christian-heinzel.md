# Christian Heinzel

Christian Heinzel is a student in the Economics department, currently enrolled in the M.Sc. programme via the Master of Economic Research (MAER) track.

### Academic Profile

Christian was born in 1999 in Krefeld, Germany. He obtained his bachelor’s degree in Economics with honours from Maastricht University. During his studies, he gained practical experience working as a student aide to a member of the German Bundestag budget committee, focusing specifically on European fiscal policy.

His primary research interest lies in Public Economics, with a specialized focus on European Union and member state fiscal policy.

In his free time, he enjoys hiking, trail running, and reading political philosophy and modern history.
