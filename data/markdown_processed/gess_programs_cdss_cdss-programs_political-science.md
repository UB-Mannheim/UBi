# Political Science Programs and Research

## CDSS Doctoral Program in Political Science

The **CDSS Doctoral Program in Political Science** offers courses in topical areas of research and advanced methods. These advanced methods provide students with tools for rigorous and thorough theoretical and empirical analysis, including:

- Theories of rational and social choice
- Game theory
- Bayesian learning models
- Advanced quantitative methods, such as multivariate analysis (OLS) and maximum likelihood estimation.

[CDSS course catalog](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/course-catalog/)

## Research at the Department of Political Science

Research at the [Department of Political Science](https://www.sowi.uni-mannheim.de/en/research/political-science/) is characterized by an empirical-analytical approach. The department utilizes quantitative methods for data analysis and covers a broad range of topics. Empirical-analytical research with a quantitative focus, methodological diversity, and interdisciplinarity are central to the research conducted within the department and the [School of Social Sciences](https://www.sowi.uni-mannheim.de/en/).

**Main Research Fields:**

- Elections and party competition in established democracies, as well as processes of democratization and political decision-making in other forms of government.
- The European integration process.
- National conflicts and civil wars.
- Drivers and consequences of reform processes.

Research in the subject area of political science is typically conducted at the [Mannheim Center for European Social Research (MZES)](https://www.sowi.uni-mannheim.de/en/research/political-science/).

## Academic Collaborations

Research at the chairs of Political Economy, Political Sociology, and Political Psychology serves as a bridge between political science and closely related fields like sociology and psychology.

The School of Social Sciences maintains close collaborations with:

- [GESIS Leibniz Institute for the Social Sciences](https://www.gesis.org/en/home/)
- [Centre for European Economic Research (ZEW)](https://www.zew.de/en/)
