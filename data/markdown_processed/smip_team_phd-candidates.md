# PhD Candidates in the SMiP Research Training Group

PhD Candidates join the Research Training Group SMiP regularly in October when a new cohort begins the PhD program. The first PhD Candidates joined the group in October 2017.

The SMiP PhD Candidates are encouraged to actively contribute to the PhD program and the SMiP Research Training Group as a whole.
