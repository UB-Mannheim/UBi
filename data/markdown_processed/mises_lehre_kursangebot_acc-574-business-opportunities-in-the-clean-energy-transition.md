# ACC 574: Business Opportunities in the Clean Energy Transition

This course provides an examination of business models and opportunities specifically related to clean energy and decarbonized energy.

The curriculum examines emerging trends within the context of technological change, fundamental business principles, and the parameters set by public policy.

### Key Topics Covered

The course will specifically address the following critical areas:

- Carbon Emissions and the Clean Energy Transition
- Modeling Cost Competitiveness of Alternative Energy Technologies
- The Momentum of Renewable Energy
- Integration of Renewable Energy Sources
- Energy Storage Solutions
- Sustainable Transportation
