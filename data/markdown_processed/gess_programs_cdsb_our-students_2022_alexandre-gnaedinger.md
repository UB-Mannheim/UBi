# Alexandre Gnaedinger, M.Sc.

**Year:** 2022
**Center:** Business

## Profil

Alexandre Gnaedinger was born in Nancy, France. His academic journey began in 2013, where he studied Industrial Engineering at the French Grande Ecole University of Technology of Troyes. He completed his Bachelor of Economics from the University Grenoble Alpes in 2020 and subsequently joined the Economics, Data Analytics and Corporate Finance Master program at Ecole Polytechnique.

Throughout his studies, Alexandre gained significant professional experience, including two years as an engineer, a research internship at HEC Paris, and a role as a research assistant at the European Tax Observatory (PSE). Prior to joining the Taxation Track in 2022, he completed a traineeship in financial regulation at the European Central Bank.

**Research Interests:**

- Political Economy
- Tax evasion
- Tax competition
