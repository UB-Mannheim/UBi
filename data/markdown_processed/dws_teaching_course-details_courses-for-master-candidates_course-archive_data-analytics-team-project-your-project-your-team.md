## Data Analytics Team Project (HWS25)

This team project is designed for students who wish to work on a project of their own choosing with a team of their choice. The project must be related to [machine learning and/or data processing](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/research/focus-groups/data-analytics-prof-gemulla/) and align with predefined scope and goals, while maintaining flexibility.

**Ideal Project Focus:**

- Projects that generate assets useful to a wider community.
- Projects that explore a relevant research question (with the potential goal of publishing results in relevant academic venues).

### 🗓️ Application and Timeline

- **Application Deadline:** Students must submit a short proposal and a list of team members to [Prof. Dr. Rainer Gemulla](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/professors/prof-dr-rainer-gemulla/) by **August 17, 2025**.
- **Project Refinement:** Discussions and project refinement will take place throughout August.
- **Decision:** Final decisions regarding project acceptance will be made by the beginning of September.
- **Start Date:** Accepted projects begin alongside regular team projects in September.
- **Alternative:** If the primary project is not accepted, students are encouraged to apply for regular team projects, as sufficient time will be available.
