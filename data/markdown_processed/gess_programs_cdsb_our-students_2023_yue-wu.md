# Yue Wu

Yue Wu is a student joining the Finance track at the Center for Doctoral Studies in Business (GESS) in 2023.

## Academic and Professional Background

Yue Wu was born in Shandong, China. Her academic journey includes:

- **Nanyang Technological University (Singapore):** Obtained an MSc. in Finance.
- **École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL):** Spent one year as a research assistant specializing in quantitative finance.

## Research Experience and Focus Areas

Yue has extensive experience in green finance, quantitative finance, and development economics, gained through several key roles:

- **Tsinghua University:** Served as a junior research fellow at the Research Center for Green Finance Development. Her work focused on green finance policy and green bond empirical research. She was involved in major green finance research programs in collaboration with institutions such as the People’s Bank of China, the World Bank, and Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ).
- **National School of Development (Peking University):** Spent six months with the Macro and Green Finance Lab, continuing her research on green finance policy and carbon markets.
- **Development Research Center (DRC) of the State Council (China):** Worked as an intern research fellow. Her research covered development economics, particularly focusing on the global GDP growth rate slowdown and potential stagflation. She is a co-author of the Global Development Report by DRC.
