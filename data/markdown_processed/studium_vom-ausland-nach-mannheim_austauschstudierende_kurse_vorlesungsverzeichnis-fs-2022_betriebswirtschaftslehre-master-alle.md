## Course Catalog for Exchange Students (Spring 2022)

### Eligibility Requirements

The selection of suitable courses depends on your current academic level:

- **If you are a Bachelor's student:** You must select business courses only from the bachelor's level catalog. Master's level courses are generally not open to undergraduate students. Exceptions are only possible if you are in your 4th year of an undergraduate program when you arrive in Mannheim.
- **If you are a Master's/MBA student:** You are eligible to choose business courses from all master's level courses. If your home university permits you to take selected bachelor's level courses, these are also available to you.

**For questions regarding eligibility,** please contact the Business School Exchange Team via incoming.business@uni-mannheim.de.

### Available Course Areas

The following areas offer specialized modules:

- Area Accounting and Taxation
- Area Finance
- Area Information Systems
- Area Management
- Area Marketing
- Area Operations Management

### Detailed Module Information

#### Information Systems (IS)

For a detailed course description, please consult the [Modulkatalog MMM | Universität Mannheim](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/modulkatalog-mmm/is-540-management-of-enterprise-systems/).

**Important Application Notes:**

- This course has limited capacity.
- Applications must be submitted using the chair's [application tool](https://wi1vm-web.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/cvportal/#/upload). This tool is accessible only within the university network or via [VPN](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/it/services/internet-and-servers/vpn/).
- If you are applying for IS 613 and IS 615, a single application is sufficient; however, you must indicate that you are applying for both courses.
- Remember to register via Portal2 as well.

#### Marketing

All lectures for this module will be uploaded as recordings to [ILIAS](https://ilias.uni-mannheim.de/ilias.php?baseClass=ilrepositorygui&ref_id=1491552).

For information regarding the schedule of lectures and exercise sessions, please visit the ILIAS group. Select "Folders" and "Dateien," then "Lecture Slides and Recordings," followed by "Lecture 00 Organizational Details," and finally the PDF file "Lecture 0."
