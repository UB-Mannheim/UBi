# Data Analytics Team Project: Your Project, Your Team (FSS26)

This team project is designed for students who wish to work on a self-selected project with a chosen team. The core focus must be related to **machine learning and/or data processing** [Data Analytics Prof. Gemulla](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/research/focus-groups/data-analytics-prof-gemulla/).

### Project Scope and Goals

The project must align with predefined scope and goals but maintains flexibility regarding the specific topic. Ideal candidates for this project are those that:

1. Produce assets useful to a wider community.
1. Explore a relevant research question with the goal of publishing the results in relevant academic venues.

### Key Project Details

- **Supervisor:** [Prof. Dr. Rainer Gemulla](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/professors/prof-dr-rainer-gemulla/)
- **Course Type:** Team project
- **ECTS Points:** 12 ECTS
- **Topic:** Student choice (must relate to ML/Data Processing)
- **Participants:** 3 – 6 students
- **Duration:** 6 months
- **Language:** English
- **Applicable For:** Master of Business Informatics, MMDS

### Application Process

To participate, students must submit a short proposal and a list of team members.

- **Application Deadline:** January 18, 2026
- **Submission:** Proposals must be sent to Rainer Gemulla.
- **Timeline:** Accepted projects will begin in February. The project will be discussed and refined throughout January, with final decisions made by the beginning of February.
- **Alternative:** If the project is not accepted, students are encouraged to apply for regular team projects, as sufficient time will be available.
