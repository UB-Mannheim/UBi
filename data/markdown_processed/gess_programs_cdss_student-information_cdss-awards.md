# CDSS Doctoral Student Awards

The Center for Doctoral Studies in Social and Behavioral Sciences (CDSS) annually honors its doctoral students through the CDSS Doctoral Student Awards. These awards recognize outstanding achievements in various areas, including research, academic performance, publications, and teaching contributions.

## GESS Young Scholar Award in the Social Sciences

This award is designed to honor exceptional ideas and achievements in doctoral research and to support professional activities that advance the professionalization and networking of young scientists.

**Winners:**

- **2025:** [Lisa-Marie Müller](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/our-students/2023/lisa-marie-mueller/), [Hannah Rajski](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/our-students/2022/hannah-rajski/), & [Malena Ullrich](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/our-students/2024/malena-ullrich/) for their project 'Women's Square – Mannheim Network of Female Social Scientists'
- **2025:** [Leonard Wendering](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/our-students/2023/leonard-wendering/) for his project 'Triadic Competition: An Experiment on Triadic Contact among East, West, and Turkish-Germans'
- [Winners of previous years (PDF, 110 kB)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/media/Einrichtungen/GESS/Dokumente/CDSS/Winners_YSA.pdf)

## Teaching Award

This award recognizes the substantial contributions of CDSS doctoral students to teaching and learning at the School of Social Sciences.

**Winner:**

- **2025:** [Julius Diener](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/our-students/2023/julius-diener/)
- [Winners of previous years (PDF, 85 kB)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/media/Einrichtungen/GESS/Dokumente/CDSS/Winners_TA.pdf)

## Publication of the Year Award

This award recognizes an outstanding publication that was published or accepted for publication in a peer-reviewed journal during a given academic year.

**Winner:**

- **2025:** [Johanna Hölzl](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/our-students/2021/johanna-hoelzl-geb-mehltretter/) et al. for '[The (mis)use of Google Trends data in the social sciences – ](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0049089X24001212)[A systematic review, critique, and recommendations](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0049089X24001212)' in Social Science Research
- [Winners of previous years (PDF, 150 kB)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/media/Einrichtungen/GESS/Dokumente/CDSS/Winners_PA.pdf)
