## CDSE Seminar Fall 2022 Schedule

This document outlines the schedule and topics for the CDSE Seminar in Fall 2022. All sessions took place at L7, 3–5, 001.

### Seminar Sessions

The seminar ran from September 6, 2022, through December 6, 2022, with sessions held every two weeks.

- **September 6, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** Levelling the Playing Field? Knowledge Production in the Digital Age
  - **Speaker:** Maximilian Mähr

- **September 13, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** Pay-as-bid Auctions with Private Information
  - **Speaker:** Daniel Linnenbrink

- **September 20, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** Can Work from Home Reduce the Gender Wage Gap?
  - **Speaker:** Ursula Berresheim

- **September 27, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** Do Stereotypes Cause the Gender Competition Gap? / More Now, Less Later? The Behavioral Effects of Parliamentary Pension Reform
  - **Speakers:** Michael Hilweg / Felix Köhler

- **October 4, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** Access to Renewable Energy: Micro-Finance for Small Businesses and Micro Enterprises in Pakistan.
  - **Speaker:** David Müller

- **October 11, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** On the Relevance of Perceived Relative Incomes: Spousal Income Splitting and Biased Intra-Household Bargaining
  - **Speaker:** Jakob Schmidhäuser

- **October 18, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** Road to Green? The Effect of Highway Expansion on Exporting Firms' Emissions
  - **Speaker:** Siyu Zhang

- **October 25, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** Physical Climate Risk, Mortgage Lending and Bank Solvency
  - **Speaker:** Marina Hoch

- **November 8, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** Tiebout in the 21st Century – Local Fiscal Competition, Foot Voting and Legacy Debt
  - **Speaker:** Paul Steger

- **November 15, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** Prescription Drug Co-payments and Health Outcomes: Evidence from Insulin
  - **Speaker:** Matilde Cappelletti

- **November 22, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** From Confiscation to Competition: the Effect of Goods Re-allocation in Italy
  - **Speaker:** Luca Currado

- **November 29, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** Why Don't You Deduct to Donate More? A Field Experiment with Carol Singers / The Consequences of Rural-to-urban Migration: Evidence from the Indiana Gas Boom
  - **Speakers:** Argun Aman / Assaf Abraham

- **December 6, 2022** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):

  - **Topic:** Household Trading & Performance along Stock Price Cycles
  - **Speaker:** Ulrich Roschitsch
