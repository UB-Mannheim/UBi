## Rongrong Wang

**Role:** Doctoral Student (Doktorandin)
**Program:** CDSB, GESS (Finance track)
**Year:** 2022

### Academic Profile

Rongrong Wang was born in Anhui, China, in 1998. She obtained her bachelor’s degree in Accounting from Hohai University, China. During her studies, she gained experience assisting in establishing an internal business journals database and completed an internship at an energy-recycling corporation.

She pursued her master’s degree in International Accounting and Finance from Bayes Business School (formerly Cass) in Britain. She joined the Finance track at the CDSB, GESS, in 2022.

### Research Interests

- Corporate Governance
- Social Corporate Responsibility
- Mergers and Acquisitions
