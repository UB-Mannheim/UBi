### Speaker Biography

Sorabh Tomar is an Assistant Professor of Accounting at Southern Methodist University's Cox School of Business. He holds a PhD in Accounting from the University of Chicago.

His research focuses on the effects of disclosure regulation in both corporate and non-corporate settings. Specifically, he has empirically investigated:

- Whether disclosure can ameliorate a negative externality driven by firm outputs.
- How a change in performance-disclosure regime affects a contracting agent's effort.

Tomar has a growing interest in firms' CSR/environmental performance disclosures, believing that the prominence of these disclosures will only increase over time. He earned his bachelor's degrees in Commerce and Science at The University of Melbourne.
