# Felix Feuerstein

Felix Feuerstein is a student who earned his first Bachelor's degree in Business Administration through a dual study program at DHBW Mannheim in cooperation with a large German bank. He subsequently focused on Economics, obtaining two additional Bachelor's degrees from the University of Mannheim: one in Economics (with a minor in Mathematics) and one in Mathematics in Business and Economics.

He is currently pursuing his Master's in Economics at the University of Mannheim and joined the CDSE via the Master of Economic Research (MAER) track.

### Academic and Professional Focus

- **Experience:** During his studies, he continued working in the banking sector, specializing in credit risk.
- **Research Interests:** His academic interests include microeconomic theory, game theory, and asset pricing.
