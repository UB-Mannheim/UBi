## Academic Background and Research Interests

Naifeng Liu, who was born in Shanghai, China, initially studied computer science, earning degrees from the University of Shanghai for Science and Technology, The State University of New York at Albany, and the CUNY Graduate Center. His academic focus later shifted towards economics, leading him to pursue further study at the CDSE.

His current research interests are highly interdisciplinary, focusing on:

- Game Theory
- Political Economy
- Industrial Organization

He is particularly interested in applying advanced techniques from theoretical computer science, such as approximation algorithms and complexity theory, to solve problems within economics.
