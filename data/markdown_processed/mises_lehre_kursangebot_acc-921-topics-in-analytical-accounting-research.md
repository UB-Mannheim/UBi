# ACC 921: Topics in Analytical Accounting Research

This course provides an advanced introduction to the role of accounting information across several critical areas of business management and finance.

## Target Audience

The course is specifically designed for doctoral students in accounting and related fields, including:

- Economics
- Finance
- Political Management
- Operations Management

## Core Topics Covered

The curriculum provides in-depth coverage of how accounting information is used to measure and analyze:

1. **Cost and Profitability:** Measuring the efficiency and profitability of operations.
1. **Managerial Performance:** Utilizing accounting-based metrics for evaluating managerial performance.
1. **Cost Allocation and Pricing:** Techniques for cost allocation and internal pricing within multi-divisional firms.
1. **Financial Analysis:** Understanding financial ratios and firm valuation methods.
