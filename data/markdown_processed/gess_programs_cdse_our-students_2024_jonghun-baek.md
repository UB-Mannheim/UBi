## Jonghun Baek

Jonghun Baek is a student with a strong academic background in economics, specializing in labor economics and microeconometrics.

### Academic Background & Research Interests

- **Education:** He obtained his Master’s degree in Economics from the University of Bonn and an advanced Master’s degree in Economics from KU Leuven.
- **Research Focus:** His interests combine labor economics and microeconometrics. During his studies, he focused on estimating the impact of labor unions on wages using marginal treatment effects with semi-IV methods (during his time at KU Leuven).
- **Specialization:** His master's thesis at Bonn focused on developing IV estimators for functional data. He also gained experience working as a research assistant in Bonn, specializing in textual analysis.
