# Marcel Schmitt, M.Sc.

Marcel Schmitt is an alumnus of the SMiP Cohort 2020 and serves as an SMiP mentor. For general inquiries, please contact the coordination center.

## Professional Profile

**Current Position:** Researcher
**Department:** Research Methods and Evaluation
**Institution:** RPTU Kaiserslautern-Landau
[More Information](https://psy.rptu.de/en/wus/personality-psychological-assessment-and-psychological-methods/research-methods-and-evaluation/team/academic-staff/marcel-schmitt)

## Research Focus

Marcel's research interests lie in advanced statistical modeling and psychological data analysis:

- Modeling of intensive longitudinal data
- Latent class models
- Emotion dynamics

## Academic Background

**PhD Thesis Topic:** Between-person and within-person variability in emotional granularity: Substantive insights and methodological contributions
**Degree:** Dr. phil. (Expected July 2025)
**SMiP Dissertation Committee:** Tanja Lischetzke, Thorsten Meiser, Sabine Sonnentag, Eunike Wetzel
