# ENGAGE.EU eJourney Online Help

## Introduction to eJourney

**eJourney** is an application developed by the university alliance **ENGAGE.EU**. It serves as the central repository for documenting all learner achievements within the alliance, acting as the **memory core** of the **ENGAGE.EU** learning journey.

By consolidating various accomplishments, **eJourney** enables the creation of an **individual learning account**, supporting students throughout their educational pathway.

*Note: Currently, only a few features have been implemented, primarily focusing on staff tasks. However, the system will be gradually expanded over the next three years to include additional functionalities.*
