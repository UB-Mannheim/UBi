# Gunther Glenk: Corporate Decarbonization Seminar

**Event Details**

- **Topic:** Carbon Emission Pledges and Corporate Decarbonization Initiatives
- **Date:** September 27, 2023
- **Location:** Frankfurt
- **Affiliations:** University of Mannheim and Harvard Business School
- **Admission:** Invitation only (due to limited seating capacity)

## Speaker Profile: Gunther Glenk

[Gunther Glenk](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar-fall-2023/gunther-glenk/) is an Assistant Professor for Business at the University of Mannheim and a Climate Fellow at Harvard Business School.

**Research Focus**
His research centers on corporate transitions toward zero net emissions. Key areas of expertise include:

- The economics and management of corporate carbon emissions.
- Decarbonization and sustainable energy technologies.
- Incentives for climate action.

**Recent Work**
His recent work has specifically focused on the competitiveness of clean energy technologies, such as:

- Energy storage
- Renewable hydrogen
- Electric mobility

**Education**
He obtained his BSc, MSc, and Doctorate in Management and Technology from the Technical University of Munich.
