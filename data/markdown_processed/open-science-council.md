# Open Science Council

The Open Science Council functions as an advisory body that supports the Open Science Office at the University of Mannheim. Its primary role is to provide guidance and support to researchers who are adopting Open Science practices within the university.

## Composition and Membership

The Council is composed of the Vice President for Research and Academic Career Paths for the University of Mannheim, alongside three external Open Science experts.

### Current Members

The council includes representatives from various academic and research fields:

- **Vice President for Research and Academic Career Paths** (University of Mannheim)
- **External Expert 1:** Apl. Professor of Psychological Methodology and Diagnostics and Managing Director of the LMU Open Science Center.
- **External Expert 2:** Professor for Business and Psychology, Wirtschaftsuniversität Wien, Department of Strategy and Innovation, Institute for Cognition and Behavior.
