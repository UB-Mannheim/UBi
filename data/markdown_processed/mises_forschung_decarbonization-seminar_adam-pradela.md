## Seminar Abstract

The DHL Group has set ambitious sustainability goals and successfully integrated sustainability into its core strategy and steering processes. The finance department is leading the Group's sustainability agenda by establishing integrated and structured reporting and controlling processes. The seminar emphasizes that sustainability must be given the same priority as financial data at all decision-making levels.

## Speaker Biography

Adam Pradela has served as the CFO Corporate Sustainability for the DHL Group since March 2024. In this role, he is responsible for consolidating all sustainability activities within the finance area, aiming to integrate sustainability topics more closely into the steering processes.

Since joining the DHL Group in 2002, he has held various regional CFO positions and was most recently the EVP Corporate Accounting, Reporting & Controlling.

His professional background includes:

- Graduating in business administration from the University of Cologne (1989).
- Holding senior finance positions at multinational companies such as Unilever, Pepsi-Cola, and Tetra Pak.
- Serving as a member of the Transition Implementation Group at the International Sustainability Standards Board (ISSB).
