## Taro Fujita

Taro Fujita is a student in the Economics center, profiled in 2022.

### Academic and Professional Background

- **Origin:** Born in 1990 in Shizuoka, Japan.
- **Education:**
  - Bachelor’s degree in Economics from Hitotsubashi University.
  - Master’s degree in International Economics and Public Policy from Johannes Gutenberg-Universität Mainz.
- **Professional Experience:** Prior to joining CDSE, he worked for eight years as a financial analyst at the Bank of Japan.

### Research Interests and Supervision

Taro is interested in microeconometrics, specifically focusing on the intersection of causal inference and machine learning.

- **Supervisor:** [Prof. Dr. Christoph Rothe](https://www.vwl.uni-mannheim.de/rothe/)
- **Interests:** He is also enthusiastic about Mahjong, a poker-like Asian board game.
