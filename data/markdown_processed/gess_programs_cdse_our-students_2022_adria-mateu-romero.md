# Adrià Mateu Romero

Adrià Mateu Romero is a student whose research interests lie in macroeconomics, particularly growth and development, international economics, and labor economics.

### Academic Background and Experience

- **Education:** Adrià was born in 1997 in Barcelona. He obtained his bachelor’s degree in economics from Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF). He subsequently completed his master’s studies in International Trade, Finance and Development at the Barcelona School of Economics (BSE).
- **Professional Experience:** During his studies, Adrià gained experience working as a research assistant for Professor Carles Vergara at the IESE Business School and as an adjunct lecturer at UPF.
- **Research Focus:** His main research interest is macroeconomics, with a specific focus on growth and development and international economics. His master thesis investigates the effects of growing Chinese import penetration on Italian firm dynamics.
