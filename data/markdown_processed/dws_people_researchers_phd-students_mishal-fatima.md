## 🔬 Research Focus

Mishal Fatima is a Ph.D. candidate specializing in Machine Learning, supervised by [Prof. Margret Keuper](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/professors/prof-dr-ing-margret-keuper/).

**Core Interests:**
The research focuses on deep learning applications for practically efficient computer vision tasks. The work explores novel model architectures and data-driven approaches aimed at improving the robustness and efficiency of vision systems. A key area of investigation is the co-designing of sensor system parameters with neural networks.

**Current Project:**
The research is conducted within the inter-disciplinary DFG research unit, [Learning to Sense](https://www.learning2sense.de/), which investigates how sensor systems can be co-designed with neural networks.
