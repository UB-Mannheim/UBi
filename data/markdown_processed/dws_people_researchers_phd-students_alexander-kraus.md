## Academic Background and Supervision

Alexander Kraus is a Ph.D. candidate within the [Data and Web Science Group](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/) at the [School of Business Informatics and Mathematics](https://www.wim.uni-mannheim.de/) of the [University of Mannheim](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/). He is supervised by Prof. Dr. Han van der Aa.

## Research Focus

His primary research interests include:

- Process Mining
- System-state analytics of business processes (current focus)
