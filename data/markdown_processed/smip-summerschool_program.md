## Program Components

### Parallel Workshops

Each participant is assigned to one thematic workshop throughout the summer school. These workshops involve 15–20 participants and cover selected subjects of statistical modeling and quantitative methods. The content ranges from basic introductions to advanced issues and new developments in the field, including practical exercises.
[View workshop details](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/workshops/)

### Keynote Talks

Keynote talks provide an overview of current cutting-edge topics of common interest in the domain of statistical modeling and quantitative methods. These talks are presented by invited experts in their respective fields and are held as plenary sessions for all participants and instructors.
[View keynote details](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/keynotes/)

### Poster Session

Participants are invited to present a poster of their ongoing project during the poster session. This session allows young researchers to discuss their work with peers and senior researchers, including workshop instructors. Furthermore, an active poster presentation may facilitate the reimbursement of travel and accommodation costs from participants’ home institutions. The session includes refreshments and snacks.

### Get-together: Compatibility of Work and Private Life

This dedicated get-together offers an opportunity for summer school participants interested in the topic to meet successful researchers who will provide insights into their individual approaches to balancing work and private life.
[More information](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/program/compatibility-of-work-and-private-life/)
