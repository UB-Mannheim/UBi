# Machine Learning and Computer Vision Group

The research group focuses on advanced topics in **Computer Vision** and **Machine Learning**.

## 🔬 Research Focus Areas

Our research interests are highly interdisciplinary and include:

- Robustness and Reliability of Deep Learning Models
- Neural Architecture Search (NAS)
- Grouping Problems (e.g., Image and Motion Segmentation, Multiple Object Tracking)
- Efficient Solvers for Large Grouping Problems
- Motion Estimation
- Image Generation and Deep Fake Detection
- Vision for Medical Imaging
- Understanding and building Vision Language Models (VLMs) and other Vision Foundation Models
