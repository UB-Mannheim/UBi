# Johannes Lattmann

Johannes Lattmann is a PhD candidate in Political Science at the Center for Digital Social Science (CDSS). He is also listed as an academic staff member.

## Academic Background and Experience

Johannes completed his B.A. in Social Sciences at Humboldt University in Berlin and his M.Sc. in Political Science & Political Economy at the London School of Economics (LSE) in London. During his studies, he gained experience working as a research assistant at Humboldt University, LSE, and the Social Research Center in Berlin.

## Research Focus

Johannes' research is primarily focused on the causal analysis of political behavior. Key areas of study include:

- Polarization dynamics
- Voting decisions
- Protests and the occurrence of political violence

Methodologically, he has utilized quasi-experimental designs. He is also interested in applying automated text analysis to political science and has examined the economic and political impact of centralized funding allocations on local regions.
