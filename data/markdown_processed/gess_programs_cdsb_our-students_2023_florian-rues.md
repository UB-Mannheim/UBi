# Florian Rues

[Florian Rues](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdsb/our-students/2023/florian-rues/)

## Academic Profile

Florian Rues is a student who joined the Management track of the Center for Doctoral Studies in Business (CDS) in 2023.

**Education:**

- **Bachelor's Degree:** Studied Business Administration with a focus on International Management and Data Mining at the University of Applied Sciences in Kempten.
- **Master's Degree:** Completed the Mannheim Master in Management program, specializing in Strategic Management and Finance.
- **Thesis Focus:** His Master Thesis provided an empirical investigation into the causes and effects of the global shrinking stock markets phenomenon.

**Experience:**
Florian has gained practical experience through various internships and working student positions in both large corporations and smaller start-ups.

## Research Interests

His primary areas of academic and professional interest include:

- Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A)
- Corporate Restructuring
