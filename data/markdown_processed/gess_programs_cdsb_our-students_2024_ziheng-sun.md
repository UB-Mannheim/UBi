# Ziheng Sun

**Current Role:** PhD Student
**Department:** Business School, Center for Doctoral Studies in Business (CDSB)

## 🎓 Academic Profile & Experience

Ziheng Sun is a PhD student affiliated with the Center for Doctoral Studies in Business at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences.

**Education:**

- **2024:** Master’s degree in Quantitative Finance (MQF) from the University of Maryland, College Park, US.
- **2022:** Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration in Finance from Temple University, Philadelphia, US.

**Research & Professional Experience:**
During his studies at the University of Maryland, College Park, Ziheng gained significant experience:

- **Teaching Assistant (TA):** Assisted Professor Haluk Unal with supervising two master-level classes: *Fixed Income Analysis* and *Fixed Income Derivatives*.
- **Graduate Assistant (GA):** Provided data retrieval support for research conducted by Finance Professors and assisted undergraduate and master students with various financial software tools, including:
  - Refinitiv Workspace
  - Bloomberg
  - Morningstar

In September 2024, he joined the Finance track of the Center for Doctoral Studies in Business.
