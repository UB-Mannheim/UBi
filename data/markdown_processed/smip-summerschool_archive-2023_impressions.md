# SMiP Summer School 2023 Impressions

This page provides an archive of the events and presentations from the SMiP Summer School held in 2023.

## Program Highlights

The summer school featured a comprehensive program including:

- **Welcome Event**
- **Workshops**
- **Keynote Talks**
- **Poster Session**
- **Get-together:** "Women, increase your network!"
- **Farewell**

## Featured Presentations

The program included sessions on various advanced topics in modeling and statistics:

- **Chris Donkin & Nathan Evans:** An introduction to cognitive modelling
- **Mirka Henninger, Esther Ulitzsch & Thorsten Meiser:** Modeling heterogeneity of response processes in item response theory
- **Lesa Hoffman & Jonathan Templin:** Multilevel measurement models
- **Manuel Voelke & Charles Driver:** Dynamic longitudinal modeling
