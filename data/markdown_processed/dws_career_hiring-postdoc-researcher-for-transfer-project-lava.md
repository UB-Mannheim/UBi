# Postdoc Researcher for LAVA Transfer Project

LAVA is a transfer project focused on making knowledge graph (KG) related research results available as accessible, reusable software components. The project aims to test and evaluate these components within a medical context.

## Project Focus Areas

The research will concentrate on advanced problems within knowledge graph technology, including:

- Relation extraction
- Fact validation
- Entity resolution
- Knowledge graph validation and maintenance

## Ideal Candidate Profile

We are seeking a Postdoc researcher with a strong background in knowledge graph technology.
**Required Experience:**

- Experience with one or more KG techniques (e.g., knowledge graph embedding, relation extraction, knowledge graph matching).
- A passion for creating reusable software artifacts.

## What We Offer

- **Team:** Working within a great, international, and diverse team of researchers specializing in knowledge graphs, machine learning, and NLP.
- **Research:** Rich opportunities for pursuing independent research ideas.
- **Location:** A nice campus situated in the city center of one of Germany's sunniest cities.
- **Compensation:** Salary is E13 of the public payscale (52k to 65k per year, depending on prior work experience).
- **Start Date:** April 2025.
