# Bjarne Steffen: Seminar zu Dekarbonisierung und Klimafinanzierung

**Speaker:** [Bjarne Steffen](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar-fall-2023/bjarne-steffen/)
**Institution:** Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Zurich
**Date:** September 27, 2023

### Event Details

This talk was part of the Symposium on „Carbon Emission Pledges and Corporate Decarbonization Initiatives” held in Frankfurt.

**Admission:** Admission is limited and will be based on invitation only.

### Speaker Profile: Bjarne Steffen

Bjarne Steffen is an Assistant Professor of Climate Finance and Policy and the head of CFP. His research, published in high-impact journals, focuses on analyzing policies related to the low-carbon transition across the energy and financial sectors.

**Research Focus:**

- **Intersection:** Environmental/energy economics, financial economics, and innovation studies.
- **Scope:** Covers both developed and developing countries.
- **Developed Countries:** Current projects examine the impact of public policy on technology pathways for energy storage, and the effect of incorporating technology- and country-specific cost of capital into energy system models.
- **Developing Countries:** Current projects address clean energy technology diffusion and the role of multilateral development banks.
