# Decarbonization Seminar Spring 2022

The Decarbonization Seminar, held in Spring 2022, was an academic event that brought together speakers and participants from academia and industry. The seminar focused on discussing the latest advances and challenges associated with the transition towards a decarbonized energy economy.

## Organization and Sponsorship

The seminar was jointly sponsored by:

- **Mannheim Institute for Sustainable Energy Studies (MISES)**
- **Center for European Economic Research (ZEW)**

The organizing team included:

- **Gunther Glenk** (MISES)
- **Marion Ott** (ZEW)
- **Nikolas Wölfing** (MISES)

*Note: The current version of the seminar can be found [here](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar/).*
