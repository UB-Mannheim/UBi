## Speaker Biography

Brandon Gipper completed his PhD at the University of Chicago Booth School of Business in 2016. He holds a BS in economics and mathematics and a master in accounting from the University of Michigan Ross School of Business.

His primary research focuses on empirical accounting topics, including:

- The effects of disclosure on corporate governance.
- Regulatory interaction in the audit industry.
- Accounting standard setting.
- Economic questions related to auditing, the firm’s information environment, and capital formation.
