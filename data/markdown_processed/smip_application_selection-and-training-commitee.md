# Selection and Training Committee (STC) for the Research Training Group

Admission to the PhD program requires formal acceptance by the Selection and Training Committee (STC).

## Application Process Overview

The final admission decision follows a two-stage process:

1. **Preselection:** An initial screening based solely on written application documents.
1. **Interview:** The final decision includes an interview for the respective SMiP applicant. This interview must be conducted by at least one member of the Selection and Training Committee and another member of the SMiP group.
