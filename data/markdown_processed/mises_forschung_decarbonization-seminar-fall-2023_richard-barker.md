## Seminar: Conceptual Foundations of Sustainability Reporting

This seminar, hosted by the University of Mannheim, focuses on the evolving landscape of sustainability reporting standards.

**Event Details**

- **Topic:** ISSB and Conceptual Foundations of Sustainability Reporting
- **Speaker:** Richard Barker
- **Date & Time:** October 23, 2023, 06:15 – 07:15 PM (CEST)
- **Format:** Online only.

### Seminar Abstract

Sustainability reporting is entering a new era, driven by the launch of major international standards. This talk will address how the European (ESRS) and International (ISSB) standards work alongside one another. Furthermore, it will examine how these new standards build upon existing practices and frameworks, including GRI, TCFD, SASB, and Integrated Reporting. The focus will be on underlying concepts, evaluated in the light of evolving institutional practice.

### Speaker Profile: Richard Barker

Richard Barker is a leading expert in sustainability reporting and accounting.

- **Affiliation:** Professor of Accounting at Saïd Business School, University of Oxford.
- **Role:** Full-time member of the International Sustainability Standards Board (ISSB) since July 2022.
- **Experience:** He has served on EFRAG’s Academic Panel and previously led the establishment of the European Accounting Association’s Stakeholder Reporting Committee.
