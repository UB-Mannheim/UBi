# Seminar: Circular Economy and Decarbonization

This seminar features **Atalay Atasu** from INSEAD, focusing on the intersection of circular economy principles and the renewable energy transition.

## Event Details

- **Speaker:** Atalay Atasu (INSEAD)
- **Topic:** Circular Economy, Enthusiasm, Realism and Research
- **Date:** Monday, October 20, 2025
- **Time:** 15:00 – 16:30 PM (CEST)
- **Format:** Hybrid Event
  - **In Person:** Room O 131 (East Wing, Level 1)
  - **Virtual:** Registration required via [this link](https://uni-mannheim.zoom-x.de/meeting/register/jk91ikOHS9-p4bUhRHxaJg)

## Seminar Abstract

The seminar will introduce the broader concept of a circular economy, demonstrating its critical role in the renewable energy transition. The discussion will explore the fundamental principles of circularity, address common misconceptions, and highlight the need for research realism in this field.

Using a series of academic papers, the presentation will cover:

- The core principles of the circular economy.
- The necessity of circularity for energy transition.
- Opportunities for impactful research and how academia can influence policy in this space.

## Speaker Profile: Atalay Atasu

Atalay Atasu is a Professor of Technology and Operations Management at INSEAD. His research focuses on sustainable operations management.

**Academic Contributions:**

- His publications appear in top journals including *Management Science*, *Manufacturing and Service Operations Management*, *Production and Operations Management*, and *Journal of Industrial Ecology*.
- He frequently publishes in practitioner outlets such as *Harvard Business Review*, *Sloan Management Review*, and *California Management Review*.
- His research on circularity was recognized by *Harvard Business Review* as a McKinsey Best Article Award finalist in 2022.

**Awards and Recognition:**
Atalay has received numerous research awards, including:

- Wickham Skinner Best Paper Award (Winner 2007, Runner up 2014)
- Wickham Skinner Early Career Research Award (2012)
- Paul Kleindorfer Award in Sustainability (2013)
- MSOM Responsible Research Award (2019)
- MSOM Best OM Paper Award (2021)

**Affiliations:**

- He co-founded and co-directs the [INSEAD Business Sustainability Programme](https://www.insead.edu/executive-education/strategy/business-sustainability-programme).
- He is involved with the [INSEAD Sustainable Business Initiative](https://www.insead.edu/sustainable-business-initiative).
