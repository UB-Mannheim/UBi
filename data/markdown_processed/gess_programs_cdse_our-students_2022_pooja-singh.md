# Pooja Singh

**Year:** 2022
**Center:** Economics

### Biography and Academic Background

Pooja was born in 1997 and raised in Bangalore, India. She pursued her undergraduate studies in Economics from Christ University, Bangalore. During her bachelor's degree, she interned at research institutes including J-PAL South Asia and the Center for Development Studies, focusing primarily on topics related to education.

She obtained her master's degree in Economics from the University of Bonn. During this time, she worked concurrently as a teaching assistant for mathematics and a research assistant at the Institute of Labor Economics (IZA), Bonn. For her master's thesis, she developed a model to determine the extent of media informativeness under competition.

Pooja's current areas of interest include political economy, gender, and the economics of education.
