# CDSS Associate Member Program at the University of Mannheim

The CDSS Associate Membership Program is designed for Master's students at the University of Mannheim who are planning to pursue doctoral research in the field of social data science.

## Eligibility Requirements

The membership is open to first-year M.A./M.Sc. students in the following disciplines:

- Political Science
- Psychology
- Sociology
- Social Data Science

**To be eligible, you must:**

1. Be a first-year M.A./M.Sc. student at the University of Mannheim.
1. Maintain a grade point average of at least 2.0.
1. Demonstrate a clear intention to pursue a doctorate in one of these subjects.

## Benefits of Membership

Successful Associate Members gain the opportunity to:

- Join GESS graduate-level courses during their membership year.
- Receive a research and conference budget of €100 to attend events such as summer schools or conferences.

## How to Apply

Applications must be submitted as a single PDF file via email to **cdss.gess@uni-mannheim.de**.

**Application Deadline:** June 15 of any given year.

**Required Documents:**

1. **CV:** Must include the names and contact details of up to two referees familiar with your academic potential.
1. **Letter of Motivation:** Maximum 700 words.
1. **Writing Sample:** Maximum 3,500 words.
1. **Transcripts:** Your final bachelor’s transcript and your current (1st-year) master’s transcript.

**Important Notes:**

- You may submit an application starting in April of any given year.
- Associate Membership does not guarantee a place in the CDSS doctoral program. You must separately [apply](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/apply/) to join the CDSS program after completing your master's degree.
- Any courses attended during the membership year will be credited against the requirements for the CDSS doctoral program.
