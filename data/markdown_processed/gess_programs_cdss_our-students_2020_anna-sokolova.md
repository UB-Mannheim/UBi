# Anna Sokolova

Anna Sokolova is a Doctoral Candidate in the School of Social Sciences at the University of Mannheim.

## Academic Background and Research Focus

Anna Sokolova obtained her Bachelor’s degree in Sociology, specializing in Economic Sociology, from the National Research University Higher School of Economics in Moscow, Russia. She subsequently completed a Research Master in Sociology and Social Research at Utrecht University in the Netherlands.

**Professional Experience:**

- **Moscow:** Worked as a research assistant at the Laboratory for Studies in Economic Sociology, focusing on projects in the sociology of medicine and healthcare. She also served as a teaching assistant at the Department of Sociology.
- **Utrecht University:** During her research internship, she worked on a project investigating how the network structure of online marketplace communities affects the cooperative behavior of sellers. For this study, textual and network data were collected from a social networking site.

**Research Interests:**
Anna’s primary interests lie in economic and analytical sociology, social networks, trust, and cooperation. She has a strong interest in experimental methods in sociology, formal theoretical modelling, and network analysis.
