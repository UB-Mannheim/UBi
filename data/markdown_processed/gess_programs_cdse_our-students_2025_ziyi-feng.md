# Ziyi Feng

**Year:** 2025
**Center:** Economics

### Academic Background and Interests

Ziyi Feng was born in 2001 in Xiangtan, Hunan Province, China. He obtained a bachelor’s degree in Economics from Sun Yat-sen University. He subsequently pursued graduate studies at Tufts University, where he earned a Master of Science in Economics.

He is currently interested in a broad range of topics, including:

- Labour economics
- Macroeconomics
- Development economics

### Personal Interests

In his free time, Ziyi enjoys reading, watching anime, and playing video games.
