# Corporate and Financial Databases

This page provides an overview of key corporate and financial databases available for business administration research at the University of Mannheim, including access information, content descriptions, and help resources.

## Overview: Where to Find Which Data

| Data Type | Databases |
|----------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Prices | LSEG Workspace, Aktienführer-Archiv |
| Analyst forecasts | I/B/E/S (via LSEG Workspace) |
| Mutual funds | CRSP Mutual Funds, Institutional (13F) Holdings, Mutual Fund Holdings |
| Accounting data | Orbis, COMPUSTAT North America, COMPUSTAT Global, LSEG Workspace |
| Banks and financial service | Bank Focus, SNL Unlimited (S&P Global) |
| M&A and ownership structure | Aktienführer data archive, Zephyr, SDC Worldwide M&A |
| Executive information | Orbis, Dun & Bradstreet Hoovers, COMPUSTAT Execucomp, Aktienführer data archive |

## Database Descriptions and Access

### Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS)

Many financial databases are accessible via the web-based WRDS interface.

- [Access and university specific information](https://dbis.ur.de/UBMAN/resources/8646)
- [WRDS Website](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/)

### Aktienführer Data Archive

- **Content:** Historic German financial market and corporate data (1870–2018), including standardized reports on corporations traded at German stock exchanges.
- **Available history:** 1956–2016 (Database), 1870–2016 (digitized PDFs)
- **Access:** [Free access](https://digi.bib.uni-mannheim.de/aktienf%C3%BChrer/data/index.php) within the University of Mannheim IP range

### Bank Focus (Bureau van Dijk and Moody's Analytics)

- **Content:** Detailed, standardized reports and ratios for 44,000 banks worldwide, updated templates reflecting latest accounting and regulatory disclosures.
- **Help:** [Bank Focus user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/131_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** [Bank Focus](https://bankfocus.bvdinfo.com/ip) (Bureau van Dijk interface), or via [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT Execucomp (Standard & Poor's)

- **Content:** Executive compensation data from annual proxy statements (DEF14A SEC form), including salary, bonus, options, stock awards, and more.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT Global (Standard & Poor's)

- **Content:** Financial and market data for publicly traded companies in 80+ countries, with up to 12 years of annual history.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT North America (Standard & Poor's)

- **Content:** U.S. and Canadian company fundamentals and market data for 30,000+ companies, including indices, industry segments, and more.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### CRSP (Center for Research in Security Prices)

- **Content:** Security prices, returns, and volume data for NYSE, AMEX, and Nasdaq; includes stock indices, portfolio data, treasury bond and risk-free rates, and mutual fund databases.
- **Help:** Data description and definition guide via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Dun & Bradstreet Hoovers

- **Content:** Over 220 million business records from 30,000 sources in 190 countries, including company data, online sources, and daily updates.
- **Access:** [Dun & Bradstreet Hoovers](https://dbis.ur.de/UBMAN/resources/105992)

### I/B/E/S

- **Content:** Analyst consensus and detail forecasts, including earnings per share, revenue, cash flow, growth projections, and stock recommendations.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Institutional (13F) Holdings

- **Content:** Institutional Common Stock Holdings and Transactions as reported on SEC Form 13F, for managers with $100 million+ in assets.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### LSEG Workspace (including Datastream and Worldscope)

- **Content:** Comprehensive company and market data, including financial indicators, stock, bond, money, foreign exchange, and commodity markets.
- **Help:**
  - [LSEG Finance Essentials](https://www.lseg.com/en/training/learning-centre/certification/lsegfe-certification)
  - [LSEG Workspace tutorials](https://www.lseg.com/en/training/learning-centre/)
  - [Excel Add-In tutorial](https://www.lseg.com/content/lseg/en_us/training/learning-centre/learning-centre-catalogue/video-details.html?id=getting-started-with-refinitiv-workspace-excel-add-in&playlistId=1_6rg1cjpw)
  - [Datastream datatype description (PDF)](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/media/Lehrstuehle/bwl/Maug/Database_info/Datastream_dataypes.pdf)
- **Access:** Restricted to University of Mannheim faculty and students. [Request a personal account](https://www.bib.uni-mannheim.de/medien/zugang-zum-lseg-workspace/) for semester or three months. For access questions, contact the [subject librarian for business administration](https://www.bib.uni-mannheim.de/en/resources/subject-specific-research/).

### Mutual Fund Holdings

- **Content:** Security holding information for all registered mutual funds reporting to the SEC, plus 3,000 global funds.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Orbis (Bureau van Dijk)

- **Content:** Information on over 275 million companies worldwide, with a focus on private companies. Enables company research, group analysis, and cross-border comparisons.
- **Help:** [Orbis user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/68_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** [Orbis](http://dbis.uni-regensburg.de/warpto.php?bib_id=ubman&color=8&titel_id=12630&url=https%3A%2F%2Forbis4.bvdinfo.com%2Fip) (Bureau van Dijk interface)
- **Extended Access:** For bulk downloads and historical updates, professors and academic staff (including supervised student theses) can request special access via the Dean's Office. Contact: [orbis@uni-mannheim.de](mailto:orbis@uni-mannheim.de)

### SDC Worldwide Mergers, Acquisitions & Alliances (Thomson Financial)

- **Content:** Data on worldwide mergers, acquisitions, alliances, and stock repurchases, covering 672,000+ global M&As since 1985 (U.S. since 1979).
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### SNL Unlimited (S&P Global)

- **Content:** Financial data, news, and analyses for 6,500+ public and 50,000+ private companies in banking, insurance, and financial services.
- **Help:** In-database help after login.
- **Access:** Restricted to University of Mannheim members; personal registration with university email required via [S&P Global registration page](https://platform.spgi.spglobal.com/)

### Zephyr (Bureau van Dijk)

- **Content:** M&A, IPO, private equity, and venture capital deal information, with 1.6 million deals and rumors.
- **Help:** [Zephyr user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/83_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** [Zephyr](https://zephyr.bvdinfo.com/ip) (Bureau van Dijk interface)
