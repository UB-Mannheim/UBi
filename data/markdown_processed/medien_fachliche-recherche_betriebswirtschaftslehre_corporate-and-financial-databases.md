# Corporate and Financial Databases

This page provides an overview of key corporate and financial databases available at the University of Mannheim, including access information, content descriptions, and help resources.

## Where to Find Which Data

| Data Type | Databases |
| --- | --- |
| Prices | LSEG Workspace, Aktienführer-Archiv, CRSP |
| Analyst forecasts | I/B/E/S |
| Mutual funds | CRSP Mutual Funds, Institutional (13F) Holdings, Mutual Fund Holdings |
| Accounting data | Orbis, COMPUSTAT North America, COMPUSTAT Global, LSEG Workspace |
| Banks and financial service industry | Bank Focus, SNL Unlimited (S&P Global) |
| M&A and ownership structure | Aktienführer data archive, Zephyr, SDC Worldwide M&A |
| Executive information | Orbis, Aktienführer data archive, Dun & Bradstreet Hoovers, COMPUSTAT Execucomp |

## Overview of Databases

### Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS)

Many financial databases are accessible via the web-based WRDS interface.

- [Access and university specific information](https://dbis.ur.de/UBMAN/resources/8646)
- [WRDS Website](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/)

### Aktienführer Data Archive

- **Contents:** Historic German financial market and corporate data (1870–2018), including standardized reports from German and foreign corporations traded at German stock exchanges. Reports include information on head offices, management, operations, investments, shareholder structure, and income statements.
- **Available history:** 1956–2016 (Database), 1870–2016 (digitized full-text PDFs)
- **Access:** [Free access](https://digi.bib.uni-mannheim.de/aktienf%C3%BChrer/data/index.php) within the IP-range of the University of Mannheim

### Bank Focus (Bureau van Dijk and Moody's Analytics)

- **Contents:** Comprehensive database of 44,000 banks worldwide, with detailed, standardized reports and ratios. Data sourced from annual reports, information providers, and regulatory sources.
- **Help:** [Bank Focus user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/131_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** [Bank Focus](https://bankfocus.bvdinfo.com/ip) (Bureau van Dijk interface), via [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT Execucomp (Standard & Poor's)

- **Contents:** Executive compensation data from company annual proxies (DEF14A SEC form), including salary, bonus, options, stock awards, incentive plans, pensions, and other compensation.
- **Help:** Documentation and manuals available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT Global (Standard & Poor's)

- **Contents:** Financial and market data for publicly traded companies in over 80 countries, covering more than 90% of global market capitalization. Includes hundreds of data items, ratios, and up to 12 years of annual history.
- **Help:** Documentation and manuals available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT North America (Standard & Poor's)

- **Contents:** U.S. and Canadian fundamental and market information on over 30,000 companies, including income statements, balance sheets, cash flows, indices, industry segments, banks, market prices, dividends, and earnings.
- **Help:** Documentation and manuals available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### CRSP (Center for Research in Security Prices)

- **Contents:** Security prices, returns, and volume data for NYSE, AMEX, and Nasdaq markets. Includes stock indices, portfolio data, treasury bond and risk-free rates, and mutual fund databases. University of Mannheim subscribes to the CRSP stock and indices database.
- **Help:** Data description and definition guide via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Dun & Bradstreet Hoovers

- **Contents:** Over 220 million business records from 30,000 sources in 190 countries. Includes company data (address, sector, employees, risk classes) and enriched online and signal information, updated daily.
- **Access:** [Dun & Bradstreet Hoovers](https://dbis.ur.de/UBMAN/resources/105992)

### I/B/E/S

- **Contents:** Consensus and detailed forecasts from security analysts, including earnings per share, revenue, cash flow, growth projections, and stock recommendations.
- **Help:** Documentation and manuals available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Institutional (13F) Holdings

- **Contents:** Institutional Common Stock Holdings and Transactions as reported on SEC Form 13F, covering managers with $100 million or more in assets.
- **Help:** Documentation and manuals available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### LSEG Workspace (including Datastream and Worldscope)

- **Contents:** Comprehensive company and market data, including financial indicators, stock market, bonds, money and foreign exchange markets, and major commodities. Data is available for download.
- **Help:**
  - [LSEG Finance Essentials](https://www.lseg.com/en/training/learning-centre/certification/lsegfe-certification) – eLearning program
  - [LSEG Workspace tutorials and courses](https://www.lseg.com/en/training/learning-centre/)
  - [Excel Add-In tutorial](https://www.lseg.com/content/lseg/en_us/training/learning-centre/learning-centre-catalogue/video-details.html?id=getting-started-with-refinitiv-workspace-excel-add-in&playlistId=1_6rg1cjpw)
  - [Datastream datatype description (PDF)](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/media/Lehrstuehle/bwl/Maug/Database_info/Datastream_dataypes.pdf)
- **Access:**
  - Access limited to University of Mannheim faculty and students.
  - [Request a personal account](https://www.bib.uni-mannheim.de/medien/zugang-zum-lseg-workspace/) for frequent use (faculty and doctoral students: semester-long; students: three months).
  - For access questions, contact the [subject librarian for business administration](https://www.bib.uni-mannheim.de/en/resources/subject-specific-research/).

### Mutual Fund Holdings

- **Contents:** Security holding information for all registered mutual funds reporting to the SEC, plus 3,000 global funds. Includes NYSE/AMEX/NASDAQ, Toronto, and Montreal common stocks.
- **Help:** Documentation and manuals available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Orbis (Bureau van Dijk)

- **Contents:** Information on over 275 million companies worldwide, with a focus on private companies. Enables research on individual companies or groups, company analysis, and cross-border comparisons. Extended access for bulk downloads and historical updates is available for professors, academic staff, and supervised student theses.
- **Help:** [Orbis user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/68_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** [Orbis](http://dbis.uni-regensburg.de/warpto.php?bib_id=ubman&color=8&titel_id=12630&url=https%3A%2F%2Forbis4.bvdinfo.com%2Fip) (Bureau van Dijk interface)
- **Extended Access:** For bulk downloads and historical updates, contact orbis@uni-mannheim.de (access administered by the Dean's Office of the Business School).

### SDC Worldwide Mergers, Acquisitions & Alliances (Thomson Financial)

- **Contents:** Data on worldwide mergers, acquisitions, alliances, and stock repurchases. Covers approximately 672,000 global M&As from 1985 to present (U.S. data since 1979), and stock repurchase programs from 1994 to present.
- **Help:** Documentation and manuals available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### SNL Unlimited (S&P Global)

- **Contents:** Financial data, news, and analyses for over 6,500 public and 50,000 private companies in banking (worldwide), insurance (USA, Canada, Europe), and financial services (USA, Canada).
- **Help:** After login, click the "Help" button in the database header.
- **Access:** Restricted to University of Mannheim members; personal registration with university email required via [S&P Global registration page](https://platform.spgi.spglobal.com/).

### Zephyr (Bureau van Dijk)

- **Contents:** Comprehensive deal database for M&A, IPO, private equity, venture capital deals, and rumors. Contains information on 1.6 million deals and rumors, with over 100,000 added annually.
- **Help:** [Zephyr user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/83_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** [Zephyr](https://zephyr.bvdinfo.com/ip) (Bureau van Dijk interface)
