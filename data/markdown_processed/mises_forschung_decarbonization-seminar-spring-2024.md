# Decarbonization Seminar Spring 2024

The **Decarbonization Seminar** is a recurring academic event that hosts speakers and participants from academia and industry. The seminar discusses the latest advances and challenges associated with the transition towards a decarbonized energy economy.

The seminar is jointly sponsored by:

- **Mannheim Institute for Sustainable Energy Studies (MISES)**
- **Center for European Economic Research (ZEW)**

**Organizing Team:**
The organizing team includes experts from both institutions:

- [Gunther Glenk](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/accounting-taxation/team/gunther-glenk/) (MISES)
- [Marion Ott](https://www.zew.de/team/mot) (ZEW)
- [Nikolas Wölfing](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/area-accounting-taxation/team/dr-nikolas-woelfing/) (MISES)

*You can find the current version of the seminar [here](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar-fall-2024/).*
