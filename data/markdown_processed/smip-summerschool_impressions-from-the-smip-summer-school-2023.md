# Impressions from the SMiP Summer School

This document summarizes the key components and activities of the SMiP Summer School.

## Program Highlights

The Summer School featured a comprehensive program designed to advance knowledge in modeling techniques for psychology. Key components included:

- **Welcome Event:** An introductory session for all participants.
- **Workshops:** Specialized training sessions covering advanced modeling techniques:
  - David Goretzko & Philipp Sterner: Machine learning methods for modeling in psychology
  - Lesa Hoffman & Jonathan Templin: Multilevel measurement models
  - Markus Janczyk & Valentin Koob: An introduction to modeling experimental data with dynamic diffusion models
  - Manuel Voelke & Charles Driver: Dynamic longitudinal modeling
- **Keynote Talks:** Presentations from leading experts in the field.
- **Poster Session:** An opportunity for participants to present and discuss ongoing research.
- **Special Sessions:** Dedicated discussions on professional development and work-life balance, including:
  - Games & Discussion on “Structured Doctoral Programs”
  - Compatibility of Work and Private Life
- **Farewell:** Concluding event for the Summer School.
