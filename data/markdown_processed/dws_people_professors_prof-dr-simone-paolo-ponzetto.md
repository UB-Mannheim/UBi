# Prof. Dr. Simone Paolo Ponzetto

Prof. Dr. Simone Paolo Ponzetto is a full professor (W3) at the University of Mannheim, holding the Chair of Information Systems III: Enterprise Data Analysis. He joined the Data and Web Science Group in February 2013.
