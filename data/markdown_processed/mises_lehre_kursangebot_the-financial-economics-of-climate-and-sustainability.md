# The Financial Economics of Climate and Sustainability

This course is designed to address the complex intersection of climate change, finance, and economic systems, fostering collaboration among global researchers.

## Course Objectives

The primary goals of the course are twofold:

1. **Education:** To introduce graduate students to the core questions and advanced methodologies within the rapidly evolving fields of climate and sustainable finance.
1. **Collaboration:** To connect researchers from across the globe who share an interest in this topic, thereby stimulating more rigorous, relevant, and collaborative academic work.

## Faculty and Instruction

Addressing climate change requires systemic changes across natural, social, and economic systems, necessitating greater global collaboration. In this spirit, the course is offered by a diverse team of professors from various schools and universities worldwide.

The teaching group comprises current and former presidents of the AFA and EFA, alongside leading scholars in climate finance.
