# Management Area Research Fields

The [Management Area](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/management/) within the Business School of the University of Mannheim covers a wide range of fields, including corporate social responsibility, human resource management, international management, public and non-profit management, and small- and medium-size enterprises (SMEs).

Research in this area is highly interdisciplinary, drawing methodological strength from the social sciences and requiring an interfunctional approach across business administration, economics, and social sciences. Given that insights often originate from industrial practice, survey design and data collection methods play a prominent role in research concerning business strategy and management.

## Core Research Areas and Chairs

The members of the Management Area conduct fundamental research and frontier research applicable to industry. The research environment is dynamic, supporting state-of-the-art teaching and producing substantial yearly research contributions across several specialized chairs:

### Strategic and International Management

The research interests of the [Chair of Strategic and International Management](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/brauer/) focus primarily on:

- Strategic corporate transformation and restructuring.
- Corporate strategy and governance.
- Management of growth.
- Effectiveness of management tools and practices in strategy formulation and implementation.
- Strategic decision-making.

### Business Administration, Human Resource Management and Leadership

The main research fields of the [Chair of Business Administration, Human Resource Management and Leadership](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/biemann/) include:

- Leadership studies.
- Strategic and international human resource management.
- Careers and career patterns.
- Methods in management research.
- Knowledge transfer in teams.

### Business Administration, Public and Nonprofit Management

The [Chair of Business Administration, Public and Nonprofit Management](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/public-and-nonprofit-management/) aims to develop and improve management concepts and instruments for public administrations, state-owned enterprises, and private nonprofit organizations. Key research fields are:

- Strategic management.
- Human resource management for public and nonprofit organizations.
- Organization theory for public and nonprofit sectors.

### SME Research and Entrepreneurship

The focus research fields of the [Chair of SME Research and Entrepreneurship](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/woywode/) cover:

- Management concepts in small and medium-sized businesses.
- Information and communication technology (ICT) in SMEs.
- Family businesses.
- Self-employment and entrepreneurship.

### Organization and Innovation

The main research fields of the [Chair of Organization and Innovation](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/hoisl/) are:

- Organizational creativity and innovation.
- Knowledge management.
- Gender gaps in science and innovation.
- IP strategy and management.
- Entrepreneurial finance.
