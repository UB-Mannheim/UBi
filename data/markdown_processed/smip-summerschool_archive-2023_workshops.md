# Parallel Workshops Archive: Summer School 2023

This archive details the workshops offered during the Summer School 2023.

## Workshop Offerings

Participants attend one specialized workshop throughout the summer school. The topic is selected at the time of registration.

- **[An introduction to cognitive modelling](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/archive-2023/workshops/introduction-to-cognitive-modelling/)**
  - *Speakers:* Chris Donkin (LMU Munich) & Nathan Evans (University of Queensland)
- **[Multilevel measurement models](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/archive-2023/workshops/multilevel-measurement-models/)**
  - *Speakers:* Lesa Hoffman (University of Iowa) & Jonathan Templin (University of Iowa)
- **[Dynamic longitudinal modeling](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/archive-2023/workshops/dynamic-longitudinal-modeling/)**
  - *Speakers:* Manuel Voelkle (Humboldt University Berlin) & Charles Driver (University of Zurich)
- **[Modeling heterogeneity of response processes in item response theory](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/archive-2023/workshops/modeling-heterogeneity-in-irt/)**
  - *Speakers:* Mirka Henninger (University of Zurich), Esther Ulitzsch (IPN Kiel/University of Mannheim) & Thorsten Meiser (University of Mannheim)

## Workshop Structure and Requirements

**Format:**
Each workshop provides a comprehensive overview of the dedicated method, including:

- Introduction to the topic
- Practical applications
- Hands-on exercises
- In-depth overview of advanced topics

**Participation Details:**

- **Size:** Workshops are limited to a maximum of 15–20 participants.
- **Prerequisite:** Basic familiarity with R is required for all workshops.
