# Center for Doctoral Studies in Business (CDSB)

The Center for Doctoral Studies in Business (CDSB) administers the doctoral program at the [Business School](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/) of the University of Mannheim. The Business School holds triple accreditation from AACSB, EFDM (EQUIS), and AMBA.

## Available Doctoral Programs

CDSB offers specialized doctoral programs across several key business disciplines:

- [Accounting](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdsb/cdsb-programs/accounting/)
- [Finance](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdsb/cdsb-programs/finance/)
- [Information Systems](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdsb/cdsb-programs/information-systems/)
- [Management](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdsb/cdsb-programs/management/)
- [Marketing](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdsb/cdsb-programs/marketing/)
- [Operations Management](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdsb/cdsb-programs/operations-management/)
- [Taxation](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdsb/cdsb-programs/taxation/)

## Academic Achievements and Placement

CDSB emphasizes high academic standards, reflected in the success of its graduates and alumni:

### Research Output

CDSB graduates frequently publish in top-tier academic journals, including:

- Journal of Financial Economics
- Review of Financial Studies
- Management Science

### Alumni Placements

Our alumni have secured academic positions at leading global institutions, such as:

- Tilburg University
- University of St. Gallen
- Erasmus University Rotterdam
- University of Warwick
- University of Michigan
- The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania

For detailed information regarding career placements, please visit the [CDSB placement section.](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/alumni-placements/cdsb-placements/)
