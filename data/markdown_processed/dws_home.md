# The Data and Web Science Group

Welcome to the Data and Web Science Group. We conduct research and offer teaching in the areas of data analytics, artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and data integration. The group comprises 7 professors and approximately 35 researchers and supporting staff members.

## 🔬 Research Focus

The Data and Web Science Group focuses on methods for managing, integrating, and mining large amounts of heterogeneous information within both enterprise and open Web contexts.

We are actively involved in several [national and international projects](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/research/projects/) and maintain strong cooperation with industry partners, research institutes, and other universities.

**Resources:**

- Details on research results are published in conference and journal papers.
- Open-license datasets, open-source software packages, and benchmarks are available on the [resources page](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/research/resources/).

**Focus Groups:**

## 📚 Teaching and Education

We offer regular courses for bachelor and master students covering topics such as data mining, data integration, information retrieval, natural language processing, knowledge engineering, and knowledge management.

Students also have the opportunity to write bachelor or master theses related to our group's research topics, and excellent alumni can pursue a PhD within our group. We are currently seeking motivated students to work as research assistants.

## 🚀 Projects and Transfer

Beyond fundamental research, we emphasize applied research and technology transfer, maintaining close cooperation with industry partners. Key collaborations include:

- The [Mannheim Center for Data Science](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/datascience/) (MCDS)
- The [Institute for Enterprise Systems](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/ines/) (InES)
