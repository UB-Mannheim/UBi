# Carlos Gueiros

**Role:** Research Associate and Doctoral Candidate
**Affiliation:** University of Mannheim, School of Social Sciences, Chair of Political Science, Political Economy
**Supervisor:** [Thomas Bräuninger](https://www.sowi.uni-mannheim.de/braeuninger/)

## Academic Profile and Research Focus

Carlos Gueiros is a doctoral candidate in political science at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS).

**Background:**

- **Master's Degree:** Development Economics from the University of Göttingen. During this time, he served as a student assistant at the research training group “Transformation of Global Agri-Food Systems.” His master's thesis examined the effect of local legislature size on Twitter political sentiment in Brazil.
- **Bachelor's Degree:** Economics from the Federal University of Pernambuco (Brazil).

**Research Interests:**
His research centers on political economy, institutions, and development. His dissertation investigates political and non-political phenomena that drive institutional change, political accountability, and shifts in the political process.

**Methodology:**
He is methodologically interested in the empirical implications of theoretical models, including causal inference, geospatial analysis, and quantitative text analysis.
