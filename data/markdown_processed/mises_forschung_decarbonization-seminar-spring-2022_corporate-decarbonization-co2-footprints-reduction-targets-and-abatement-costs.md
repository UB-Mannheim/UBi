# Corporate Decarbonization Seminar: CO2 Footprints, Reduction Targets, and Abatement Costs

This seminar, presented by Jürgen Peterseim of PwC, focused on the critical aspects of corporate decarbonization, including measuring CO2 footprints, setting reduction targets, and analyzing abatement costs.

## Event Details

- **Topic:** Corporate Decarbonization: CO2 footprints, reduction targets, and abatement costs
- **Speaker:** Jürgen Peterseim (PwC)
- **Date:** May 23, 2022
- **Time:** 05:00 – 06:15 PM (CET)
- **Format:** Live virtual event
- **Registration:** Attendees were required to register for the talk [via Zoom](https://uni-mannheim.zoom.us/meeting/register/u50rde2qrT8oHdBEdwZIM5HDUeaKPsQhkYBo).

## Speaker Profile: Jürgen Peterseim

Jürgen Peterseim is a senior professional with over 15 years of experience specializing in:

- Power plant design
- Industrial process analysis and optimization
- Strategy development and product commercialization

His core expertise lies in energy efficiency and developing low-carbon/green energy sources for industry (covering electricity, heating, and cooling). Mr. Peterseim has a proven track record of developing technology solutions for industrial clients, guiding them from initial market need identification and idea creation/patenting through to full product commercialization.

Prior to joining PwC in 2019, he worked for an industrial engineering company active in oil & gas, distributed energy generation, industrial energy efficiency, and energy storage. In parallel to his PwC role, he serves as an Adjunct Professor, facilitating research and industry synergies and providing the latest insights into market trends.
