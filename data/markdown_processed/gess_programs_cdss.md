# Center for Doctoral Studies in Social and Behavioral Sciences (CDSS)

The Center for Doctoral Studies in Social and Behavioral Sciences (CDSS) administers doctoral programs in close collaboration with the [School of Social Sciences at the University of Mannheim](https://www.sowi.uni-mannheim.de/en/).

The research and teaching approach is empirical-analytical, informed by theory, and relies heavily on quantitative methods.

## Doctoral Programs

CDSS offers specialized doctoral programs across several key disciplines:

- [Political Science](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/cdss-programs/political-science/)
- [Psychology](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/cdss-programs/psychology/)
- [Sociology](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/cdss-programs/sociology/)
- [Social Data Science](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/cdss-programs/social-data-science/)

## Research Highlights

CDSS doctoral students have a strong track record of academic success:

**Publications:**
Students have published in top academic journals, including:

- European Journal of Political Research
- Political Analysis
- Educational Psychology
- European Sociological Review

**Alumni Placements:**
Graduates have secured academic positions at leading national and international universities, such as:

- Technical University of Munich
- London School of Economics
- University of Oxford
- Autonomous University of Madrid
- Harvard Kennedy School
