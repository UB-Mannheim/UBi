# Johanna Höhs

Johanna Höhs is a PhD candidate focusing on cognitive psychology, particularly the role of memory in advice taking and motivated forgetting.

## 👤 Kontaktinformationen

- **Adresse:** University of Tübingen, Schleichstrasse 4, 72076 Tübingen
- **E-Mail:** johanna.hoehs@uni-tuebingen.de
- **ORCID-ID:** [0000-0003-1386-4804](https://orcid.org/0000-0003-1386-4804)

## 🎓 Akademisches Profil

- **Primary Advisor:** [Prof. Dr. Mandy Hütter](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/researchers/prof-dr-mandy-huetter/) (University of Tübingen)
- **Additional Supervisors:** Beatrice Kuhlmann, Edgar Erdfelder
- **Dissertation Proposal:** A Model-Based Approach to the Role of Memory in Advice Taking
