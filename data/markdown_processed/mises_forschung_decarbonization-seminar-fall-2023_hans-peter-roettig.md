# Decarbonization of Automotive Retail from OEM's Perspective

**Speaker:** [Hans-Peter Röttig](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar-fall-2023/hans-peter-roettig/)
**Company:** Mercedes-Benz AG
**Date & Time:** October 16, 2023 – 05:15 – 06:30 PM (CEST)

### Seminar Details

This seminar discusses the decarbonization strategy for the Retail Network of Mercedes-Benz.

- **Topic:** Decarbonization of Automotive Retail from OEM's perspective.
- **Abstract:** With Ambition 2039, Mercedes-Benz has set the target of making the fleet of new vehicles net carbon-neutral over the vehicles' entire life cycle by 2039. The Sales Organization and Retail Network are a crucial part of the value chain. The talk will provide an introduction to the Decarbonization strategy for the Retail Network and discuss open questions and challenges encountered during the strategy design.

### Speaker Biography: Hans-Peter Röttig

Hans-Peter Röttig earned his Dipl.-Kfm. degree from the University of Mannheim (1985–1991), specializing in Marketing, Industrial Management, and Physical Technology.

Since joining Mercedes-Benz AG in 1991, he has held various pivotal roles within the Marketing & Sales domain. His experience includes:

- Overseeing Sales and Operations Planning for Commercial Vehicles.
- Implementing Customer-based process costing.
- Managing Customer Relationship Management in the Marketing of Passenger Cars.
- Spearheading the Customer Satisfaction Programme CSI No. 1.
- Contributing to Brand Management at Retail.

Currently, Hans-Peter Röttig serves as **Manager Retail Strategy**. In this role, his primary focus is developing strategies and providing support for the implementation of sustainability initiatives within Mercedes-Benz's global sales organizations.
