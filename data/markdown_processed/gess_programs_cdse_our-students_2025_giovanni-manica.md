# Giovanni Manica

Giovanni Manica is a student affiliated with the Center for Doctoral Studies in Economics (CDSE) at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS) in Mannheim, having joined in September 2025.

## Academic Background and Research Focus

Giovanni is originally from Rome, Italy. He holds a Master of Science in Economic and Social Sciences and a Bachelor's degree from Bocconi University in Milan.

**Key Academic Highlights:**

- **Master's Thesis:** Focused on Labor Economics, examining how collective wage bargaining institutions mediate the impact of import competition on the French labor market.
- **International Experience:** Completed a semester abroad at the London School of Economics (LSE) and contributed to research projects at Bocconi.
- **Traineeship:** Completed a traineeship at the European Commission Representation in Italy during his final year of Bachelor's studies.
- **Current Research:** He is affiliated with the “Labor in the Low-carbon Transition” research group at Fondazione Eni Enrico Mattei as a Junior Researcher, specializing in the intersection of Labor and Environmental Economics.
