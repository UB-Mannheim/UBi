## 🔬 Core Program Components

### Parallel Workshops

Each participant is assigned to one thematic workshop throughout the summer school. These workshops are designed for small groups (15–20 participants) and cover selected subjects of statistical modeling and quantitative methods. The content ranges from basic introductions to advanced issues and new developments, incorporating practical exercises.
*For detailed workshop information, see: [Workshops](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/workshops/)*

### Keynote Talks

Keynote talks provide an overview of current, cutting-edge topics of common interest in statistical modeling and quantitative methods. These sessions are presented by invited experts and are held as plenary sessions for all participants and instructors.
*For detailed keynote information, see: [Keynotes](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/keynotes/)*

### Poster Session

Participants are encouraged to present a poster of their ongoing research project. This session is an excellent opportunity for young researchers to discuss their work with peers and senior researchers, including workshop instructors. Furthermore, active poster presentation may assist in the reimbursement of travel and accommodation costs from participants’ home institutions. Refreshments and snacks will be provided.

### Get-together: “Women, increase your network!”

This dedicated event focuses on the importance of networking and cooperation for career development in academia. It provides an informal setting to meet successful women in science, gain first-hand information on building successful collaborations, and connect with fellow early-career researchers.
*All female participants are cordially invited.*
*More information is available here: [Compatibility of work and private life](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/program/compatibility-of-work-and-private-life/)*
