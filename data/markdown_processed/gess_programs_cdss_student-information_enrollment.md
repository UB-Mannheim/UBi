# Enrollment for Doctoral Candidates at the University of Mannheim

All doctoral candidates must enroll at the University of Mannheim due to the Act on Higher Education of the Land of Baden-Württemberg (LHG).

## Required Documents for Enrollment

Please submit the following documents:

- Completed enrollment application form
- Confirmation of admission at the GESS
- Signed Milestones (doctoral advising agreement)
- Copy of your current employment contract (if applicable)
- Copy of your university entrance qualification (e.g., 'Abitur', 'baccalaureate') (only if you are not already enrolled at the University of Mannheim)
- Copy of your degree certificates (i.e., Bachelor and Master) (only if you did not obtain the degree at the University of Mannheim)
- ID card/passport (including visa, if applicable)

## Fees and Payment Details

**Semester Fees:**
The current semester fee is **169 Euro** and must be paid after you have obtained a student ID number.

**Tuition Fees:**
Doctoral candidates pay **no tuition fees**.

## Special Note: Enrollment Exemption

**For Doctoral Candidates Employed by the University of Mannheim:**
If you are employed by the University of Mannheim on work contracts of 50% or more (based on a full-time position and according to the Collective Agreement for Public Service Employees of the German Länder (TV-L)), you have the option to either enroll or be exempted from enrollment.

- **To be Exempted:** Contact Johanna Fatokun to obtain the form 'Exemption from obligation to enroll'. You must immediately inform her of any changes to your work contract.
- **To Enroll:** Submit the required documents and follow the procedure outlined above.
