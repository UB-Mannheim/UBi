# Interdisciplinary Research Group Family Firms

The Interdisciplinary Research Group Family Firms at the University of Mannheim builds bridges across multiple academic fields to explore complex organizational dynamics.

## 🔬 Interdisciplinary Focus

Our research approach is inherently transdisciplinary, drawing expertise from:

- Business Studies
- Sociology
- Psychology
- Information Systems
- Equality & Intercultural Studies

We utilize a range of methodologies, including quantitative, qualitative, positivist, and inductive approaches.

## 💡 Core Research Areas

Our research explores several critical and evolving fields, including:

- AI & Supercomputing for Sustainability
- Artificial Intelligence & Human Organizations
- Diversity, Equality, and Intercultural Management
- Personality & Well-being, Values, Brands, & Organizations
- Family Firms & Entrepreneurship

## 🏛️ Foundation and Collaboration

The foundation of our research is rooted in the principles of organization and the work of the Carnegie School (including scholars like HA Simon, J March, and A Kieser).

We are committed to openness and international scholarly exchange, cultivating our roots through active partnerships with:

- SCANCOR at Stanford University
- Harvard University

We invite you to explore our research and discover the team behind our work.
