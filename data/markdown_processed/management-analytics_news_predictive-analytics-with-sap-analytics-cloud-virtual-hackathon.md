## Predictive Analytics Hackathon: SAP Analytics Cloud

This hackathon provides University of Mannheim students with a unique opportunity to participate in an SAC Smart Predict Hackathon, typically reserved for SAP customers.

### Event Details

- **Topic:** Predictive Analytics using SAP Analytics Cloud (SAC).
- **Format:** A one-day, hands-on, assisted learning, and group work session.
- **Date & Time:** May 4, 2021, 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM.
- **Platform:** Zoom.
- **Skills Focus:** Participants will use SAC advanced analytics capabilities to identify data patterns, develop analytical models for predictions, visualize results, and recommend actionable courses of action for a given business problem.
- **Prerequisites:** No specific level of coding expertise is required.

### Participation and Application

Spots are limited, so early application is recommended.

**To Apply:**

1. Send your CV and current transcript of records.
1. **Email:** `applicat@mail.uni-mannheim.de`
1. **Subject Line:** Analytics Hackathon Application
