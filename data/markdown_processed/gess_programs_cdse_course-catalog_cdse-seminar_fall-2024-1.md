# CDSE Seminar Schedule: Fall 2024

The CDSE Seminar series for Fall 2024 will take place every week from September 3rd to December 3rd.

**Details:**

- **Time:** 3:30 pm – 5:00 pm
- **Location:** S031
- **Source:** [CDSE Seminar Fall 2024](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdse/course-catalog/cdse-seminar/fall-2024-1/)
