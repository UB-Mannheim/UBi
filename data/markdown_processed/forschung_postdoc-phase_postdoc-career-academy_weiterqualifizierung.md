# Postdoc Career Development and Training Opportunities

The University of Mannheim offers various workshops, seminars, and informational events designed to expand your competencies and prepare you for a successful academic career.

## 🗓️ Upcoming Workshops and Seminars

### Hybrid Seminar: Improving Skills in Scientific Writing and Grant Applications

- **Goal:** To train and improve skills in writing scientific journal articles and grant applications for national and international funding agencies. Primarily for postdocs in social and behavioral sciences, but open to other disciplines with empirical, quantitative, or methodological research.
- **Format:** Hybrid (Seminar room: C216, University of Mannheim)
- **Dates:** 7 Sessions: 16 February – 18 May 2026 (Mondays, 12:00–13:30 CET)
- **Deadline:** 15 January 2026
- **Target Group:** Postdocs (all disciplines) at the University of Mannheim.
- **Language:** English (German possible if necessary).
- **Instructor:** [Prof. Edgar Erdfelder](https://www.sowi.uni-mannheim.de/erdfelder/)
- **Registration:** Register by writing to postdocs@uni-mannheim.de.

### Online One-Day Workshop: Basics of Grant Applications

- **Objectives:** Provides basic knowledge and competencies for developing grant applications for external funding bodies.
- **Description:** Examines the requirements for successful research proposals, offering tools to structure and present project proposals convincingly. Includes hands-on exercises.
- **Format:** Online via Zoom
- **Date:** 16 April 2026, 9:30 – 16:30
- **Deadline:** 20 March 2026
- **Target Group:** Postdocs and final-stage doctoral candidates (all disciplines). Junior professors without grant experience are also welcome.
- **Language:** English
- **Instructor:** [Dr. Beate Scholz](https://www.scholz-ctc.de/#werwirsind)
- **Registration:** Register by writing to postdocs@uni-mannheim.de.

### ENGAGE.EU Online Training: From Academia to Industry

- **Goal:** Fundamental career training for transitioning from academia to industry.
- **Content:**
  - **Day 1 (EU-wide Industry):** Identifying competencies, analyzing job ads, and using AI for applicants.
  - **Day 2 (German Industry):** Job search methods (active/passive), CV/Cover Letter design, and adapting applications.
- **Format:** Online via Zoom
- **Dates:** 24 and 25 November 2025
- **Deadline:** 31 October 2025
- **Target Group:** Early career researchers (doctoral and postdoctoral candidates) from ENGAGE.EU partner universities.
- **Language:** English
- **Instructor:** [Heidi Störr](https://www.push-your-career.com/about-heidi-storr)
- **Registration:** Register by writing to postdocs@uni-mannheim.de.

## 📚 General Research Skills & Career Development

- **Content:** Overview of central funding opportunities for postdocs, ranging from general funding programs to specific personnel grants, presented alternately in German and English.
- **Upcoming Dates:**
  - 10 February 2026: The Emmy Noether Programme
  - 11 March 2026: DFG-Förderangebote für Postdocs (German)
  - 14 April 2026: Das Walter Benjamin-Programm (German)
  - 20 May 2026: DFG funding Opportunities for Postdocs
  - 25 June 2026: The Emmy Noether Programme
- **Information:** Current details are available on [the DFG website](https://www.dfg.de/de/foerderung/foerdermoeglichkeiten/wissenschaftliche-karriere/veranstaltungen).

  - [ZLL Events Overview](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/zll/events/)
  - [Baden-Württemberg Certificate for University Didactics](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/zll/lehre-gestalten/qualifikation-und-weiterbildung/baden-wuerttemberg-zertifikat-fuer-hochschuldidaktik/)

- **ENGAGE.EU Events:** For information on upcoming events from ENGAGE-partner universities, please visit [https://www.engageuniversity.eu/events/](https://www.engageuniversity.eu/events/).
