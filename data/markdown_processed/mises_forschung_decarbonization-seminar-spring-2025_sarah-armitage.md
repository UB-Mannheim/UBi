[Sarah Armitage](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar-spring-2025/sarah-armitage/)

- **Date:** Monday, March 31, 2025

- **Format:** Online only.
- **Registration:** Advance registration is required to join virtually. (Registration opens approximately two weeks before the event.)

## Speaker Biography

Sarah Armitage is an Assistant Professor at Boston University's Questrom School of Business.

**Education & Expertise:**

- **Ph.D.:** Harvard University, specializing in environmental economics, industrial organization, and public finance.
- **M.Phil.:** University of Cambridge (Gates Cambridge Scholar), focusing on Economic and Social History.
- **B.A.:** Yale University, in History.

**Professional Experience:**

- **Environmental Defense Fund:** Economist Fellow (2022–2023 academic year).
- **Consulting:** Worked as a consultant at Industrial Economics, Inc., advising state and federal government clients on various environmental projects.
- **Research:** Served as a research assistant at MIT's Center for Energy and Environmental Policy Research.
- **Impact:** Held the role of Impact Fellow at Prime Coalition, overseeing Prime's initial impact audit of investments with gigaton-scale emissions reduction potential.
