# Course Selection for Exchange Students

## Eligibility Guidelines

The suitability of courses depends on your current academic level:

- **If you are a Bachelor's student:** You must select your business courses exclusively from the bachelor's level offerings. Master's level courses are generally not open to undergraduate students, unless you are in your 4th year of an undergraduate program when you arrive in Mannheim. For specific questions, please contact the Business School Exchange Team at [incoming.business@uni-mannheim.de](mailto:incoming.business@uni-mannheim.de).
- **If you are a Master's/MBA student:** You are eligible to choose Business courses from all master's level courses. If your home university permits you to take selected bachelor's level courses, these are also available to you.

## Available Course Areas

The Business School offers courses across several specialized areas:

- Area Accounting and Taxation
- Area Finance
- Area Information Systems
- Area Management
- Area Marketing
- Area Operations Management

## Detailed Course Information: Information Systems

For a detailed overview of the course catalog, please visit the [Modulkatalog MMM | Universität Mannheim](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/modulkatalog-mmm/is-540-management-of-enterprise-systems/).

**Note:** The following instructions apply to courses IS 613 and IS 615, which have limited capacity.

### Application Process

Applications must be submitted using the chair's [application tool](https://wifo1-52.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/cvportal/#/upload). This tool is accessible only within the university network or via [VPN](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/it/services/internet-and-servers/vpn/).

- **Combined Application:** If you are applying for both IS 613 and IS 615, a single application is sufficient; however, you must indicate that you are applying for both courses.
- **Application Period:** 01.08.23 – 01.09.23

### Requirements

Applicants must submit:

1. A short cover letter (1–2 paragraphs) detailing your motivation and previous experience in software development.
1. Curriculum Vitae (CV).
1. Study results (transcript of records).

### Confirmation and Cancellation

- **Confirmations:** Will be sent on Monday, 04 September 2023.
- **Cancellation:** Possible until 10 September 2023.
