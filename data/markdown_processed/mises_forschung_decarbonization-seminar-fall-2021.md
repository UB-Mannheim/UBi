# Decarbonization Seminar Fall 2021

The Decarbonization Seminar in Fall 2021 brought together speakers and participants from academia and industry to discuss the latest advances and challenges associated with the transition towards a decarbonized energy economy.

**Sponsorship and Organization:**
The seminar was jointly sponsored by the Mannheim Institute for Sustainable Energy Studies (MISES) and the Center for European Economic Research (ZEW).

**Organizing Team:**

- Gunther Glenk (MISES)
- Marion Ott (ZEW)
- Nikolas Wölfing (MISES)

*Note: The current version of the seminar can be found [here](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar-spring-2023/).*
