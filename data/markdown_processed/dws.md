# The Data and Web Science Group

Welcome to the Data and Web Science Group. We conduct research and offer teaching in the areas of data analytics, artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and data integration. The group comprises 7 professors and approximately 35 researchers and supporting staff members.

## Research Focus

The Data and Web Science Group conducts research on methods for managing, integrating, and mining large amounts of heterogeneous information within both enterprise and open Web contexts.

We are actively involved in several [national and international projects](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/research/projects/) and maintain strong cooperation with partners from industry, research institutes, and other universities.

- **Resources:** Details on the results of our research, including open-license datasets, open-source software packages, and benchmarks, are available on the [resources page](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/research/resources/).
- **Updates:** Updates from individual group members can be found on the [X twitter feed](https://twitter.com/dwsunima).

Beyond fundamental research, we focus on applied research and technology transfer, collaborating closely with industry partners. Key institutional involvements include:

- [Mannheim Center for Data Science](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/datascience/) (MCDS)
- [Institute for Enterprise Systems](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/ines/) (InES)

## Teaching and Education

We offer [regular courses](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/teaching/course-details/) for bachelor and master students. Our curriculum covers topics such as data mining, data integration, information retrieval, natural language processing, knowledge engineering, and knowledge management.

Students also have the opportunity to write bachelor or master theses related to our group's research topics, and excellent alumni can pursue a PhD within our group. We are currently seeking motivated students to work as research assistants.
