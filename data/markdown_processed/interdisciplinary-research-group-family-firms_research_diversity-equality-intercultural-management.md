# Diversity, Equality, & Intercultural Management Research

Our research group focuses on empowering organizations to create inclusive and innovative environments in a globalized world. By embracing cultural differences and promoting equity, we aim to unlock the full potential of every individual and build fairer workplaces.

### Core Research Areas

Our research encompasses several critical areas:

- Gender equality and female leadership
- Cultural diversity in teams and top management
- Openness, respect, and inclusiveness in the workplace

### Scope and Impact

Research on diversity, equality, interculturality, and intercultural competence is vital for fostering inclusive environments that drive innovation and collaboration across diverse teams.

**Key Focus Areas:**

- **Global Competitiveness:** Understanding and addressing cultural differences enhances communication, reduces conflicts, and improves global market competitiveness, leading to more sustainable and equitable business practices.
- **Leadership & Equity:** Promoting gender equality in leadership ensures diverse perspectives in decision-making, driving better business outcomes and fostering a culture of fairness and opportunity for all.
- **Inclusion:** Openness and inclusiveness in the workplace foster a culture of respect and collaboration, where diverse perspectives drive innovation and create a sense of belonging for everyone.
