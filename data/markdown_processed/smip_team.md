# SMiP: Statistical Modeling in Psychology Research Group

The DFG Research Training Group “Statistical Modeling in Psychology” (SMiP) is a transregional collaboration dedicated to behavioral research.

## 👥 Team und Forschungsgruppe

The SMiP group comprises thirteen senior researchers and is a collaboration spanning five German universities.

**Key Personnel and Affiliations:**
The group includes renowned behavioral researchers from:

- **University of Mannheim:** Arndt Bröder, Edgar Erdfelder, Beatrice G. Kuhlmann, Thorsten Meiser (Spokesperson), Sabine Sonnentag.
- **Albert-Ludwigs-University Freiburg:** Andrea Kiesel, Karl Christoph Klauer.
- **Ruprecht-Karls-University Heidelberg:** Andreas Voss.
- **RPTU Kaiserslautern-Landau at Landau:** Benjamin E. Hilbig, Tanja Lischetzke, Eunike Wetzel.
- **Eberhard Karls University Tübingen:** Mandy Hütter, Rolf Ulrich.

**Coordination:**
The coordinating university and center for the SMiP group is the **University of Mannheim**. Key affiliated personnel include the spokesperson, Prof. Dr. Thorsten Meiser, and the group's postdoc, Dr. Martin Schnuerch.

## 🎓 Programm und Kandidaten

The doctoral program operates on a regular cycle:

- **Start:** A new cohort of PhD Candidates begins every October.
- **History:** The first cohort started in October 2017. The most recent cohort started in October 2024.

## 🌍 Kollaboration und Standorte

The SMiP group is a collaboration of five universities:

- University of Mannheim
- Albert-Ludwigs-University Freiburg
- Ruprecht-Karls-University Heidelberg
- RPTU Kaiserslautern-Landau
- Eberhard Karls University Tübingen

**Administration:**
The coordination center is located in Mannheim.

## 🤝 Kooperationen

The SMiP group maintains active collaborations with:

- GESS (Mannheim)
- IOPS (Belgium & Netherlands)
