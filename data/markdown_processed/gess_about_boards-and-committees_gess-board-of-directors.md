## GESS Board of Directors

The GESS Board of Directors serves as the governing body for the graduate school.

### Composition and Leadership

The board is composed of:

- The University Vice President for Research and Early-Stage Researchers.
- Three Academic Directors, each representing a distinct center for doctoral studies.

The Academic Directors are elected by the graduate school members of their respective school or department. The GESS Board is headed by the Dean of Graduate Studies, who reports directly to the Office of the President of the University of Mannheim and acts as the primary representative of the school externally.

### Governance and Responsibilities

The GESS Board is strongly committed to the principle of shared collegial governance. Its core responsibilities include:

- Supervision of the graduate school’s academic programs.
- Administration of the school’s budget.
- Defining and overseeing the school’s long-term mission and vision.
