# eJourney Account Management Guide

This guide outlines the process for accessing, configuring, and exiting your account on the eJourney platform.

## 🔑 Logging In and Registering an Account

The process for logging in is the same as registering. Since the platform uses Single Sign-On (SSO) via your home university, manual registration is not required. You only need an **active local account** at one of the ENGAGE.EU member universities.

**Steps to Access eJourney:**

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the eJourney portal: [https://ejourney.engageuniversity.eu/](https://ejourney-qa.engageuniversity.eu/).
1. Click on „Select University“ and choose your home university.
1. Click on „Sign In“. The browser will redirect you to your home institution's dedicated login page.
1. Log in using your university username and password, and agree to submit the required attributes.

**What you can do:**

- **Logged In:** You gain access to personalized features, such as viewing learning activities or, if you are an administrator, uploading files.
- **Logged Out:** You can still access public features, such as the Course Catalogue.

## ⚙️ Adjusting Settings

To personalize your experience, you can adjust various settings within your profile.

**Steps to Access Settings:**

1. Click on your profile icon located in the top-right corner of the screen.
1. From the drop-down menu, select **Settings**.

**Customization Options:**

- **Language:** Choose your preferred language for the platform display.
- **Last Visited:** Decide if the system should remember the page visited during the current session or keep it for future sessions.
- **ILIAS Time Zone:** Select your local time zone (e.g., GMT+1:00 Amsterdam, Berlin, Bern, Rome, Stockholm, Vienna) to ensure all deadlines and appointments are displayed correctly.
- **Date Input Format:** Choose your preferred date format (e.g., 31.10.2025).
- **Time Format:** Set the time display format (e.g., 13:00 for 24-hour format).

***Note:*** After making all changes, remember to click **Save** to apply them.

## 🚪 Logging Out

Logging out ends your current eJourney session and returns you to the start page. After logging out, you lose access to all personalized features, including learning activities and administrator functions.

**Steps to Log Out:**

1. Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner of the screen.
1. From the drop-down menu, select „Logout“.

**Security Information:**

- A confirmation page will appear stating: "You logged off from eJourney. Your session has been closed."
- For security purposes, the system automatically ends a user session after a period of inactivity.
- You can log in again at any time.
