# CDSE Seminar Fall 2021 Schedule

This document outlines the schedule and topics for the CDSE Seminar in Fall 2021, held online. All seminars took place between 3:30 pm and 5:00 pm.

## Seminar Topics by Date

**September 2021**

- **September 7, 2021:** The surplus-sharing channel of taxation: Empirical evidence from the US.
  - *Speaker:* Lukas Hack
- **September 14, 2021:** HANK, housing and monetary policy transmission in the Euro Area.
  - *Speaker:* Hannes Twieling
- **September 28, 2021:** Can FDI reduce fiscal capacity? – The role of the informal sector.
  - *Speaker:* Daniel Overbeck

**October 2021**

- **October 5, 2021:** The effects of Filter Bubbles on Social Utility.
  - *Speaker:* Giacomo Ferraro
- **October 12, 2021:** Media for Development: Empowering Women through Radio in India.
  - *Speaker:* Felix Rusche
- **October 19, 2021:** Persuasion with an optimal frame choice.
  - *Speaker:* Yulia Evsyukova
- **October 26, 2021:** The asymmetric effects of monetary policy on banks' behaviour.
  - *Speaker:* Thibault Cézanne

**November 2021**

- **November 2, 2021:** Demand Estimation with Product Reviews: An Application to the US Beer Market / Competition, Markups, and the Gains from Input Trade Liberalization.
  - *Speakers:* Alexander Kann / Andrii Tarasenko
- **November 9, 2021:** Granular Time Series Detection: A Geometric Approach / The Impact of Infrastructure on Labour Mobility and Bargaining Power. Evidence from High-Speed Railways.
  - *Speakers:* So Jin Lee / Chiara Malavasi
- **November 16, 2021:** Behavioral Interventions and Transport Mode Choice: A Field Experiment.
  - *Speaker:* Johannes Gessner
- **November 23, 2021:** The impact of resource demand shocks on manufacturing firms: Evidence from Indonesia.
  - *Speaker:* Sandra Kurniawati
- **November 30, 2021:** Auction Design for Reinsurance.
  - *Speaker:* Chang Liu

**December 2021**

- **December 7, 2021:** Size-Dependent Private Monitoring: Evidence from Ecuador (co-author: Andrés Plúas-López) / Offshoring Emissions: Evidence from the China Shock.
  - *Speakers:* Tania Guerra / Sabine Stillger
