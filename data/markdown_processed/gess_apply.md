# Application Guide for Doctoral Studies

Please read the admission requirements and FAQs carefully before starting the application process. Applications for the fall semester 2026 are currently open.

## Admission Requirements by Center

Specific admission requirements must be reviewed for the intended doctoral center:

- [Admission Center for Doctoral Studies in Business](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/apply/admission-requirements/for-cdsb/)
- [Admission Center for Doctoral Studies in Economics](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/apply/admission-requirements/for-cdse/)
- [Admission Center for Doctoral Studies in Social Science](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/apply/admission-requirements/for-cdss/)

## Application Process and Deadlines

### General Information

Admission to our programs is highly competitive. We strongly encourage applicants to thoroughly familiarize themselves with the contents of the website to prepare the best possible application.

- **Online Application Portal:** [https://gess-application.uni-mannheim.de/portal/](https://gess-application.uni-mannheim.de/portal/)
- **Final Application Deadline:** 31 March 2026

### Early Applications

Applications submitted before **15 January 2026** will be treated as early applications and reviewed before the final deadline.

- *Note:* Applications from candidates who are not accepted in the early round will be reviewed again with all other applications after the final deadline of 31 March.
