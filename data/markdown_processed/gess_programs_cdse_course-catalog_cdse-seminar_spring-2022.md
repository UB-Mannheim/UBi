# CDSE Seminar Spring 2022

This document outlines the schedule and topics for the CDSE Seminar held in Spring 2022 at the University of Mannheim.

**Location:** L7, 3–5, S031
**Time:** 3:30 pm – 5:00 pm

### Seminar Schedule

**February 15, 2022**

- **Topic:** Kick-Off and Q&A

**February 22, 2022**

- **Topic:** Monetary policy counterfactuals: Time series evidence on the fiscal multiplier
- **Speakers:** Lukas Hack, Giovanni Ballarin

**March 1, 2022**

- **Topic:** Linked out? Measuring discrimination in job network formation / The housing channel of monetary policy in the euro area
- **Speakers:** Felix Rusche, Hannes Twieling

**March 8, 2022**

- **Topic:** Can social comparisons and moral appeals change transportation behavior: A field experiment / Do filter bubbles impact social utility? An experimental approach
- **Speakers:** Johannes Gessner, Giacomo Ferraro

**March 15, 2022**

- **Topic:** Taxing FDI in a developing economy: the case of informality
- **Speakers:** Daniel Overbeck, Karl Schulz

**March 22, 2022**

- **Topic:** (Dis)Inflation Targeting
- **Speakers:** Mridula Duggal (ENTER Guest from UAB)

**March 29, 2022**

- **Topic:** The Impact of Pension Income on Mortality: Evidence from Germany
- **Speakers:** Chiara Malavasi, Matilde Cappelletti

**April 5, 2022**

- **Topic:** Bank managers' sentiment and loan growth
- **Speakers:** Thibault Cézanne, Alexander Kann

**April 26, 2022**

- **Topic:** Optimal Advertisement for Information
- **Speakers:** Yulia Evsyukova

**May 3, 2022**

- **Topic:** Granular Time Series Detection: A Geometric Approach
- **Speakers:** So Jin Lee

**May 10, 2022**

- **Topic:** Letting (Beer-)Advocates Speak: Using Reviews to Define Nests in a Nested Logit Demand Estimation
- **Speakers:** Alexander Kann, Andres Daniel Pluas Lopez

**May 17, 2022**

- **Topic:** The impact of resource demand shocks on manufacturing sectors: Evidence from Indonesia / Efficient Allocation of Greenhouse Gas Emissions
- **Speakers:** Sandra Kurniawati, Chang Liu

**May 24, 2022**

- **Topic:** Assessing the Effects of Size-dependent Financial Audits on Firm Behavior: Evidence from Ecuador/Offshoring Emissions: Did Trade Make German Firms Cleaner?
- **Speakers:** Tania Guerra, Sabine Stillger

**May 31, 2022**

- **Topic:** Input Trade Liberalization with Strategic Sourcing
- **Speakers:** Andrii Tarasenko
