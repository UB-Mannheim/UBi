# Domantas Undzėnas

Domantas Undzėnas is a doctoral candidate at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS) at the University of Mannheim. He also serves as a research associate at the Chair of Political Science, Quantitative Methods in the Social Sciences.

## Forschungsschwerpunkte (Research Focus)

Domantas's research primarily focuses on explaining and reducing prejudice against minorities, populism, and the far-right in Europe.

Methodologically, his interests include:

- Simulation-based inference
- Imputation algorithms for missing data
- (Quasi)experimental methods

## Ausbildung (Education Background)

- **Master’s Degree:** Political Science from the University of Mannheim (Majors: Political Psychology and Quantitative Methods). During his Master’s studies, he worked as a student assistant at the Chair of Econometrics at the Department of Economics.
- **Bachelor’s Degree:** International Relations from the University of Leeds, UK.
