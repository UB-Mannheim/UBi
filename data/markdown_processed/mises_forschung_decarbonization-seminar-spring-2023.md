# Decarbonization Seminar Series

The Decarbonization Seminar is a research initiative that brings together speakers and participants from academia and industry. The seminar discusses the latest advances and challenges associated with the transition towards a decarbonized energy economy.

### Organization and Sponsorship

The seminar is jointly sponsored by:

- **Mannheim Institute for Sustainable Energy Studies (MISES)**
- **Center for European Economic Research (ZEW)**

The organizing team includes:

- **Gunther Glenk** (MISES)
- **Marion Ott** (ZEW)
- **Nikolas Wölfing** (MISES)

[View the current version of the seminar series here](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar-1/).
