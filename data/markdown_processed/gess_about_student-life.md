# Student Life and Support

GESS doctoral students enter a highly personalized and supportive academic and administrative environment. Not only do they share offices with their fellow students during the course of the program phase, they will also regularly interact with peers from other doctoral centers through classes, social events, and brown-bag seminar series.

## Location and Surroundings

The GESS is located in the heart of the city of [Mannheim](https://www.visit-mannheim.de/en). The University of [Mannheim](https://www.visit-mannheim.de/en) is mainly located in one of the largest baroque palaces in Europe.

Mannheim is a major urban center in the vibrant metropolitan area of the [Rhine-Neckar-Triangle](https://m-r-n.com/). Together with Heidelberg and Ludwigshafen, this region constitutes 2.4 million inhabitants, boasting great infrastructure and businesses active on a global scale. Mannheim is surrounded by low mountain ranges that belong to one of the most famous wine growing regions in Germany, the Electoral Palatinate.

## Student Resources and Services

### Housing

A wide choice of housing options helps students to quickly become familiar with Mannheim and its surroundings.
[Housing](https://www.stw-ma.de/en/Housing.html)

### German Language Courses

Participants can access preparatory courses, courses during the semester, and the International Summer and Winter Academy. These courses not only teach the German language but also provide insight into German culture and popular mindsets.
[German Language Courses](https://www.daf.uni-mannheim.de/en/)

### Welcome Center

The Welcome Center informs, advises, and assists foreign students and researchers regarding issues ranging from practical training, immigration, to housing.
[Welcome Center](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/welcome-center/)

### Sports Facilities

The University of Mannheim offers a broad range of athletic and fitness facilities, exercise classes, and equipment, all accessible to GESS doctoral students.
[Sports Facilities](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/sports/)

## Academic Support

### Teaching and Learning Center (ZLL)

For teaching activities, the [Teaching and Learning Center (ZLL)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/teaching-and-learning-center-zll/) offers a variety of courses and individual consultations for teachers. To improve didactic skills, teachers can also earn the '[Baden-Württemberg Certificate for Teaching and Learning](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/teaching-and-learning-center-zll/course-design/qualification-and-continuing-education/baden-wuerttemberg-certificate-for-teaching-and-learning-at-university-level/)' at the ZLL, which meets international higher education standards and is widely accepted in tenure procedures.

## Equal Opportunity and Social Diversity

The University of Mannheim is committed to advancing equality, diversity, and inclusion. Since the establishment of the [Department of Equal Opportunity and Social Diversity](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/gender-equality-and-equal-opportunity/equal-opportunity-commissioners/department-of-equal-opportunity-and-diversity/) in 2011, the department has continually worked to develop sustainable structures in the fields of gender equality and social diversity.

The department aims to create a pleasant, inclusive, and diverse working environment for all across the University. Resources offered include:

- Tailor-made mentoring programs for junior female scientists (ranging from personal development workshops to workshops on applying for third-party funding).
- Resources for achieving work-study-life balance.
- Individual counseling when necessary.
