# Maria-Luisa Redin Wander

**Current Status:** Doctoral Candidate (2024)
**Center:** Social Sciences

### Academic Profile

Maria-Luisa Redin Wander (known as Marisa) is a doctoral candidate in Psychology at the Graduate School of Economics and Social Sciences (GESS).

**Education:**

- **Bachelor's Degree:** Psychology from Tilburg University (Netherlands). Focus areas included economic and consumer psychology.
- **Master's Double-Degree:** Completed through Tilburg University and the University of Osnabrück.
  - *Tilburg Specialization:* Economic Psychology.
  - *Osnabrück Focus:* High-level cross-cultural perspective on social psychology and research practices.

### Professional Experience and Research Focus

In addition to her graduate studies, Maria-Luisa gained valuable practical experience in consumer psychology and research practices by working in two market research agencies, covering both the German and international markets.

**Current Role:**
She is actively engaged as a doctoral candidate and will also serve as an academic staff member at the Chair of Consumer and Economic Psychology.
