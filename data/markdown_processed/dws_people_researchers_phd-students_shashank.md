## 🔬 Research Focus & Expertise

Shashank Agnihotri is a Ph.D. candidate specializing in the analysis and improvement of the robustness and reliability of deep learning models. His primary research interests include:

- **Adversarial and Out-of-Distribution (OOD) Robustness:** Developing methods to enhance the resilience of vision models against corruptions and novel data.
- **Vision Models:** Architectural Design Choices for OOD Robustness and Adversarial Robustness.
- **Pixel-wise Prediction Tasks:** Advanced techniques for detailed image reconstruction and prediction.
- **Neural Architecture Search (NAS):** Investigating efficient and robust NAS methodologies.
- **Signal Processing:** Analyzing the impact of sensor and sensor-related parameters on deep learning model reliability.
