## Siyu Zhang

Siyu Zhang is a student in the Economics department, currently enrolled in the CDSE through the Master of Economic Research (MAER) track at the University of Mannheim (Year: 2023).

### Academic Background

- **Education:** Siyu completed her undergraduate studies in economics at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China from 2019 to 2023, graduating with first-class honours.
- **Current Studies:** She joined the CDSE in 2024.

### Research Interests

Siyu's academic focus lies in labor economics, behavioral economics, and experimental economics.

- **Bachelor's Thesis:** Her undergraduate thesis examined the long-run educational impacts of the 1976 Tangshan earthquake on rural middle school-aged children, utilizing 1 percent microdata from China's national census in 2000.

### Personal Details

- **Interests:** In her free time, Siyu enjoys traveling, hiking, swimming, and cooking.
