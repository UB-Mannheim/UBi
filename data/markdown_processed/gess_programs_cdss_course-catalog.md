## CDSS Course Catalog Information

The CDSS course catalog is the primary resource for program offerings.

- **Future Access:** Starting in Fall 2025, the catalog will be available via Portal².
- **Previous Catalogs:** Older catalogs can be found [here](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/course-catalog/spring-2025/).

## Course Registration Guidelines

All course registration must be completed through Portal².

### Key Deadlines and Dates

- **Fall Term Registration:** Registration opens no earlier than August 15th.
- **Registration Deadline (Fall):** August 31st.
- **Registration Deadline (Spring):** January 31st.

### Enrollment Procedures

- **Non-Catalog Courses:** If you wish to take a course not listed in the GESS catalog, please contact your Center Manager.
- **Doctoral Students:** Doctoral students from [ENGAGE.EU partner universities](https://www.engageuniversity.eu/partners/) are eligible for selected courses. To apply, contact gess.registration@uni-mannheim.de with your desired courses and required documentation (e.g., confirmation of enrollment).

### Academic Calendar

For information regarding exam periods, please consult the [general academic calendar](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/academics/dates/academic-calendar/).

## Deregistration (Withdrawal)

- **General Withdrawal:** Deregistration is possible within the first four weeks of the course start date.
- **Block Courses:** Withdrawal from block courses must be done up to one week before the course begins.
- **Important:** Always inform the respective lecturer when withdrawing.

## Course Evaluation

Students are required to evaluate all courses attended to help improve the program.

- **Process:** Evaluations are anonymous.
- **Access:** Username and password for evaluation will be sent to students via email after the course concludes.
- **Data Protection:** Information regarding data handling is available [according to Article 13 GDPR](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/data-protection-declaration/).
