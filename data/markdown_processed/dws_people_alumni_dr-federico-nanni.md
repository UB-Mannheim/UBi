# Dr. Federico Nanni

**Researcher**

- **Email:** federico (at) informatik.uni-mannheim.de
- **Office:** B6, 26, Room C 1.05

## Research Expertise

My work is focused on adopting (and adapting) Natural Language Processing methods and Information Retrieval approaches in Digital Humanities and Computational Social Science research tasks. For more details, please visit my [website](https://github.com/fedenanni).

## Publications

### Key Research Areas

The publications demonstrate expertise in:

- Computational analysis of political texts (e.g., Hansard, parliamentary debates).
- Entity-Aspect Linking and Named Entity Disambiguation in historical and political contexts.
- Cross-lingual and topic modeling techniques for social science data.
- Web history and digital preservation methods.
