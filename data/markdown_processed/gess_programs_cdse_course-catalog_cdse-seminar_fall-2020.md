# CDSE Seminar Fall 2020 Schedule

The CDSE Seminar Fall 2020 provided a series of online academic lectures covering various topics in economics and finance. All sessions were held online.

## Seminar Sessions

**September 2020**

- **September 8, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): Capital taxation and stock market volatility.
  - *Speaker:* Pau Belda (ENTER Guest from UAB)
- **September 15, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): Falling natural rates, rising housing volatility and the optimal inflation target.
  - *Speaker:* Oliver Pfäuti
- **September 22, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): Frictional disclosure and market power.
  - *Speaker:* Jacopo Gambato
- **September 29, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): Learning about the benefits of moving: The effects of neighborhood on mobility decision.
  - *Speaker:* Suzanne Bellue

**October 2020**

- **October 6, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): What's the missing link? The role of information in changing Covid-19 related behaviour.
  - *Speaker:* Laura Montenbruck
- **October 13, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): Sabotage in contests with group size uncertainty: An experimental analysis.
  - *Speaker:* Jonathan Stäbler
- **October 20, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): Capital requirements, bank competition and stability.
  - *Speaker:* Tommaso Gasparini
- **October 27, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): Gender diversity in corporate boards: Evidence from quota-implied discontinuities.
  - *Speaker:* Valentina Melentyeva

**November 2020**

- **November 10, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): Inequality in health and wealth over the lifecycle in Germany.
  - *Speaker:* Lukas Mahler
- **November 17, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): Ridge regularized estimation of var models for inference and forecasting.
  - *Speaker:* Giovanni Ballarin
- **November 24, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): Legal or illegal cannabis? Hard drugs access and use.
  - *Speaker:* Andres Daniel Pluas Lopez

**December 2020**

- **December 1, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): An efficient dynamic mechanism with information acquisition.
  - *Speaker:* Jasmina Simon
- **December 8, 2020** (3:30 pm – 5 pm): Cannibalization and competition: Market entry following Sweden's pharmacy deregulation.
  - *Speaker:* Isak Kupersmidt (ENTER Guest from SSE)
