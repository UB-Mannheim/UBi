# CDSS Doctoral Program Structure

The Computational Social Science (CDSS) doctoral programs are structured over three years and consist of two semesters of coursework. Doctoral students must commit to advanced, graduate-level studies through mandatory and elective courses.

**Program Milestones:**

- **First Year:** Students are expected to write a literature review, which leads to the development of their dissertation proposal. During this year, students decide on their thesis topic and supervisor.

## Available Programs

The CDSS program offers specialization in the following disciplines:

- [Political Science](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/cdss-programs/political-science/)
- [Psychology](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/cdss-programs/psychology/)
- [Sociology](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/cdss-programs/sociology/)
- [Social Data Science](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/cdss-programs/social-data-science/)

## Program Requirements Overview

The coursework is divided into three phases:

### 📚 1st Semester

- Mathematics for Social Scientists (2 ECTS)
- Current Research Perspectives (2 ECTS)
- Crafting Social Science Research (6 ECTS)
- Literature review (6 ECTS)
- CDSS Workshop in respective discipline (2 ECTS)

### 📚 2nd Semester

- Theory Building and Causal Inference (6 ECTS)
- English Academic Writing (3 ECTS)
- Dissertation Proposal Workshop (2 ECTS)
- Dissertation proposal (8 ECTS)
- CDSS Workshop in respective discipline (2 ECTS)

### 📚 3rd Semester Onwards

- Research colloquia (2 ECTS per semester)
- CDSS Workshops in respective discipline (2 ECTS per semester)
- Dissertation work leading to thesis submission and defense (90 ECTS)

## Degree and Thesis Requirements

### Degree Awarded

To successfully complete the program, students must meet the following requirements:

1. Completion of the entire CDSS course program.
1. Completion, submission, and acceptance of the thesis.
1. Successful thesis defense.

The degree awarded is **Dr. rer. soc.** (Doctor rerum socialium / Doctor of Social Sciences).

### CDSS Doctoral Thesis Format

A CDSS doctoral thesis can take one of two forms:

- **Collection of Papers:** The thesis may consist of several original papers focused on one area, which are intended for publication in reputable scholarly journals.
- **Traditional Monograph:** A traditional dissertation format is also possible, provided the content is suitable for publication as articles in scholarly journals.

The final form of the thesis is decided collaboratively by the student and their supervisor.

## Doctoral Degrees in Germany

In Germany, doctoral degrees often use specific Latin designations to indicate the field of study, which differs from the general Ph.D. (doctor philosophiae) title. Both titles are awarded after an intensive study and research period culminating in the successful submission and defense of a doctoral thesis.

At the GESS, the following doctoral titles are conferred:

- **Faculty of Business and Economics:** Dr. rer. pol (doctor rerum politicarum)
- **Faculty of Social Sciences:** Dr. rer. soc. (doctor rerum socialium)
