## CDSB Area Seminar Series

All areas of the CDSB run prominent external seminar series. These series are an integral part of the PhD Program.

The seminars feature internationally renowned researchers from all over the world who present and discuss their latest research topics. This provides a valuable opportunity for students to gain new insights into various disciplines and receive impulses for their own research activities.

**Note:** For the most recent seminars, please check the respective course catalog.
