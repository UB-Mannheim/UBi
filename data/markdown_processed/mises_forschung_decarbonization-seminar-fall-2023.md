# Decarbonization Seminar Fall 2023

The **Decarbonization Seminar** in Fall 2023 brought together speakers and participants from academia and industry to discuss the latest advances and challenges associated with the transition towards a decarbonized energy economy.

The seminar was jointly sponsored by the Mannheim Institute for Sustainable Energy Studies (MISES) and the Center for European Economic Research (ZEW).

**Organizing Team:**

- [Gunther Glenk](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/area-accounting-taxation/team/gunther-glenk/) (MISES)
- [Marion Ott](https://www.zew.de/team/mot) (ZEW)
- [Nikolas Wölfing](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/area-accounting-taxation/team/dr-nikolas-woelfing/) (MISES)

You can find the current version of the seminar [here](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar/).
