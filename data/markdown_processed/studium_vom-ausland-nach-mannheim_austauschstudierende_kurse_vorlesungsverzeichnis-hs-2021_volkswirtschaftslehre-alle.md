# Economics Course Enrollment for Exchange Students

This guide outlines the rules for exchange students enrolling in Economics courses at the University of Mannheim, differentiating between Bachelor's and Master's levels.

## 🎓 Enrollment Guidelines

### For Bachelor's Students

- **Course Selection:** Bachelor's students must restrict their course selection to courses available at the bachelor's level only.
- **Restriction:** Master's level courses are generally not open to undergraduate students.
- **Prerequisites:** Exchange students from other departments must meet all required prerequisites to attend economics lectures.
- **Seminars:** Bachelor's level seminars are typically reserved for enrolled economics students.

### For Master's Students

- **Course Selection:** Master's students have greater flexibility and can choose elective modules available within the department.
- **Prerequisites:** Fulfillment of prerequisites for each chosen course is mandatory.
- **Restrictions:** Core modules (E601–603 and E700–703) are generally not open to exchange students.
- **Exception:** Students may attend bachelor's level courses if their home university grants explicit permission.
