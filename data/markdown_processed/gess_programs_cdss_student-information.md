# CDSS Doctoral Program: Information for Prospective Students

This page provides an overview of various topics related to the CDSS doctoral program for prospective students.

*Note: Doctoral students already enrolled in the program can find more detailed information [here](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/for-active-students/).*

### Resources and Further Information

For comprehensive details, please consult the CDSS Info Booklet (PDF, 444 kB). This booklet elaborates extensively on:

- The GESS program in general.
- The CDSS program structure.
- Information for international students.
- Studying and living in Mannheim.
- And many other relevant topics.

For any further questions, please feel free to contact the CDSS Center Manager via email.

### Key Program Topics

The following sections provide detailed information on specific aspects of the CDSS program:

- **[Enrollment](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/student-information/enrollment/)**: Details regarding the application and enrollment process.
- **[Regulations](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/student-information/program-regulations/)**: Information concerning the academic regulations governing the program.
- **[CDSS Awards](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/student-information/cdss-awards/)**: Details about available awards and recognition within the program.
