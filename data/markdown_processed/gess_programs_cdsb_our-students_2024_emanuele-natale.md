# Emanuele Natale

Emanuele Natale is a student who joined the Marketing Track of the Center of Doctoral Studies in Business (CDS) at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences in September 2024.

### Academic Background and Experience

- **Education:** Emanuele graduated in 2021 from the University of Mannheim with a Bachelor’s Degree (B.Sc.) in Business Administration. He continued his studies in the same year, pursuing a Master’s Degree (M.Sc.) in the Mannheim Master in Management (MMM) program with a focus on Marketing.
- **Experience:** During his studies, he spent one semester abroad at Mahidol University in Bangkok, Thailand, and gained several years of experience as a student research assistant at the Chair of Quantitative Marketing and Consumer Analytics.

### Research Interests

His primary areas of research interest include:

- Machine Learning in Marketing
- Consumer Behavior
- Generative Artificial Intelligence
