# Youpeng Zhang

**Current Position:** Doctoral Student, Center for Doctoral Studies in Business

**Contact Information**

- **Address:** University of Mannheim, Mannheim
- **Email:** [youpzhan@mail.uni-mannheim.de](mailto:youpzhan@mail.uni-mannheim.de)

**Research Focus**
Youpeng Zhang's primary research interests lie in the following areas:

- Behavioral Finance
- Household Finance
- Labor Economics

**Academic Background and Experience**
Youpeng Zhang obtained his master’s degree in economics from the University of Cologne and completed studies as a guest student at the University of Bonn. During his master's studies, he gained extensive research experience at several institutions, including:

- ECONtribute Excellence Cluster
- Macrofinance & Macrohistory Lab at the University of Bonn
- briq – Institute on Behavior and Inequality
- IZA – Institute of Labor Economics

He further enhanced his academic profile by serving as a visiting scholar at Harvard University and working as a research assistant at Harvard Law School. In 2021, he joined the doctoral program in finance at the Center for Doctoral Studies in Business.
