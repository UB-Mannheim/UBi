# Generative Computer Vision Models (CS-668)

This course provides fundamental concepts of deep generative models, using image generation as a primary example.

## 🎓 Course Details

- **Instructor:** [Prof. Margret Keuper](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/professors/prof-dr-ing-margret-keuper/)
- **Target Audience:** Students of the Master Business Informatics and Mannheim Master in Data Science (MMDS).
- **Participation:** The course is restricted to 50 participants. Places are assigned on a first come/first serve basis.
- **Schedule:**
  - Lecture: Starts February 2026
  - Exercise: Starts February 2026

## 📚 Course Topics and Outline

The curriculum covers a wide range of advanced deep learning architectures and techniques:

**Core Generative Models:**

- Autoregressive Models (e.g., RixelRNN, PixelCNN)
- Normalizing Flows and RealNVP-like models
- Variational Autoencoders (VAEs)
- Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)
- Diffusion Models

**Advanced Topics:**

- Autoencoders and Latent Space Visualization
- Deep Clustering
- Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
- Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs) and Transformers
- Clustering Techniques
