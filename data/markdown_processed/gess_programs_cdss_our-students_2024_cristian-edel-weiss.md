# Cristian Edel Weiss (he/him)

**PhD Candidate**
*University of Mannheim, Graduate School of Economics and Social Sciences (GESS)*

## 🎓 Academic Background & Research Focus

Cristian Edel Weiss is a doctoral student in political science at the University of Mannheim.

**Education:**

- **Master's Degree:** Media, Technology and Society (Darmstadt University of Applied Sciences, Germany).
- **Bachelor's Degree:** Social Communication and Journalism (Brazil).

**Research Interests:**
His research centers on the right to information, press freedom, social accountability, and transparency. He is particularly interested in the intersection of media and power, and utilizes computational methods for both quantitative and qualitative research.

**Master's Thesis Focus:**
His master's thesis examined how President Jair Bolsonaro's policies and attitudes toward the critical press and transparency procedures affected information requests submitted by journalists under the Freedom of Information (FOI) law in the field of education. The research involved:

- A quantitative analysis of open public data from the Brazilian FOI database (since 2012).
- Computational textual analysis using topic modeling and natural language processing techniques on information requests submitted by journalists between 2019 and 2020.

## 📰 Professional Experience

Since 2010, Cristian has worked as a journalist for major media companies in Brazil, covering diverse topics including politics, education, human rights, environment, science, and public policy.

**Key Experience Highlights:**

- **Data Journalism:** Trained as a data journalist by the Deutsche Welle Akademie (Germany) and worked at the Deutsche Welle Newsroom in Bonn (2019 and 2020).
- **Fact-Checking:** Served as a member of the Comprova Project, a coalition of major Brazilian media outlets dedicated to combating misinformation during the 2018 presidential election and the 2020 pandemic. (The team was coordinated by the First Draft Organization, affiliated with Harvard's Kennedy School).
