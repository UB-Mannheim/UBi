### Academic Profile

Yiwei Yin was born in September 1997. She obtained her bachelor’s degree in Economics at the University of Reading in England, graduating as the top student in her field. During her undergraduate studies, she co-authored three research papers with her professors in the fields of spatial econometrics, labor economics, and sports economics.

She subsequently attended the Scottish Graduate Program in Economics at the University of Edinburgh for her master’s degree. Her master’s thesis focused on the empirical relationship between fertility decisions and employment risk among couples in the United Kingdom.

**Research Interests:**
Her primary research interests include labor economics, immigration economics, family economics, and applied econometrics.

**Interests:**
In her free time, she enjoys watching movies, reading books, cooking, and dabbling in psychology. She particularly enjoys the company of her two dogs, Dulu and Xiaogang.
