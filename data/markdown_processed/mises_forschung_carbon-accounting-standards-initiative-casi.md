# Carbon Accounting Standards Initiative (CASI)

## Integrating Corporate- and Product-Level Carbon Accounting

The Carbon Accounting Standards Initiative (CASI) is dedicated to advancing the practice of corporate carbon accounting and reporting.

CASI brings together academic scholars and practitioners to develop robust standards for integrated corporate- and product-level carbon accounting. By building on established financial accounting frameworks, the resulting carbon metrics provide comprehensive, reliable, and decision-useful information regarding a company’s carbon emissions performance.
