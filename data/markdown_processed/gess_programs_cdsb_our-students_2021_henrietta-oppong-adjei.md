## Henrietta Oppong-Adjei

**Title:** Doctoral Student
**Affiliation:** Center of Doctoral Studies in Business (CDSB), University of Mannheim

### 🎓 Academic and Professional Background

Henrietta Oppong-Adjei was born in Ghana. Her academic journey includes:

- **Undergraduate Studies:** She earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Mathematics and Statistics from the University of Ghana.
- **Teaching Experience:** Following her first degree, she gained extensive teaching experience in Ghana, instructing mathematics at both the middle/primary school level (International Community School) and in high school settings.
- **Master's Degree:** In 2018, she pursued a Master’s degree in Quantitative Finance and Insurance at the University of Torino, Italy. She completed this degree in November 2020.
- **Doctoral Studies:** She commenced her PhD in the Finance Track at the Center of Doctoral Studies in Business (CDSB) in September 2021.

### 🔬 Research Interests

Her primary areas of research focus include:

- Asset Pricing
- Sustainable Finance
- Risk Management
- Corporate Governance
