# Admission and Assessment Committees (AAC)

The Admission and Assessment Committees (AAC) serve as the governing bodies for the three Centers at GESS. They are composed of program directors, each representing a specific department. Furthermore, each Center elects a designated Academic Director who represents the Center at the GESS Board of Director Meetings.
