## Julia Spix: Research Profile

Julia Spix began her academic journey at the University of Mannheim, starting with a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration.

### Academic and Professional Experience

- **B.Sc. Studies:** During her Bachelor's studies, she completed a semester abroad at ITESM in Monterrey, Mexico. She also began working as a research assistant at the ZEW – Leibniz Centre for European Economic Research, focusing on Corporate Taxation and Public Finance.
- **M.Sc. Studies:** She continued her studies at the University of Mannheim, earning the Mannheim Master in Management with a minor in tax law. She maintained her role as a research assistant at the ZEW.
- **Current Role:** Julia joined the Taxation Track of the Centre of Doctoral Studies in Business (CDS) in September 2022.

### Research Interests

Her primary areas of research focus include:

- International Tax Planning
- Impact of Tax Incentives
- Transfer Pricing
