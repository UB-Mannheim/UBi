# Richard Barker: Conceptual Foundations of Sustainability Reporting

Richard Barker, associated with the ISSB and the University of Oxford, presented a seminar on the evolving landscape of sustainability reporting standards.

## Seminar Details

- **Topic:** Conceptual foundations of sustainability reporting
- **Date:** May 22, 2023
- **Time:** 05:30 – 06:45 PM (CEST)
- **Location (In-Person):** Room O 129 (Castle, Ostflügel, Level 1, Room 29)
- **Location (Virtual):** Advance registration required (Link no longer available).

## Seminar Abstract

Sustainability reporting is undergoing a significant transformation with the introduction of new global and European standards. This talk provided an overview of how these standards interact and build upon existing frameworks.

The discussion covered:

- The relationship between European (ESRS) and International (ISSB) standards.
- How these new standards integrate with established practices and frameworks, including:
  - GRI (Global Reporting Initiative)
  - TCFD (Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures)
  - SASB (Sustainability Accounting Standards Board)
  - Integrated Reporting

The focus remained on the underlying concepts that drive the evolution of institutional sustainability practice.

## Speaker Biography

**Richard Barker**
Richard Barker is a full-time member of the International Sustainability Standards Board (ISSB) since July 2022. He is currently on leave from his role as Professor of Accounting at Saïd Business School, University of Oxford.

His professional experience includes:

- Serving on EFRAG’s Academic Panel.
- Leading the establishment of the European Accounting Association’s Stakeholder Reporting Committee.
