## Research Interests

Timo Seitz specializes in the statistical modeling of psychological assessments. His core research areas include:

- Statistical modeling in psychometrics.
- Item Response Theory (IRT) modeling, specifically for disentangling substantive traits and faking tendencies in high-stakes assessments.
- Examination of psychological processes related to test-taking behavior.
