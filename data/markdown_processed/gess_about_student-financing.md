## Cost of Living in Mannheim

Living in Mannheim is considered relatively affordable compared to other major German cities. Students should budget approximately **EUR 850 to EUR 1,000 per month**. This budget typically covers:

- **Rent:** EUR 250 to EUR 450
- **Health Insurance:** EUR 60 to EUR 200 (This cost varies depending on your specific situation and insurer. For more details, please visit the [Health Insurance](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/welcome-center/relocation/preparation/health-insurance/) page.)
- **Necessities:** Remaining funds for groceries, clothing, and other living expenses.

*Note: Studying at the GESS is free of tuition fees. For comprehensive information on housing, flat hunting, rent prices, and neighborhoods, please visit the university's [Living in Mannheim website](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/campus/mannheim/living-in-mannheim/).*

## Scholarship and Financial Support

The GESS is dedicated to financing doctoral students' research activities through various scholarships.

### Funding Guarantees and Stipends

- **Duration:** Admitted students are guaranteed **three to five years of funding**, depending on their chosen program and academic performance.
- **Family Support:** Doctoral students with children receive additional financial support.
- **Initial Stipend (Years 1â€“2):** All students receive a monthly stipend of **EUR 1,500** for the first one to two years of coursework.
- **Service Commitments:** Students are generally free from teaching, administrative, or other service commitments during the initial years (exceptions may apply for students with RA or TA contracts in the second year).

### Sources of Funding

The GESS administers and provides an extensive range of scholarships (totaling around EUR 18,000 per year), sourced from:

- [German Academic Exchange Service](https://www.daad.de/en/) (DAAD)
- Fontana-Foundation (private donor)
- Funding program for the promotion of postgraduates of the Land of Baden-WĂĽrttemberg (LandesgraduiertenfĂ¶rderung, LGF)
- Land of Baden-WĂĽrttemberg
- [University of Mannheim Foundation](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/foundation/)
- Matching funds (private donors)

*Please note that all financial assistance and scholarships are subject to the existing provisions and guidelines of the respective funding body and scholarship program.*

### Additional Funding Opportunities

Beyond the core scholarships, funding is available for:

- Completion scholarships
- Research stays abroad (for periods between 3 to 6 months)
- Scientific experiments
- Comprehensive travel support
- Awards
