# Dingbang Feng

**Academic Year:** 2025
**Center:** Economics

## Profile Summary

Dingbang Feng is a student specializing in Economics. He is originally from China and has a strong background in engineering, which he successfully transitioned into academic research in economics.

## Academic and Professional Background

- **Education:** Dingbang graduated from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT, China) in 2014 with a Bachelor's degree in Optical Information Science and Engineering. He subsequently pursued his Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Economics at the University of Mannheim.
- **Early Career:** After his undergraduate studies, he worked for two years as a CAE engineer at Samsung in Tianjin, China.
- **Research Experience (University of Mannheim):** During his studies at the University of Mannheim, he gained practical experience by serving as a long-term Teaching Assistant for Microeconomics A and working as a Research Assistant in the “Economics of Innovation and Industrial Dynamics” department at ZEW.

## Research Interests

His primary research interests lie in:

- Industrial Organization
- International Economics
