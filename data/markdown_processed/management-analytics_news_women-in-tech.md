# WoMen in Tech Mannheim: An Inclusive IT Network

WoMen in Tech Mannheim is an inclusive student network designed for all University of Mannheim students interested in the rapidly growing field of Information Technology. Recognizing that IT literacy is crucial for a fulfilling career, the network serves as a platform for discussion, exchange, and upskilling across various tech topics.

**Topics Covered:**
The network focuses on diverse areas within the tech sector, including:

- Analytics and Data Science
- Coding and Programming
- Corporate IT and Big Tech
- The intersection of technology and politics

**Inclusivity Statement:**
As a truly inclusive tech network, all University of Mannheim students are welcome, regardless of gender expression, ethnicity, background, age, or field of study.

## Event Spotlight: Demystifying IT Spring 2021

Students from all faculties are invited to the spring semester's WIT event, **“Demystifying IT – Job Profiles of the Future.”** This session provides a unique opportunity to engage with industry experts and explore promising career paths in IT.

**Event Details:**

- **Date:** May 6
- **Time:** 8:00 PM
- **Format:** The event includes a discussion round on the future of IT, followed by break-out rooms for direct interaction and Q&A.

**Featured Industry Experts:**
Attendees will have the opportunity to meet and speak with leading professionals:

- **Nina Weingarten:** Business Lead Cloud Migration at Microsoft
- **Sean Kask, Ph.D.:** Artificial Intelligence Strategy Officer at SAP
- **Anne Lauscher:** NLP Doctoral Researcher at University of Mannheim
- **Philipp Wortmann:** Program Manager at Google Cloud

**Facilitation:**
The discussion will be facilitated by **Hannah Sperling**, a Doctoral Student at the University of Mannheim and Analytics Specialist at SAP SE.
