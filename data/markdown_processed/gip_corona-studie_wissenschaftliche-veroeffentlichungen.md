# Wissenschaftliche Veröffentlichungen zur Corona-Studie

Die folgenden Veröffentlichungen stammen aus der Mannheim Corona Studie (MCS) und behandeln die Auswirkungen der COVID-19-Pandemie auf Deutschland.

## Fachartikel und Studien (2020–2022)

### 2022

- **Cornesse et al. 2022**
  [Measurement instruments for fast and frequent data collection during the early phase of COVID-19 in Germany: reflections on the Mannheim Corona Study](https://measurementinstrumentssocialscience.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s42409-022-00030-5)
- **Ebbinghaus et al. 2022**
  [Welfare state support during the COVID-19 pandemic: Change and continuity in public attitudes towards social policies in Germany](https://doi.org/10.1002/epa2.1152)
- **Juhl et al. 2022**
  [Preferences for Centralized Decision-Making in Times of Crisis: The COVID-19 Pandemic in Germany](http://www.ronilehrer.com/docs/papers/JHET.pdf)

- **Alon et al. 2021**
  [From Mancession to Shecession: Women's Employment in Regular and Pandemic Recessions](https://www.nber.org/papers/w28632)
- **Blom & Möhring 2021**
  [Soziale Ungleichheit in der Beschäftigungssituation während der frühen Phase der Coronakrise](https://www.destatis.de/DE/Service/Statistik-Campus/Datenreport/Downloads/datenreport-2021-kap-14.html)
- **Blom et al. 2021**
  [Barriers to the Large-Scale Adoption of the COVID-19 Contact-Tracing App in Germany: Survey Study](https://doi.org/10.2196/23362)
- **Bujard et al. 2021**
  [Eltern während der Corona-Krise: Zur Improvisation gezwungen](https://www.bib.bund.de/Publikation/2020/Eltern-waehrend-der-Corona-Krise.html?nn=9859974)
- **Cornesse et al. 2021**
  [From German Internet Panel to Mannheim Corona Study: Adaptable probability-based online panel infrastructures during the pandemic](https://doi.org/10.1111/rssa.12749)
- **Möhring et al. 2021**
  [Inequality in Employment Trajectories and Their Socio-economic Consequences During the Early Phase of the COVID-19 Pandemic in Germany](https://osf.io/preprints/socarxiv/m95df/)
- **Möhring et al. 2021**
  [Is the Recession a ‘Shecession’? Gender Inequality in the Employment Effects of the COVID-19 Pandemic in Germany](https://osf.io/preprints/socarxiv/tzma5/)

### 2020

- **Blom 2020**
  [Zum gesellschaftlichen Umgang mit der Corona-Pandemie. Ergebnisse der Mannheimer Corona-Studie](https://www.bpb.de/apuz/314345/zum-gesellschaftlichen-umgang-mit-der-corona-pandemie)
