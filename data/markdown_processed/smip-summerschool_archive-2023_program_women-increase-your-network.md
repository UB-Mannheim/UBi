# Networking Event: Women, increase your network! (Summer School 2023)

This event was a networking gathering held during the 2023 Summer School.

## 🗓️ Event Details

- **Date & Time:** Wednesday, June 28, 2023 (6 p.m. – 9 p.m.)
- **Location:** Mannheim Palace, Rector's Courtyard (outside) or Fuchs-Petrolub Hall (O 138)
- **Target Participants:** All registered female participants of the Summer School.
- **Catering:** Drinks & Finger Food

## 🎤 Program Highlights

- **Panel Discussion Contributors:** Lesa Hoffman, Sabine Sonnentag, Mirka Henninger & Esther Ulitzsch
- **Moderators:** Theresa Koch & Anke Söllner

## 💰 Funding Information

This networking event was funded by the Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft (DFG, German Research Foundation) under the grant GRK 2277 “Statistical Modeling in Psychology”.
