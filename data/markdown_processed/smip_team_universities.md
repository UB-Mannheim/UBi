# DFG Research Training Group “Statistical Modeling in Psychology” (SMiP)

The SMiP is a transregional collaboration of thirteen renowned behavioral researchers from five German universities.

## 🎓 Participating Universities and Researchers

### University of Mannheim (Coordinating Center)

The University of Mannheim is the coordinating center for the SMiP group and hosts five senior researchers:

- [Prof. Dr. Arndt Bröder](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/researchers/prof-dr-arndt-broeder/)
- [Prof. Dr. Edgar Erdfelder](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/researchers/prof-dr-edgar-erdfelder/)
- [Prof. Dr. Beatrice G. Kuhlmann](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/researchers/prof-dr-beatrice-g-kuhlmann/)
- [Prof. Dr. Thorsten Meiser](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/researchers/prof-dr-thorsten-meiser/)
- [Prof. Dr. Sabine Sonnentag](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/researchers/prof-dr-sabine-sonnentag/)

Additionally, the group includes:

- [Dr. Martin Schnuerch](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/researchers/dr-martin-schnuerch/) (Post Doc)
- The [Coordinating Center](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/admin/coordination/)
