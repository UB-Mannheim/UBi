# From Paris to Mannheim

## Speaker Profile: Georg Müller

Georg Müller is the Chairman of the Executive Board (CEO) of MVV Energie AG. Previously, the lawyer Müller held several key positions, including Chairman of the Executive Board of RWE Rhein-Ruhr AG in Essen and Member of the Executive Board of VSE AG in Saarbrücken. He is also a member of comparable supervisory boards for German and foreign companies, such as Energieversorgung Offenbach AG, Grosskraftwerk Mannheim AG, and MVV Umwelt GmbH, Mannheim.
