## Speaker Biography

**Florian Egli** heads the Public Policy for the Green Transition research group at TUM.

His academic background includes:

- Completing his PhD at the Energy and Technology Policy Group at ETH Zurich, where he served as a Senior Researcher and remains a Lecturer.
- Serving as a Lecturer at the University of St. Gallen.
- Previously being an Honorary Research Fellow with Prof. Mariana Mazzucato at the Institute for Innovation and Public Purpose (IIPP) at UCL.

He is also a co-founder and co-president of Expedition Zukunft, an inaugural member and former speaker of the board of the Swiss Young Academy, and a regular contributor on Monocle M24.
