# Workshop on Advances in Climate Technologies

The **Mannheim Institute for Sustainable Energy Studies (MISES)** is hosting an interdisciplinary workshop on **Advances in Climate Technologies** at the University of Mannheim on **October 28, 2024**.

This workshop aims to convene academic researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers. The primary goal is to assess the readiness and economic viability of technologies crucial for transitioning to a decarbonized economy. Topics of focus include:

- Hydrogen and E-Fuels
- Energy storage
- Shared autonomous electric vehicles
- Carbon capture and sequestration (CCS)
- Carbon removals
- Climate engineering

Each subject presentation will be followed by a 20-minute open discussion, culminating in a plenary discussion.
