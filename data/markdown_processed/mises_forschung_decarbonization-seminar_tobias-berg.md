## Speaker Profile: Tobias Berg

**Tobias Berg** is a Professor of Banking at Goethe University Frankfurt. His research focuses on financial intermediation, with a current emphasis on financial technology and climate change.

**Research Highlights:**

- Tobias Berg was awarded a European Research Council Consolidator Grant (EUR 1.84 million) for his project, “The Role of the Banking Industry in Climate Change.”
- With this grant, he is building a research group at Goethe University to advance understanding of the banking sector's role in addressing climate change.
- He serves as an Associate Editor at the *Review of Financial Studies* and the *Review of Finance*.
- He has received the *Review of Financial Studies* Distinguished Referee Award and was ranked among the Top 10 Economists in Germany (Handelsblatt-Ranking).
- He has also won a best teaching award for corporate finance and a star lecturer award for financial intermediation.

**Academic Background:**
Before joining Goethe University, Tobias Berg was Professor of Finance and Head of the Finance Department at the Frankfurt School of Finance and Management. Prior to that, he was an Assistant Professor at the University of Bonn and held visiting positions at the European Central Bank and the Stern School of Business at New York University. He received his PhD from the Technical University Munich.
