# Identifying and Integrating Climate Related Risks and Opportunities in a Portfolio Context – View from BlackRock

**Event Details**

- **Speaker:** Victoria Arnold (BlackRock)
- **Date:** March 14, 2022
- **Time:** 05:00 – 06:15 PM (CET)
- **Format:** Live virtual event (Registration required via Zoom)

## Speaker Biography: Victoria Arnold

Victoria Arnold holds a Master in Management (Diplôme de Grande Ecole) with a specialization in financial markets from ESCP Europe.

Her professional experience includes:

- **BNP Paribas (2008–2015):** Worked in the Global Equities and Commodity Derivatives division.
- **DWS (2015–2018):** Responsible for covering institutional clients in Germany and Austria for index solutions.
- **BlackRock (Joined July 2018):**
  - Initially covered German Corporates & Pensions for ETFs & Index Investing.
  - Appointed local BlackRock ESG-representative for Germany, Austria, and Eastern Europe (September 2019).
  - Represents BlackRock in the working group Sustainable Investing at the BVI (German Asset Management Industry association).
  - Since September 2021, she represents BlackRock in the Green and Sustainable Finance Cluster Germany (Industry Think Tank on Sustainable Finance in Hesse and Germany).
