# Marilena Adleff

**Research Associate, TA for MAN 655**
*Center of Doctoral Studies in Business (CDSB)*

### 🎓 Akademischer Werdegang und Erfahrung

Marilena Adleff completed her Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in Philosophy & Economics at the University of Bayreuth in 2019. She subsequently pursued a Master’s program in Business Administration at Bayreuth University, specializing in strategic management and human resource management. Her Master's thesis examined the effects of digitalization on interorganizational learning, culminating in her M.Sc. in 2022.

During her studies, Marilena gained practical experience by working for two smaller consulting firms between 2017 and 2022, focusing on medium-sized companies and developing expertise in strategy consulting. She joined the Management Track at the Center of Doctoral Studies in Business in September 2022.

### 🔬 Forschungsinteressen

- Strategic alliances
- Organizational learning
- Effects of digitalization on business
- Sustainability in business
