# Center for Doctoral Studies in Economics (CDSE)

The Center for Doctoral Studies in Economics (CDSE) administers the doctoral program at the [Department of Economics](https://www.vwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/) of the University of Mannheim.

- [CDSE Program Details](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdse/cdse-program/)

## ZEW Cooperation Track

The CDSE also runs a specialized doctoral track in cooperation with the [Centre for European Economic Research (ZEW)](https://www.zew.de/en/). This track is designed to provide participants not only with an excellent academic education through the CDSE program within the GESS, but also with key skills in the field of economic policy advice—an area where the ZEW has a strong international reputation.

**Program Benefits:**

- **First Year:** Students opting for this track receive scholarships from the ZEW (1,500 euro/month). Scholarship holders benefit from close exchange with ZEW researchers and a dedicated research unit.
- **Second Year Onwards:** Conditional on successful completion of core courses and a successful interview with their preferred research unit at the ZEW, students become fully integrated into the ZEW as researchers while continuing the CDSE program.

For more detailed information on the ZEW PhD track, please visit [this link](https://www.zew.de/en/career/phd-track/cdse-phd-track-in-cooperation-with-the-zew).
