# Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS)

The [Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/) at the University of Mannheim offers comprehensive graduate training. The program focuses on empirical and quantitative methods and their application across business, economics, and the social sciences.

GESS provides access to world-class teaching and a vibrant, international faculty through 11 full-time PhD programs (three to five years). The structure emphasizes achieving research excellence within a discipline while encouraging the exchange of ideas and methods across the boundaries of the social and economic sciences.

## Center for Doctoral Studies (CDSS)

The [GESS Center for Doctoral Studies in Social and Behavioral Sciences (CDSS)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/) coordinates specialized courses. SMiP members are invited to participate in CDSS courses, and conversely, SMiP students are included in CDSS activities.
