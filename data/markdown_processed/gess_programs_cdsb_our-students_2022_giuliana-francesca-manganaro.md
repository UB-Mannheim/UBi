# Giuliana Francesca Manganaro, M.A.

**Research Associate**
University of Mannheim

## 🎓 Academic and Professional Profile

Giuliana F. gained profound insights into banking operations and organization during her business studies with a focus on finance at Goethe University (Frankfurt a.M.).

**Key Professional Experience:**

- **Founders Club:** Served as Chief Financial Officer, managing the initiative's financial budget.
- **KfW Banking Group:** Worked as a student, supporting the state development bank in project organization, departmental work, and business analysis.
- **Consulting & Finance:** Broadened her experience through internships in Mergers and Acquisitions/Business Development, management direction, sales, personnel consulting, and company formatting.
- **Private Banking:** Gained experience as a portfolio manager, overseeing high-net-worth clients.

**Academic Focus:**
During her Master's degree, she supported the Chair of Quantitative Marketing and Consumer Research at the University of Mannheim. She also voluntarily built and led the Mannheim Investment Club as CEO. Her research interests expanded from her bachelor's thesis (emotional and rational-theoretical financial allocation) to focus on behavioral anomalies in financial service sales.

**Research Interests:**

- Behavioral Finance
- Choice optimization in interactions
- Self-awareness and imaging
