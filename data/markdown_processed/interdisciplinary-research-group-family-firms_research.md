# Research Focus: Interdisciplinary Studies

Our research is guided by the search for truth, reflecting a passion for pushing the frontiers of knowledge. With over 30 international peer-reviewed awards and nominations, we are dedicated to sharing our insights through leading journals and fostering a vibrant academic community by organizing and participating in international academic conferences.

We aim to inspire positive change and new discoveries by combining tradition with modernity, offering a truly interdisciplinary approach where family meets business.

We invite you to discover our diverse topics and get inspired:
