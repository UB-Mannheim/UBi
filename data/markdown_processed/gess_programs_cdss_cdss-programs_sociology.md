## CDSS Doctoral Program in Sociology

The **CDSS Doctoral Program in Sociology** offers comprehensive training through topical coursework complemented by advanced methods in empirical social research. Students gain tools for rigorous theoretical and empirical analysis, learning to handle complex data types such as event history and panel data, experimental and survey designs, network analysis, game theory, and causal analysis.

- [CDSS course catalog](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/programs/cdss/course-catalog/)

## Research at the Department of Sociology

Researchers at the [Department of Sociology](https://www.sowi.uni-mannheim.de/en/research/sociology/) focus on central social phenomena, including:

- Demographic change
- Education and labor markets
- Migration and integration
- Developments in private sector and welfare state organizations and institutions

### Research Methodology and Scope

Research activities within the department are fundamentally **empirical-analytical**. This approach draws heavily on empirical data while remaining informed by theory and grounded in rigorous methods. Empirical studies are frequently enhanced by international comparisons and longitudinal analyses, utilizing extensive surveys, administrative data, experimental designs, and historical sources.

The core of the research is characterized by:

- Quantitative focus
- Methodological diversity
- Interdisciplinarity
