# Adiya Sadanova

**Academic Year:** 2025
**Center:** Economics

## Profile Overview

Adiya Sadanova is a student originally from Karaganda, Kazakhstan. She holds a Bachelor's degree in Economics with a minor in History from Nazarbayev University. She further completed a Master’s degree in Economics through the RoME program, a joint offering by EIEF and Luiss University.

## Research Focus and Experience

During her studies, Adiya gained valuable research experience as a research assistant, focusing on projects in political economy and applied microeconomics.

- **Research Interests:** Her primary research interests lie in political economy and industrial organization, with a specific focus on how institutional and market dynamics shape responses in emerging economies.
- **Master's Thesis:** Her master’s thesis examined the differential impact of identity policy change on attitudes across ethnic groups in Kazakhstan.
