## Siao Xu: Doctoral Student Profile

Siao Xu is a doctoral student in the Department of Economics at the University of Mannheim.

### Academic Background and Research Focus

Siao specializes in the topic of **big data**, with a primary focus on Econometrics, statistical machine learning, and their application in quantitative finance.

**Education:**

- **Master's Degree:** Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), major in Financial Mathematics.
- **Bachelor's Degree:** London School of Economics (LSE), Mathematics and Economics.

**Professional Experience:**

- **Research Assistant (HKUST):** Worked during one summer, dealing with the Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS) database under Prof. Chu Zhang.
- **Research Assistant (Shanghai):** Spent two years as a full-time research assistant at Shanghai Technological University, working with Prof. Hanming Fang.
- **Expertise:** His main professional focus involves handling USA, China, and Europe patent data, alongside Chinese entrepreneurship data.
