## SMiP Summer School 2023 (Archive)

The SMiP Summer School 2023 took place from June 26 to 30, 2023, at the University of Mannheim, Germany. The event was organized by the DFG funded Research Training Group on [Statistical Modeling in Psychology](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/) (SMiP) in collaboration with the [Interuniversity Graduate School of Psychometrics and Sociometrics](https://www.iops.nl/) (IOPS).

### Program Content

The summer school provided an in-depth introduction into advanced statistical models in psychology. Key elements of the program included:

- **Workshops:** Parallel workshops covering various topics of quantitative methods and statistical modeling ([link](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/archive-2023/workshops/)).
- **Keynote Talks:** Plenary keynote presentations ([link](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/archive-2023/keynotes/)).
- **Poster Session:** A dedicated session for participants to present research with senior researchers.
- **Networking:** A specific networking session for female participants ([link](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/archive-2023/program/women-increase-your-network/)).
- **Social Events:** Various social gatherings.

A detailed program brochure is available for download: [Summer School Brochure (PDF, 570 kB)](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/media/Einrichtungen/smip/Dokumente/SummerSchool_Broschuere_digital.pdf).

### Participation and Prerequisites

**Target Audience:**
The SMiP Summer School was designed for young researchers in psychology with a special interest in statistical modeling and quantitative methods. This included doctoral researchers in the SMiP and IOPS programs, as well as early-stage researchers from other programs and institutions.

**Prerequisites:**
Participants were expected to have:

- Advanced knowledge of quantitative methods and multivariate statistics.
- Solid skills in data analysis.
- General experience with R.
