# Yağmur Deniz Çufadar Aydoğdu

Yağmur Deniz Çufadar Aydoğdu is a student and researcher with a strong background in economics, environmental economics, and international trade.

## 🎓 Education and Background

- **Bachelor's Degree:** Economics from Sabancı University (Graduated 2023). During this time, she served as a Learning and Teaching Assistant and completed internships at two major banks.
- **Master's Degree:** Sabancı University (Graduated 2023).
- **Research Experience:** During her Master's studies, she worked as a research assistant at a center funded by the Turkish Industry and Business Association (TÜSİAD).
- **Dissertation:** Her Master's dissertation was titled “The Impact of the Paris Agreement on Turkish Manufacturing Firms.”

## 💼 Professional Experience

Following her graduation, Yağmur worked as an associate in the research departments of two of Turkey's largest development banks.

## 🔬 Research Interests

Her primary research interests include:

- Environmental Economics
- International Trade
- Applied Econometrics
