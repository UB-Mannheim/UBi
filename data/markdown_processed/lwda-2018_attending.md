## 🚌 Getting to the Conference

### By Train (Bahn)

Mannheim main station (Hauptbahnhof) is a major transportation hub located in the immediate vicinity of the University of Mannheim.

- **From Main Station to Venue:** Take bus route 60 to the stationUniversität West, which drops you directly in front of the building.
- **Local Transport:** The local public transport system is run by Rhein-Neckar-Verkehr GmbH (rnv). [Visit their website](https://www.rnv-online.de/english/).

### By Car (Auto)

- **Route:** You can reach the university via the A6 highway, exiting at Mannheim Mitte. Take the B37 and follow it past the Planetarium, then turn left following the sign for Universität.
- **Location:** Once past Mannheim main station, you are on Bismarckstrasse, which leads directly past the palace where the university is located (the Schloss).
- **Parking:** Please note that the University of Mannheim does not have a visitor's car park. We recommend using the [parking lot underneath the Mensa](https://www.parkinglist.de/parkplatz/Mannheim/Parkhaus-Universitaet-Mensa-Mannheim-2623).

### By Plane (Flug)

- **Regional Airport:** Mannheim has a regional airport with [direct flights to and from Berlin and Hamburg](http://flyrna.com/flugzeiten/).
- **International Airport:** The nearest international airport is in Frankfurt ([https://www.frankfurt-airport.com/en/flights---more/flights.html](https://www.frankfurt-airport.com/en/flights---more/flights.html)).
- **Connection:** High-speed train connections from the airport mean that you can reach Mannheim in approximately 30 minutes.
