# Qi Zhang

## Academic Profile

Qi Zhang is a doctoral student who joined the Finance track at the CDSB at the GESS in 2022.

**Background:**

- **Origin:** Jilin, China (Born 1996).
- **Education:** Completed B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Economics at Renmin University of China.
- **Experience:** During his Master's studies, he served as a Teaching Assistant (TA) for Advanced Econometrics and Advanced Mathematical Analytical Methods. His professional interests lie in Empirical Asset Pricing and Quantitative Finance.
