# Career Opportunities and Research Areas

The department is actively seeking highly motivated candidates for various roles, including:

- Postdoctoral Researchers (PostDocs)
- PhD Students
- Graduate Research Assistants (Master students)

Interested candidates are encouraged to apply directly to the chair whose research area best matches their interests.

## Application Requirements

To submit an application, please ensure the following documents are included:

- Curriculum Vitae (CV) and transcripts
- A short cover letter
- (If possible) Copies of your Bachelor and Master theses

***Note:*** *Applications that do not follow these guidelines are likely to be ignored.*
