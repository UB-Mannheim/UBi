## Economics Course Selection for Exchange Students

This guide provides important information regarding course selection for exchange students studying Economics at the University of Mannheim.

### Eligibility Rules by Degree Level

**For Bachelor's Students**

- You must select courses only from the bachelor's level curriculum.
- Master's level courses are generally not open to undergraduate students.
- Attendance in specific seminars is usually restricted to enrolled economics students.

**For Master's Students**

- You can choose elective modules freely, provided you meet the necessary prerequisites for each course.
- Core modules (E601–603 and E700–703) are generally not open to exchange students.
- *Note:* If your home university grants permission, you may be allowed to attend bachelor's level courses.

### Sample Course Topics

The department offers modules covering various advanced topics in economics and management science, including:

**Text Processing and Analysis:**

- Working with text: regular expressions/regex, text parsing, parts-of-speech tagging, web-scraping, and dictionaries.
- Text as data: word-embeddings (from bag-of-words to word2vec/GloVe), topic models, sentiment analysis, and text-similarity.
- ML with text (in Python): ML fundamentals, BERT, and text-classification.
