# AI & Supercomputing for Sustainability

We utilize advanced tools—including Artificial Intelligence (AI), big data, and supercomputers—to conduct interdisciplinary research. Our focus is identifying the optimal firm configurations and leadership types required to drive the sustainability transformation and develop effective strategies for reducing environmental impact.

By applying AI for good, we align research with long-term sustainability goals, fostering responsible growth and innovation.

### Research Focus Areas

Our research topics cover several critical areas of organizational sustainability:

- Firm Characteristics, Governance, & Organizational Sustainability
- Leadership Types & Organizational Sustainability
- Diversity, Gender Equality, Culture & Organizational Sustainability
- Theories of Organizational Behavior, AI, & Sustainability
