# International Scholarly Exchange

Science thrives on exchange. Our group facilitates advanced inquiry in organizational social science through transnational networks of scholars.

We are committed to openness, transdisciplinarity, and international scholarly exchange. Our research foundation is rooted in Organization Theory and the tradition of the Carnegie School (including scholars like H.A. Simon, J. March, and A. Kieser).

Scholarly advancement in organizational studies requires nurturing the development of social scientists and their scholarship throughout their careers while encouraging global collaborations.

## Key Academic Networks and Collaborations

We cultivate our research roots through active participation in major international consortia:

- **SCANCOR – Scandinavian Consortium for Organizational Research:** We maintain active exchange with SCANCOR at Stanford University and Harvard University.

  - [SCANCOR – Scandinavian Consortium for Organizational Research](https://scancor.org/)

- **European Family Business Research Centers Network (EUFBC):** We are an active member of this network, which embodies mutual exchange among Europe's leading research centers focusing on family firm research.

  - The EUFBC aims to create spaces for discussion, collaboration, and exchange through a bi-weekly virtual research seminar, providing support for leading scholars and early career researchers in the field.
  - [European Family Business Research Centers Network](https://eufbc.org/)
