# We have all it needs to make it work

This document summarizes the details of the seminar "We have all it needs to make it work," focusing on the transition to a decarbonized energy system.

## Event Details

- **Topic:** We have all it needs to make it work
- **Speaker:** Matthias Taft (BayWa r.e. AG)
- **Date:** April 25, 2022
- **Time:** 05:00 – 06:15 PM (CET)
- **Format:** Live virtual event
- **Registration:** Please register for this talk [via Zoom](https://uni-mannheim.zoom.us/meeting/register/u5Mrcu6grjkuHd36Ydsr1iWs1eJ0O3mehyND)

## Seminar Abstract

Renewable energy technologies are key to decarbonization and thus achieving the most challenging sustainability goals of our time. The seminar posits that the necessary components are available, and the focus must now be on executing the transition.

## Speaker Biography: Matthias Taft

Matthias Taft is an experienced professional in the renewable energy sector.

- **Education:** He studied mechanical engineering at the Technical University of Munich (1988–1993) and industrial engineering at the University of Hagen (1997–2001).
- **Career:**
  - In 2003, he took over the management of RENERCO Renewable Energy Concepts AG.
  - Since 2013, he has managed BayWa r.e. renewable energy GmbH.
  - From 2015 to 2021, he served as a member of the Board of Management of BayWa r.e. AG, responsible for the Renewable and Conventional Energy divisions.
  - Since 2021, he has served as the CEO of BayWa r.e. AG.
