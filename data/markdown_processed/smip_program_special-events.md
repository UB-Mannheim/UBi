# SMiP Group Special Events and Programs

The SMiP group organizes various special events and programs to complement the regular qualification curriculum. These initiatives cover topics from career development and sustainability to specialized research workshops.

## Key Programs and Initiatives

### Small Group Meetings

Since 2023, the SMiP group organizes Small Group Meetings approximately once per semester. These meetings focus on specific topics, bringing together SMiP members working in the respective field and featuring one external expert as a guest.
[Learn more about Small Group Meetings](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/program/special-events/small-group-meetings/)

### SMiP Summer School

The SMiP Summer School takes place at the University of Mannheim. The next session is scheduled from June 30 to July 04, 2025.
[Details for the SMiP Summer School](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip-summerschool/)

### Career Options after the Doctorate

This recurring event series features a SMiP alumna or alumnus who shares their individual professional experiences and answers participants' questions.
[More information on this Coaching measure](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/program/qualification-program/fall-winter-2025/#c441433)

### Digital Consultation Hours (Mercator Fellow)

The SMiP group's Mercator Fellow, [Lesa Hoffman](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/team/researchers/prof-lesa-hoffman-phd/), offers digital office hours specifically for SMiP PhD Candidates. During the first half of 2025, Lesa is available every second Wednesday from 3:15 – 4:45 p.m.

## Specialized Workshops and Projects

### Sustainability in Science

The project "Sustainable Futures" is a key focus area. In May 2025, [Asheley R. Landrum](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/smip/program/special-events/sustainability-in-science/) will visit the University of Mannheim, offering two workshops and one research talk as part of this project.

### General Special Events

The SMiP group regularly organizes various special events, including:

- **Informal Gatherings:** Informal SMiP gatherings after work.
- **Consultation Hours:** Digital PI Consultation Hours (on demand).
- **Past Initiatives:** Previous successful events included the "February of Failures" initiative (Spring 2021) and a talk series on career options after the doctorate (Fall 2021).
- **PhD Colloquium:** A voluntary PhD colloquium organized by SMiP PhD candidates.

### New Elements (Since 2022)

Following the second funding phase of the Research Training Group SMiP (April 2022), several new elements were established for the upcoming years:

- Small Group Meetings with external experts.
- Regular Consultation Hours with the Mercator Fellow.
- SMiP Summer Schools.
