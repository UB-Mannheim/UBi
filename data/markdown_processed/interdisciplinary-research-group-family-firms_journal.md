# The Journal of Family Business Strategy (JFBS)

The Interdisciplinary Research Group Family Firms actively contributes to the field by serving as editors and reviewers for leading academic journals, notably *The Journal of Family Business Strategy* (JFBS).

The JFBS is an international publication dedicated to advancing knowledge and understanding within the field of family business.

## Scope and Focus

The journal welcomes submissions that address all aspects of the relationship between family and business. Its scope is highly interdisciplinary and broad, covering:

- **Theoretical and Empirical Work:** JFBS seeks to be the primary outlet for high-quality theoretical and empirical papers on a wide range of strategy topics. It serves both young scholars exploring new areas and experienced scholars extending existing paradigms.
- **Interdisciplinary Approach:** The journal publishes best-in-class theory papers from multiple disciplines, including:
  - Organizational Behavior and Theory
  - Sociology and Anthropology
  - Psychology and Social Psychology
  - Strategic Management, Economics, and Finance
  - Industrial Relations
- **Methodological Variety:** JFBS maintains the highest standards for rigorous research, publishing a range of methodologies, including:
  - Quantitative research
  - Qualitative work
  - Purely theoretical/conceptual papers
  - Topics ranging from micro-level lab experiments (e.g., behavioral economics) to macro-level public policy impacts.

## Editorial Leadership

The journal is led by the following key personnel:

- **Editor-in-Chief:** Torsten Pieper
- **Senior Associate Editors:** Mathew Hughes and Jan-Philipp Ahrens

Multiple members of the Interdisciplinary Research Group serve on the editorial review board of the JFBS.
