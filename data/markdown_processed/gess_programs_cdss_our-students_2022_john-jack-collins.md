# John 'Jack' Collins

John 'Jack' Collins is a PhD Student in Sociology at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS).

**Contact:**
University of Mannheim, Mannheim

**Academic Background & Experience:**

- **PhD Studies:** Sociology at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences.
- **Master’s Degree:** Data Science from James Cook University. His Master’s project focused on predictive modelling for student attrition from sub-tertiary courses in Australia. During this time, he also assisted in research projects concerning social attitudes and voting behaviour in Australia.
- **Bachelor’s Degree:** Bachelor’s of Sociology with Honours from the Australian National University.
- **Professional Experience:** Prior to starting his PhD, Jack worked as a Senior IT Consultant, specializing in data engineering, analytics, and software development.

**Research Interests:**
Jack is interested in applying Data Science and IT methodologies to sociological research. His specific areas of focus include:

- Quantitative methods in Social Science.
- Machine learning, AI, and statistics.
- Software development applications for Social Science research.
