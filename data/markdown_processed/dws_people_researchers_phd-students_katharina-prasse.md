## 🔬 Research Profile

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Machine Learning group, supervised by [Prof. Dr.-Ing. Margret Keuper](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/professors/prof-dr-ing-margret-keuper/). My research focuses on **Computer Vision** applied to **climate change** related applications and **Explainable AI (XAI)**. My research is funded by the state of Baden-Württemberg.
