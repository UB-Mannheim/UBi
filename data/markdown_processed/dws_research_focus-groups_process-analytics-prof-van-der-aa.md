# Process Analytics Research Group

The Process Analytics group at DWS conducts research on the interplay of business processes and information systems. The focus is on developing automated methods for data-driven process analysis.

### Research Focus Areas

The research encompasses several key areas, including:

- Process analysis
- Process mining
- Robotic process automation (RPA)

Primary interests include, but are not limited to, conformance checking, natural language processing, event abstraction, data privacy, and data uncertainty.

### Group Background

- **Founding:** The Process Analytics group was founded in April 2020.
- **Support:** It is based on the Junior Professorship in Artificial Intelligence Methods held by Prof. Van der Aa, supported by the [Künstliche Intelligenz Baden-Württemberg](https://www.digital-bw.de/-/kunstliche-intelligenz-baden-wurttemberg) initiative.
- **Current Status:** As of February 2024, Prof. Van der Aa has moved to the Faculty of Computer Science of the University of Vienna.
