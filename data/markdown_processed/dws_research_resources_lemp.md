# LEMP: Maximum Inner Product Search Tool

LEMP is a specialized tool designed for maximum inner product search. This technique efficiently retrieves large entries within the product of two given large matrices. This problem is highly relevant in various fields, including data mining and information retrieval tasks.

For detailed technical descriptions and applications, please refer to the following publications:

- **SIGMOD 2015 Paper:** [PDF Link](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/media/Einrichtungen/dws/Files_Research/teflioudi15lemp.pdf)
- **TODS 2017 Article:** [PDF Link](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/media/Einrichtungen/dws/Files_Research/teflioudi16lemp-draft.pdf)
