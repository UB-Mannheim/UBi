# Decarbonization Seminar

The Decarbonization Seminar, hosted by the Mannheim Institute for Sustainable Energy Studies (MISES), is a platform for discussing the latest advances and challenges associated with the transition towards a decarbonized energy economy.

The seminar is jointly sponsored by MISES and the Center for European Economic Research (ZEW).

**Organizing Team:**

- [Gunther Glenk](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/area-accounting-taxation/team/gunther-glenk/) (MISES)
- [Marion Ott](https://www.zew.de/team/mot) (ZEW)
- [Nikolas Wölfing](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/area-accounting-taxation/team/dr-nikolas-woelfing/) (MISES)

*Note: The current version of the seminar can be found [here](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar-1/).*
