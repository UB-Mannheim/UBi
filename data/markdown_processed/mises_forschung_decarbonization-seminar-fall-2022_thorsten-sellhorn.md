### Speaker Profile: Thorsten Sellhorn

Thorsten Sellhorn is a highly experienced academic and advisor specializing in international accounting and sustainability reporting.

**Academic Career:**

- He has served as the Director of the Institute for Accounting, Auditing and Analysis at the LMU Munich School of Management since 2014.
- Previously, he held the Otto Beisheim Endowed Chair of Accounting at WHU since 2008.
- He teaches Financial Accounting, International Accounting, Accounting for M&A Transactions, and Financial Statement Analysis & Equity Valuation in various BSc, MSc, and MBA programs.

**Research Focus & Expertise:**

- His research interests center on IFRS, the role of accounting information in capital markets, and sustainability reporting.
- His work has been published in prestigious journals, including *Management Science*, *The Accounting Review*, *European Accounting Review*, and *Review of Accounting Studies*.
- He is a co-author of a leading German textbook on international accounting, *Internationale Rechnungslegung*.

**Advisory Roles:**

- Thorsten advises the IFRS Foundation (IFRS Advisory Council) and EFRAG (Academic Panel and Expert Working Group on European Sustainability Reporting Standards).
- He has over twenty years of experience designing and teaching executive education programs for major international corporations and organizations.
