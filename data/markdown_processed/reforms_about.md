## SFB 884 “Political Economy of Reforms”

The Collaborative Research Center (Sonderforschungsbereich) **SFB 884 “Political Economy of Reforms”** at the University of Mannheim was funded by the German Research Foundation (DFG) for the period 2010–2021. This successful funding period demonstrates the center's significant research output.

The center is an interdisciplinary hub where approximately **150 international researchers** conduct research across **18 projects**. The core disciplines involved include:

- Political Science
- Economics
- Sociology
- Statistics
- Computer Science

Most offices and the data center are located in the award-winning new building in B 6, 30–32.

## University Profile: A Leading Research Institution

The University of Mannheim is recognized as one of Germany's leading research universities. It consistently ranks highly in the social sciences and economics fields, placing it among the top 20 European research institutions according to the German National Science Foundation funding ranking.

The University's distinct profile is characterized by its renowned economic and social sciences, which are closely intertwined with humanities, law, mathematics, and computer science. For more details on rankings and evaluations, see the [University's rankings](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/universitaet/profil/rankings/) and [distinct profile](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/universitaet/profil/).
