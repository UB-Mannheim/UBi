# Yu Zhang

Yu Zhang is a student in the Economics Center, profiled for 2023.

### 🎓 Ausbildung und Erfahrung

Yu Zhang was born in 1998 and grew up in Gansu, China. Her academic background includes:

- **Bachelor's Degree:** Economics and a minor bachelor’s degree in Information Management and System from the University of International Business and Economics in Beijing. During this time, she spent one semester abroad at the University of Washington, Seattle.
- **Master's Degree:** Economics from Barcelona School of Economics.

She gained professional experience as a full-time research assistant at the Institute of New Structural Economics, Peking University, where she worked on regional integration and industrial transformation.

### 🔬 Forschungsinteressen

In her master's project, she and her teammates focused on the effects of biased media exposure on voting behaviors in Australia. Currently, her primary research interests lie in:

- Microeconomic theory (particularly game theory).
- Behavioral economics, especially concerning welfare and efficiency.
- She is also interested in exploring various topics that intersect with mathematics, history, or sociology.

### 🌿 Freizeit

In her leisure time, Yu enjoys reading books, playing volleyball and piano, and spending time in nature. She has a particular fondness for gourmet food and international cuisine.
