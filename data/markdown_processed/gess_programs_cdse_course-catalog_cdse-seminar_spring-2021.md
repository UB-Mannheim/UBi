# CDSE Seminar Spring 2021 Schedule

This document outlines the schedule for the CDSE Seminar held in Spring 2021. All sessions were held online.

## Seminar Sessions

**March 2021**

- **March 9, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** Capital requirements, bank competition and stability
  - **Speakers:** Tommaso Gasparini
- **March 16, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** Inflation – who cares? Monetary policy in times of low attention
  - **Speakers:** Oliver Pfäuti, Mykola Ryzhenkov
- **March 23, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** Platform information design with competing sellers
  - **Speakers:** Jacopo Gambato, Jasmina Simon

**April 2021**

- **April 13, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** Are payday loan users irrational? A credit scoring explanation
  - **Speakers:** Tsung-Hsien Li, Daniel Runge
- **April 20, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** Gender diversity in corporate boards: Evidence from quota-implied discontinuities
  - **Speakers:** Valentina Melentyeva, Suzanne Bellue
- **April 27, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** The role of intra-household insurance for optimal bankruptcy regulation
  - **Speakers:** Jan Sun, Tsung-Hsien Li

**May 2021**

- **May 4, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** An efficient dynamic mechanism with covert information acquisition
  - **Speakers:** Jasmina Simon, Jacopo Gambato
- **May 11, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** Lifestyle behaviors and wealth-health gaps over the life cycle
  - **Speakers:** Lukas Mahler, Chiara Malavasi
- **May 18, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** Ridge regularized estimation of var models for inference and sieve approximation
  - **Speakers:** Giovanni Ballarin, So Jin Lee
- **May 25, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** I can feel you – how experience shapes third-party punishment
  - **Speakers:** Jonathan Stäbler, Dam Thi Anh

**June 2021**

- **June 1, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** Employment transitions: Mechanisms for gender earnings gap
  - **Speakers:** Suzanne Bellue, Laura Montenbruck
- **June 8, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** (Non)drug-trafficking marijuana and hard drugs access
  - **Speakers:** Andres Daniel Pluas Lopez, Alexander Kann
- **June 15, 2021** (3:30 pm – 5 pm):
  - **Topic:** What is the source of the health gradient? The case of obesity
  - **Speakers:** Uta Bolt (ENTER Guest from UCL), Lukas Mahler
