# Information for Prospective Doctoral Students (CDSB)

This page provides an overview of various topics related to the doctoral program at the CDSB.

**For Active Students:**
Active students should consult the dedicated page for current resources: [For Active Students](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/gess/for-active-students/).

**Student Guidebook:**
Prospective students are strongly advised to consult the CDSB Student Guidebook. This guide provides extensive information on the GESS program, the CDSB program structure, international student guidelines, and living in Mannheim. The new Guidebook is scheduled for publication in August 2024.

**General Inquiries:**
For any questions not covered on the website or in the Guidebook, current and prospective students may contact the Center Management of the CDSB via email.
