# Academic Practice Nuggets: Educational Videos

These videos were created as part of the **„Innovative Digital Teaching at University of Mannheim (InnoMA)“** project, funded by the Stiftung Innovation in der Hochschullehre.

The primary goal of the resource is to address heterogeneity among students, particularly exchange students, regarding advanced academic practices in the social sciences.

**Usage:**
Feel free to use these videos in your classes. They can be used either selected or over the course of ten seminar weeks (Students watch a video as part of the seminar preparation and apply the content to the weekly readings).

**Credits and Feedback:**
The videos were created by Sandra Morgenstern and three of her BA-student research assistants. We welcome feedback on the existing videos or suggestions for new ones.

- **Contact:** morgenstern@uni-mannheim.de
- **Creators:** Dr. Sandra Morgenstern, Anna Pagani, Emmy Stachelscheid, Janis Beginen.
