# GESS Academic Advisory Board (AAB)

The Academic Advisory Board (AAB) advises the GESS in all significant academic and organizational matters. It consists of highly experienced and internationally renowned researchers who are familiar with successful graduate schools and the higher education systems in the USA, Europe, and Germany.

## Members of the Academic Advisory Board

The AAB is composed of leading experts from various international institutions:

- **Dirk Bergemann**: Department of Economics, Yale University ([Link](https://campuspress.yale.edu/dirkbergemann/))
- **Sir Richard Blundell**: Department of Economics, University College London ([Link](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/~uctp39a/))
- **Marnik Dekimpe**: Department of Marketing, Tilburg University ([Link](https://www.tilburguniversity.edu/webwijs/show/m.g.dekimpe.htm))
- **Ruediger Fahlenbrach**: College of Management of Technology, Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne ([Link](https://www.epfl.ch/labs/sfi-rf/))
- **Nira Liberman**: School of Psychological Sciences, Tel Aviv University ([Link](https://en-social-sciences.tau.ac.il/profile/niralib))
- **Petra Schleiter**: Department of Politics and International Relations, University of Oxford ([Link](https://www.politics.ox.ac.uk/person/petra-schleiter))
- **Norbert Schwarz**: Department of Psychology, University of Southern California Marshall ([Link](https://dornsife.usc.edu/norbert-schwarz/))
- **Jan Stallaert**: Department of Operations and Information Management, University of Connecticut ([Link](https://www.business.uconn.edu/person/jan-stallaert/))
- **Rosemarie Ziedonis**: Department of Management & Entrepreneurship, Imperial College Business School (on leave from Boston University) ([Link](https://www.bu.edu/questrom/profile/rosemarie-ziedonis/))
