# Compatibility of Work and Private Life

This evening's program is dedicated to the crucial topic of "Compatibility of work and private life."

## Expert Discussion

We will welcome distinguished guests and role models who specialize in combining family life and academic work. The discussion will feature:

- **Sophie Scharf** (University of Mannheim)
- **Marie-Ann Sengewald** (University of Bamberg)
- **Mischa von Krause** and **Andreas Voss** (Heidelberg University)

The format will include a podium discussion where experts share their individual views, followed by a Q&A session with the audience.

## Informal Networking

Following the formal discussion, attendees are invited to an informal gathering featuring drinks and a BBQ in the Rektoratshof. This provides an opportunity for further exchange of views and questions on this important topic.
