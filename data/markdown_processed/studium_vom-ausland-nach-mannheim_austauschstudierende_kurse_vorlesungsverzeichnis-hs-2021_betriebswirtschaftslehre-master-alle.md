# Course Selection Guidelines for Exchange Students (Business School)

The following guidelines detail which courses are suitable for exchange students based on their current academic level.

## 🎓 Course Eligibility Rules

### For Bachelor's Students

- You must select business courses only from the bachelor's level curriculum.
- Master's level courses are generally not open to undergraduate students.
- **Exception:** Enrollment in a Master's course may be possible if you are in your 4th year of an undergraduate program when you arrive in Mannheim.
- **Inquiries:** For specific questions, please contact the Business School Exchange Team via incoming.business@uni-mannheim.de.

### For Master's/MBA Students

- You are eligible to choose business courses from all master's level courses.
- If your home university permits, you may also take selected bachelor's level courses.

## 📚 Available Subject Areas

The Business School offers courses across the following specialized areas:

- Accounting and Taxation
- Finance
- Information Systems
- Management
- Marketing
- Operations Management
