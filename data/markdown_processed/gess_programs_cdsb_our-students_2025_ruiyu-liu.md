# Ruiyu Liu

Ruiyu Liu is joining the Finance track at the Center for Doctoral Studies in Business (CDSB), GESS, in 2025.

### Academic and Professional Background

- **Undergraduate Studies:** Ruiyu obtained her Bachelor’s degree in Economics from Fudan University.
- **Further Education:** She pursued advanced studies in management and economics at University College London and the European University Institute.
- **Professional Experience:** Prior to starting her doctoral studies, she gained practical experience in the financial industry, focusing on financial markets and institutions.
