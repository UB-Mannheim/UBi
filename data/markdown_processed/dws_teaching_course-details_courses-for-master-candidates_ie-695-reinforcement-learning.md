# Reinforcement Learning (IE-695)

This course teaches the fundamentals of Reinforcement Learning.

## 🗓️ Course Schedule and Location

The course runs from **September 2, 2024, to December 2, 2024**.

- **Lecture:**
  - **Time:** Monday, 10:15 to 11:45
  - **Location:** C 014 Hörsaal (A 5, 6 Bauteil C)
- **Exercise:**
  - **Time:** Monday, 12:00 to 13:30
  - **Location:** D 007 Seminarraum 2 (B 6, 27–29 Bauteil D)

## 🧑‍🏫 Instructor

- **Prof. Dr. Margret Keuper:** [Link to Professor's Profile](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/professors/prof-dr-ing-margret-keuper/)

## 📝 Assessment

- **Final Mark:** Exam (oral)

## 🎓 Participation and Requirements

- **Open To:** Students of the Master Business Informatics and Mannheim Master in Data Science (MMDS).
- **Capacity:** The course is restricted to 30 participants.
- **Enrollment:** Places are assigned on a first come/first serve basis. Students must register by emailing the instructor (keuper@uni...).
- **Prerequisites:** Basic knowledge of linear algebra is beneficial for the lecture.

## 📚 Literature Recommendations

- Richard S. Sutton, Andrew G. Barto, *Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction* (Second Edition, MIT Press, Cambridge, MA, 2018).
- David Silver, *UCL Course on Reinforcement Learning* (2015).
- I. Goodfellow et al., *Deep Learning* (MIT Press, 2016). (Online resource: [www.deeplearningbook.org](http://www.deeplearningbook.org/)).
