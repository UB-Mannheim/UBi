## Research Expertise

Dr. Symeonidou's research focuses on the intersection of memory, emotion, and cognitive aging, while also addressing broader social challenges.

**Core Research Interests:**

- Memory (especially source memory)
- Emotions (specifically effects on memory)
- Multinomial modeling
- Cognitive aging

**General Interests:**

- Social challenges (e.g., climate change, discrimination, diversity)
- Mental health
- Scientific communication
