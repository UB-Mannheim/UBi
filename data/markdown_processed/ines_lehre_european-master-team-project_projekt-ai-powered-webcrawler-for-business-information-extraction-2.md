# European Master Team Project: AI-Powered Webcrawler

This document details the project focused on developing an AI-powered webcrawler designed for the extraction of critical business information.

## 💡 Project Overview

The core objective of this European Master Team Project is to build a sophisticated webcrawler capable of navigating complex websites and extracting structured, actionable business data. This initiative combines advanced concepts in Artificial Intelligence (AI) with practical web scraping techniques.

### Key Focus Areas:

- **Information Extraction:** Identifying and pulling specific data points (e.g., contact details, product specifications, financial figures) from unstructured web content.
- **AI Integration:** Utilizing machine learning models to improve the accuracy and robustness of the extraction process, moving beyond simple keyword matching.
- **Business Utility:** Ensuring the extracted data is immediately useful for business intelligence and decision-making processes.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation

The project involves several technical components, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of modern data engineering practices.

### 🌐 Web Crawling Mechanism

The system is designed to crawl multiple sources, respecting ethical guidelines and technical limitations.

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is central to the project's success.

- **Data Structuring:** Transforming raw, unstructured text into clean, usable JSON or database formats.

## 🎓 Academic Context

This project serves as a capstone experience within the European Master program, allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge to a real-world industrial problem.

- **Program:** European Master Team Project
- **Goal:** To demonstrate proficiency in full-stack data solutions, from data acquisition (crawling) to data refinement (AI extraction).
