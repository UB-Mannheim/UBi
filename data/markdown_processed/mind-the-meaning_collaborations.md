# Collaborations

We closely collaborate with the research project [The First Person – Semantic, Epistemic, and Scientific Perspectives](https://www.ucf.uni-freiburg.de/people/chair-in-epistemology-and-theory-of-science/ets-archive/research).

This collaboration includes reflections on psychological and neuroscientific research on self-reports (PI: [Prof Dr Katharina Kraus](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mind-the-meaning/team/prof-dr-katharina-kraus/), University of Notre Dame).
