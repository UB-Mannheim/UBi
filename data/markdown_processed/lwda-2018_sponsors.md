# Sponsors and Exhibitors for LWDA 2018

We extend our sincere gratitude to the sponsors of the LWDA2018 conference. Their contributions were essential to the successful organization of the event.

### Event Details for Sponsors

- **Presentations:** Sponsors were invited to give 15-minute talks on Wednesday at 18:00 in room A001.
- **Networking:** Attendees had the opportunity to speak directly with the sponsors at the dedicated sponsor booths located in front of room A001 throughout the duration of the conference.

### Participating Sponsors

The following companies and institutions sponsored the LWDA2018 conference:

- BridgingIT GmbH
- Fraunhofer-Institut für Intelligente Analyse- und Informationssysteme IAIS
- German Management Consulting GmbH
- Institut für Enterprise Systems (InES) an der Universität Mannheim
- mayato GmbH
- MVV Energy
- Schönhofer Sales and Engineering GmbH
