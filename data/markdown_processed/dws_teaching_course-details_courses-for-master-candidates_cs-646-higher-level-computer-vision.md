# Higher Level Computer Vision (CS-646)

This course provides fundamentals in the field of Computer Vision.

## Course Details

**Target Audience:**
The course is open to students enrolled in the Master Business Informatics and Mannheim Master in Data Science (MMDS) programs.

**Prerequisites:**

- Basic knowledge of linear algebra is beneficial for the lecture.

**Assessment:**

- The final grade is determined by a written examination.
