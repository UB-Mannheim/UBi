# Jotham Talemwa

Jotham Talemwa is a doctoral student at the University of Mannheim, affiliated with the Graduate School of Economics and Social Sciences.

## Academic Background

- **Bachelor's Degree:** Economics from Makerere University, Kampala, Uganda.
- **Master's Degree:** Mathematics, Economics, and Statistics from the African School of Economics.

During his graduate studies, Jotham gained experience working as a Teaching Assistant and a pre-doctoral fellow at the African School of Economics.

## Research Focus

Jotham's academic interests lie in conflict analysis, with a specific focus on historical and ethnographic characteristics. Methodologically, he is particularly interested in quantitative methodology applied to conflicts and the relationships between state and non-state actors in Africa.
