# Admission to the CDSB Program (Doctoral Studies in Business)

Admission to the CDSB Program is based purely on academic performance and potential. Applications must be submitted via the online portal; applications sent by mail will not be considered.

## 📅 Key Deadlines and Requirements

- **Early Application Deadline:** January 15, 2026
- **Main Application Deadline:** March 31, 2026 (for Fall 2026 intake)
- **Prerequisite:** A Master’s degree or a four-year Bachelor's degree in the respective field or an equivalent qualification.

**Online Application Portal:** [gess-application.uni-mannheim.de/portal/](https://gess-application.uni-mannheim.de/portal/)

## 📄 Required Application Documents

Applicants must provide the following materials:

### 1. Personal Data

Full name, address, date of birth, etc.

### 2. Letter of Motivation

A statement (in English, max 500 words) explaining your current goals, career plans, and interest in the doctoral program in Business. You should discuss your study plans and fields of interest.

### 3. Certificates and Transcripts of Grades

- A transcript is required from **each** college or university attended. The degree certificate alone is insufficient.
- The transcript must show courses, grades, and degrees received, including rank or distinction if applicable.
- **Translations:** Any transcript in a language other than English or German requires a translation. (Note: Notarization or apostille is not necessary at the application stage, but will be required if accepted.)
- **Incomplete Studies:** If you have not yet finished your current degree, please attach a preliminary grade report (in English or German).

### 4. Standardized Tests (GMAT/GRE)

All applicants must upload their test-taker's copy in the online application and send the scores via the testing service.

- **Preferred Test:** GMAT.
- **Alternative:** Adequate GRE scores may be submitted.
- **Program Codes:**
  - GMAT Customer Service: VLC-95-89
  - Educational Testing Service (ETS): 0933

### 5. Letters of Recommendation

Two letters of recommendation are required from faculty members who can assess your academic ability and potential.

### 6. Writing Sample

Please attach a writing sample (in English or German), such as a bachelor's/master's thesis or a longer paper, to provide insight into your research capabilities.

### 7. Supplementary Information

Any other information relevant to your application.

## 🎓 Mannheim Master in Management Option

Students currently enrolled in the Mannheim Master in Management have the option to participate in the CDSB first-year program via the "Business Research" Program Option.

- **Application Deadline:** May 15, 2025
- **Details:** Please refer to the respective [study regulation](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/en/examinations/rechtliche-grundlagen/examination-regulations/examination-regulations-for-masters-programs/).
