# Decarbonization of Operating Assets: Cost Estimates for Vehicle Fleets

This document provides detailed cost estimates and strategies for the decarbonization of the university's operating vehicle fleet. It serves as a key resource for sustainability planning and operational efficiency within the University of Mannheim.

## 📄 Document Overview

The report, titled *Decarbonizing a Portfolio of Operating Assets: Cost Estimates for Vehicle Fleets*, analyzes the financial and logistical requirements necessary to transition the university's vehicle assets toward lower-emission or zero-emission technologies.

**Key Focus Areas:**

- Cost modeling for fleet transitions.
- Analysis of various decarbonization pathways.
- Operational asset portfolio management.
