## Hannah Reitz: PhD Candidate Profile

Hannah Reitz is a PhD candidate in Sociology at the Center for Doctoral Studies in Social and Behavioral Sciences (CDSS) at the University of Mannheim.

**Academic Supervision:**

- **Supervisor:** [Frank Kalter](https://www.sowi.uni-mannheim.de/kalter/)

**Education and Experience:**

- **Master's Degree:** Sociology, University of Mannheim.
- **Bachelor's Degree:** Sociology with a minor in Political Science, University of Munich (LMU).
- **International Experience:** Spent two semesters at the Maynooth University in Ireland during undergraduate studies.
- **Research Assistant Roles:** Worked as a research assistant for the Chair of Empirical Political Research and Policy Analysis at LMU Munich and for the Chair of General Sociology at the University of Mannheim.
- **Master Thesis Focus:** Examined the relationship between the ethnic composition in classrooms and the educational achievement and school satisfaction of adolescents.

**Research Interests:**
Hannah's research focuses on several key areas within sociology, including:

- Migration and integration
- Ethnic discrimination
- Social inequality
- Education
- Health sociology
