# ACC 922 Decarbonization Seminar

The seminar, available at [https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/lehre/kursangebot/acc-922-decarbonization-seminar/](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/lehre/kursangebot/acc-922-decarbonization-seminar/), is designed for doctoral students at GESS.

## Course Focus and Topics

The seminar brings together speakers from academia and industry to discuss the latest advances and challenges associated with the transition towards a decarbonized energy economy.

Key topics covered include:

- The economics and management of sustainability activities.
- Clean energy technologies across all sectors of the economy.
- Specific focus areas:
  - The energy sector.
  - Transportation services.
  - Carbon-free manufacturing processes.

## Course Details

- **Credits:** 3 ECTS
- **Lecturers:** Amadeus Bach, Gunther Glenk, Stefan Reichelstein

## Participation Requirements

Course participants are required to:

1. Attend all seminar talks.
1. Participate in corresponding preparation sessions.
1. In the preparation sessions, students must present a paper and take the role of a discussant.

*Note: Readings may additionally include recent theory or empirical papers.*
