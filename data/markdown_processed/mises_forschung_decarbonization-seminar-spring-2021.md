# Decarbonization Seminar Spring 2021

The **Decarbonization Seminar** in Spring 2021 brought together speakers and participants from academia and industry to discuss the latest advances and challenges associated with the transition towards a decarbonized energy economy.

### Seminar Overview

- **Sponsors:** The seminar was jointly sponsored by the Mannheim Institute for Sustainable Energy Studies (MISES) and the Center for European Economic Research (ZEW).
- **Organizing Team:** The team included [Gunther Glenk](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/area-accounting-taxation/team/gunther-glenk/) (MISES), [Marion Ott](https://www.zew.de/team/mot) (ZEW), and Nikolas Wölfing (MISES).
- **Current Seminar:** For the current version of the seminar, please visit [here](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/mises/forschung/decarbonization-seminar-spring-2023/).
