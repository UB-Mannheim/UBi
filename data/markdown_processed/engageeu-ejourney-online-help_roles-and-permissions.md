# Roles and Permissions on the ENGAGE.EU Platform

The platform distinguishes between two primary user roles: **Learners** and **Managing Staff**. Access to features and functionalities depends entirely on the assigned role.

## 🧑‍🎓 Learner Role

All users are assigned the **Learner** role by default upon registration. This group includes both students and staff members who participate in programs.

Learners are able to:

- Browse available ENGAGE.EU programs.
- Submit applications to programs.
- Track the status of their applications.
- Manage their personal profile and application information.

## 🧑‍💼 Managing Staff Role

**Managing Staff** are staff members responsible for administering ENGAGE.EU short programs.

In addition to all Learner permissions, Managing Staff can:

- Access ENGAGE.EU administrative features.
- Review and manage program applications.
- Support the selection and administration processes.

### Functional Sub-Roles

During the application process, Managing Staff may take on different functional roles depending on their institutional context:

- **Sending Staff:** Staff members affiliated with an institution that nominates or sends applicants to programs hosted by another institution.
- **Receiving Staff:** Staff members affiliated with an institution that hosts a program and is responsible for reviewing and processing incoming applications.

## ⚙️ Role Assignment Process

By default, every user is recognized as a Learner.

Staff members who require administrative access must be granted the **Managing Staff** role. To request this elevated access, they must contact the **eJourney team**, who are responsible for assigning **Managing Staff** permissions.
