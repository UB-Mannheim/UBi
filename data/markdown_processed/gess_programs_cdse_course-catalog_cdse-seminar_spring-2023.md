# CDSE Seminar Spring 2023 Schedule

The CDSE Seminar Spring 2023 featured a series of academic lectures covering various topics in economics and finance.

## Seminar Details

- **Series:** CDSE Seminar
- **Term:** Spring 2023
- **Location:** L7, 3–5, 001
- **Time:** 3:30 pm – 5:00 pm

## Lecture Schedule

**February 2023**

- **14.02.2023:** Tenure(d) Gap: Affirmative Action in Academia (Speaker: Maximilian Mähr)
- **21.02.2023:** Enough Bang for the Buck? The Pass-Through of Transfers on Real-World Public Investment (Speaker: Felix Köhler)
- **28.02.2023:** Can Index Insurances foster Technology Adoption? The Case of Solar Panels in Pakistan (Speaker: David Müller)

**March 2023**

- **07.03.2023:** Can Firms Reduce the Child Penalty? Consequences of a Mandated Increase in Firm Family-Friendliness (Speaker: Pia Heckl, Vienna University)
- **14.03.2023:** Optimal Monetary Policy in a Currency Union with Asymmetric House Price Booms (Speaker: Ulrich Roschitsch)
- **21.03.2023:** Don't Hate the Player, Hate Luck's Shape. Optimal Contest Size: Theory and Experimental Design (Speaker: Argun Aman)
- **28.03.2023:** Monetary Policy, Economic Uncertainty, and Firms R&D Expenditure (Speaker: Morteza Ghomi, ENTER Guest)

**April 2023**

- **18.04.2023:** Trickling Away? The Interaction of Local and Federal Taxes – Evidence from Swiss Cantonal Tax Reforms (Speaker: Paul Steger)
- **25.04.2023:** The Price Channel of Financial Contagion (Speaker: Daniel Linnenbrink)

**May 2023**

- **02.05.2023:** Work from Home, Work for Less? Location Flexibility and Gender Wage Inequality / Natural Disasters and Household Default (Speakers: Ursula Berresheim / Marina Hoch)
- **09.05.2023:** Uncovering the Role of Moral Hazard: Evidence from the Insulin Price Cap (Speaker: Matilde Cappelletti)
- **16.05.2023:** Road to Green? The Effect of Highway Expansion on Exporting Firms’ Pollution Emissions in China / Why Don't You Deduct to Donate More? A Field Experiment with Carol Singers (Speakers: Siyu Zhang / Michael Hilweg)
- **23.05.2023:** From Confiscation to Competition: The Economic Effect of Public Goods (Speaker: Luca Currado)
- **30.05.2023:** The Local Economic Impact of US Troop Withdrawals in Germany / The Intergenerational Consequences of Occupational Decline: Evidence from the Industrialization of the American Workforce during the 19th Century (Speakers: Jakob Schmidhäuser / Assaf Abraham)
