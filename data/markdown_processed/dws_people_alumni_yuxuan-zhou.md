# Yuxuan Zhou

**External PhD Student**
CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security

**Contact:** [yuxuan.zhou@cispa.de](mailto:yuxuan.zhou@cispa.de)

**Academic Background:**
Yuxuan Zhou completed a PhD at the Chair for Machine Learning in September 2025. The research was supervised by [Prof. Margret Keuper](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/dws/people/professors/prof-dr-ing-margret-keuper/) in collaboration with the [CISPA Helmholtz Institute for Information Security](https://cispa.de/de/research/groups/fritz), within the group of Prof. Mario Fritz.
